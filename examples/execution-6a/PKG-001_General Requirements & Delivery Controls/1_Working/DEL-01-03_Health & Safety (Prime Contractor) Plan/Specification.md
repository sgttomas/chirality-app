# Specification: DEL-01-03 Health & Safety (Prime Contractor) Plan

## Scope

### In Scope
This deliverable covers the full suite of Health & Safety (H&S) management documents required of the Design-Builder acting as Prime Contractor under the Alberta Occupational Health & Safety (OHS) Act and Regulations for the Town of Penhold Public Services Building (PSB) project (RFP 2024_004). The deliverable encompasses:

- A project-specific H&S Plan addressing construction site access, training plans, muster points, emergency procedures, and site-specific challenges
- A site orientation and training plan for all workers and visitors
- Hazard assessments for site activities and site conditions
- An emergency response plan

The plan governs the construction phase from mobilization through Substantial Performance.

Source: RFP §7.2 (Health and Safety); _CONTEXT.md; Decomposition SOW-0008

### Out of Scope
- Permanent facility operations safety programs (post-occupancy O&M phase — ASSUMPTION)
- Environmental protection plan (addressed under permitting deliverable DEL-01-04 — ASSUMPTION)
- Quality Management Plan (addressed under RFP §7.2 Quality Management and other deliverables — ASSUMPTION)

## Requirements

### REQ-01 Prime Contractor Designation
The Design-Builder shall act as the "Prime Contractor" for the Project for all purposes of the Alberta Occupational Health & Safety Act and Regulations. The Design-Builder is responsible for all responsibilities and duties prescribed under the Act.

Source: RFP §7.2 (Health and Safety), page 19

### REQ-02 Project-Specific H&S Plan — Content
The Design-Builder's project-specific Health & Safety Plan shall provide details covering, at minimum:
- Construction site access
- Training plans
- Muster points
- Emergencies
- Specific site challenges

Source: RFP §7.2 (Health and Safety), page 19

### REQ-03 Alberta OHS Act Compliance
All work on the project shall be completed in full compliance with all Alberta Occupational Health & Safety (OHS) Act, Regulations, and applicable OHS Code provisions. The specific clauses/Parts of the Act defining Prime Contractor duties and the specific OHS Code provisions applicable to this project are TBD -- to be identified upon review of the Act text by a qualified H&S professional or legal counsel. [Lensing: A-001, C-001]

Source: RFP §7.2 (Health and Safety), page 19

### REQ-04 Safe Work Narrative in Proposal
The Proposal must provide a narrative specific to the Project describing the measures that will be used to provide a safe work site for all persons on site, and describing the project-specific approach to the H&S elements listed in REQ-02.

Source: RFP §7.2 (Construction Methodology preamble), page 19

### REQ-05 On-Site File Maintenance
The Design-Builder shall prepare, administer, and maintain on-site files including the site-specific Health & Safety Plan for the duration of construction.

Source: RFP §7.3 (Construction Phase and Administration), page 20

### REQ-06 Strict H&S Policy Enforcement
The Design-Builder shall maintain and enforce a strict health and safety policy on the site throughout the construction phase.

Source: RFP §7.3 (Construction Phase and Administration), page 21

### REQ-07 Site Orientation/Training Plan
A site orientation and training plan shall be developed and implemented for all workers, subcontractors, and authorized visitors accessing the site.

Source: _CONTEXT.md (Anticipated Artifacts); ASSUMPTION based on REQ-02 training plans requirement (RFP §7.2)

### REQ-08 Hazard Assessments
Hazard assessments shall be prepared for site activities and site-specific conditions relevant to the PSB project.

Source: _CONTEXT.md (Anticipated Artifacts); ASSUMPTION that hazard assessment is a standard requirement under the Alberta OHS Act (Alberta OHS Act, location TBD)

### REQ-09 Emergency Response Plan
An emergency response plan shall be prepared as part of the H&S plan suite, addressing emergencies and muster point procedures specific to the project site.

Source: _CONTEXT.md (Anticipated Artifacts); RFP §7.2 (Health and Safety) — emergencies and muster points cited explicitly, page 19

### REQ-10 Regulatory Compliance -- All Building Codes and AHJ
All work and project designs shall meet all building codes and requirements of Authorities Having Jurisdiction (AHJ). Conformity with all required building codes and requirements of AHJ is the responsibility of the Design-Builder.

Source: RFP §7.2 (Construction Methodology preamble), page 19

### REQ-11 Subcontractor H&S Compliance Management
The Design-Builder shall establish and implement a subcontractor H&S compliance management process including: (a) pre-qualification of subcontractors with respect to H&S capabilities and records, (b) monitoring of subcontractor H&S performance on site, and (c) enforcement mechanisms for subcontractor non-compliance with the H&S Plan.

Source: ASSUMPTION -- standard Prime Contractor obligation under Alberta OHS Act (location TBD); gap identified via Guidance C3 which notes subcontractor compliance management is not addressed in existing requirements [Lensing: A-002]

### REQ-12 H&S Performance Metrics
The Design-Builder shall define and track measurable H&S performance indicators for the project, including at minimum: incident rate targets (TBD), orientation completion rate, site inspection frequency, and corrective action closure timelines.

Source: ASSUMPTION -- standard H&S management practice; no specific metrics are prescribed in RFP; metrics to be proposed by Design-Builder H&S Lead [Lensing: A-005]

## Verification

| Requirement | Verification Approach |
|---|---|
| REQ-01 (Prime Contractor) | Confirm Prime Contractor designation is stated in H&S Plan and in contract documents |
| REQ-02 (Plan Content) | Review H&S Plan against checklist of five required elements: site access, training plans, muster points, emergencies, site challenges |
| REQ-03 (OHS Compliance) | Legal review by qualified professional producing a compliance checklist mapped to applicable Alberta OHS Act sections; compliance statement with identified pass/fail criteria (TBD -- specific criteria to be defined upon review of Act text); Alberta OHS inspection/audit readiness confirmed [Lensing: A-003, C-002] |
| REQ-04 (Proposal Narrative) | Proposal submission review -- narrative present with site-specific content |
| REQ-05 (On-Site File) | Site audit -- H&S Plan present in on-site files during construction phase |
| REQ-06 (Policy Enforcement) | Site inspection observations at a defined frequency (TBD -- e.g., weekly minimum); incident log review; documented enforcement actions for violations with response timeline (TBD); escalation triggers defined [Lensing: F-001] |
| REQ-07 (Orientation/Training Plan) | Review of training plan document; attendance records for site orientations; verification that 100% orientation completion is achieved before any worker accesses the site (binding threshold: zero unoriented workers may access site) [Lensing: X-002] |
| REQ-08 (Hazard Assessments) | Review hazard assessment documents for completeness relative to identified site hazards |
| REQ-09 (Emergency Response Plan) | Review ERP document; confirm muster points identified and communicated |
| REQ-10 (AHJ Compliance) | Building permit and inspection sign-offs from AHJ; verification that AHJ notification obligations (per Procedure Step B6) are performed and documented via notification log with confirmation receipts [Lensing: X-004] |
| REQ-11 (Subcontractor H&S) | Review subcontractor pre-qualification records; review monitoring logs; confirm enforcement mechanism is documented and applied [Lensing: A-002] |
| REQ-12 (H&S Metrics) | Review defined KPIs; confirm tracking mechanism is operational; periodic metric reports produced [Lensing: A-005] |
| **Aggregate Acceptance** | The H&S Plan deliverable suite is accepted when all REQ-01 through REQ-12 verifications pass and the H&S Lead provides formal sign-off confirming completeness and compliance [Lensing: D-001] |

## Documentation

The following artifacts are required to fulfill this deliverable (from _CONTEXT.md Anticipated Artifacts):

1. **Project-specific H&S Plan** -- primary document addressing REQ-01 through REQ-06, REQ-11, REQ-12
2. **Site orientation/training plan** -- addresses REQ-07
3. **Hazard assessments** -- addresses REQ-08
4. **Emergency response plan** -- addresses REQ-09
5. **Subcontractor H&S compliance records** -- addresses REQ-11 (pre-qualification, monitoring, enforcement) [Lensing: A-002]
6. **H&S performance metrics tracker** -- addresses REQ-12 (KPI definitions, tracking, reporting) [Lensing: A-005]
7. **AHJ notification log** -- addresses REQ-10 verification (notification records with confirmation receipts) [Lensing: X-004]

All documents are to be maintained in on-site files (REQ-05) and updated as conditions change.
