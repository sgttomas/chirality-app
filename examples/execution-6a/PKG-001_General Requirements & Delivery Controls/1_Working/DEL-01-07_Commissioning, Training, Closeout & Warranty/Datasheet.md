## Construction

### Document Relationships

This deliverable is the terminal deliverable in PKG-001 and triggers the final contractual gates:
- Commissioning reports feed into Substantial Performance determination (RFP §7.5)
- Deficiency list feeds into holdback and retention calculations (RFP §7.3.7)
- As-Built drawings and O&M manuals are prerequisites for Substantial Performance (RFP §7.5)
- Legal survey satisfies SOW-0113 requirement for certified as-built survey (RFP §7.4)

### Format and Binding

Hard copy deliverables shall be in 3-ring binders separated by labelled tabs (RFP §7.5, location confirmed). Digital deliverables in format approved by Owner (RFP §7.5). As-built CAD drawings submitted in DWG and PDF.
