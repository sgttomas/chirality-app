# Datasheet: DEL-05-01 — Option: Built-in Wash Bay

> **Terminology note:** The canonical term for this deliverable is **"wash bay"** (lowercase). Variations such as "built-in wash bay," "vehicle wash bay," and "permanent washing facilities" appear in source documents and are used contextually, but "wash bay" is the standard short-form term across all four deliverable documents. *(Normalization per lensing item B-005)*
