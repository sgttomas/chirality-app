# Specification — DEL-03-04: Mechanical Design Brief

---

## Scope

**What this deliverable covers:**

- Mechanical systems selection rationale for the Penhold PSB (combined Fire Hall + Public Works Operations facility) and Cold Storage building
- Heating and cooling approach for all occupied building zones
- Ventilation strategy for each zone, with specific, Addendum-03-compliant treatment of: fire apparatus bays (direct exhaust), Public Works bays (general ventilation), and bunker gear room (room-level ventilation)
- Plumbing systems approach including domestic water supply, hot water, sanitary and storm drains, bay sumps, and 2" minimum water fill stations in fire and PW bays
- Fire protection system strategy (sprinkler system philosophy and fire alarm integration)
- Generator and emergency power philosophy, including minimum load coverage per Addendum 03 and bay door secondary opening mechanism
- Design life rationale tying mechanical system selections to the 50-year main building and 20-year Cold Storage design lives

**What this deliverable excludes** (scope boundary -- canonical phrasing per Guidance):

- Detailed mechanical equipment specifications or construction drawings (post-award detailed design scope)
- Energy efficiency quantification and solar readiness (excluded; covered in DEL-04-02 — Mechanical Energy and Solar Readiness)
- Detailed lifecycle cost analysis and maintenance scheduling (excluded; covered in DEL-05-03 — Mechanical Maintainability)
- Operations and maintenance manuals and training (post-award, post-construction commissioning scope)
- Pricing or cost implications of mechanical choices (covered in financial proposal, PKG-001)
- Adaptability/flexibility/expansion narrative for mechanical systems (covered in DEL-03-06)
- Cold Storage building heating (excluded per Addendum 03 — Cold Storage is unheated)
- Pickled Sand Storage building mechanical scope (excluded from this RFP per Addendum 03 §1.2)
