## Conditions

| Condition | Description | Source |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| **Five Design Submission Milestones** | Plan must address all five milestones: (1) Building Design Development, (2) 60% Detailed Design review, (3) Foundation Issued for Construction (if needed), (4) 100% Building Detail Design, (5) 100% IFC Detailed Design Package (full package) | RFP §7.1.8 |
| **Review and Resubmission Time** | Each milestone submission must include time for Owner review and resubmission; plan must make this cycle explicit | RFP §7.1.8 |
| **Design Brief Requirement** | Design-Builder submits a Design Brief with their proposal describing elements, products, and specifics of building assemblies and components | RFP §7.1.8 |
| **Owner Review Cycles** | Plan must document Owner review and approval authority and decision timelines at each milestone | RFP §7.1.8 |
| **QA/QC Integration** | Plan must identify QA/QC checkpoints aligned with design submission stages; all work to current industry standards and applicable codes | RFP §7.2 |
| **Discipline Coordination** | Plan must specify coordination methodology across architectural, civil, structural, mechanical, electrical, and specialty disciplines | RFP §7.3.4 (ASSUMPTION: coordination approach is implied by multi-discipline nature of project) |
| **Document Management** | All documents formatted, titled, and numbered to a standard agreed by Owner and Project Manager | RFP §7.2 |
