## Conditions

The Work is not considered fit for its intended use until the Owner has received all five of the following documents. Substantial Performance cannot be claimed until the Owner holds all five (RFP §7.5):

| # | Required Document | Produced by This Deliverable? | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Occupancy Permit | No | Regulatory; obtained from AHJ |
| 2 | Final commissioning documents | Yes | System test results, sign-offs |
| 3 | All Operations and Maintenance (O&M) Manuals | Yes | 2 hard copies (3-ring binders) + digital |
| 4 | As-Built Drawings | Yes | CAD (DWG+PDF) for E/M/P; PDF for all others |
| 5 | A Legal Survey | No | **Separate deliverable (DEL-10-02)**; not produced within the scope of DEL-08-01 (Source: Specification.md Documentation table item 5; _REFERENCES.md cross-reference) |

*Note: Items 1 and 5 are outside the production scope of this deliverable. The Legal Survey is tracked under DEL-10-02 (Due Diligence & Risk Management package). This deliverable must coordinate timing with those items to ensure all five documents are available simultaneously for the Substantial Performance claim.* (Enrichment ref: E-001)
