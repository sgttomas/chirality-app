## Conditions

| Condition | Detail | Source |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| **Warranty start condition** | Occupancy permit must be issued before warranty clock starts; NOT triggered by substantial completion or physical handover alone | RFP §7.6, page 25 |
| **Substantial performance prerequisites** | Work not considered fit for intended use until all five items received by Owner: (1) Occupancy Permit, (2) Final commissioning documents, (3) All operations and maintenance manuals, (4) As-Built Drawings, (5) A Legal Survey | RFP §7.5, page 24 |
| **Defect exclusion conditions** | Defects do not include imperfections naturally following misuse, failure to perform recommended maintenance, accident, or the wear and tear of normal use | Appendix J SC54 (adds to GC 12.5.4 preamble), page 71 |
| **Notice requirement** | Owner must give notice of observed defects promptly; Design-Builder bears overtime costs for work requested before warranty expiry | Appendix J SC54 (GC 12.5.4.3), page 71 |
| **Excessive servicing threshold** | "Excessive servicing" is not numerically defined in the RFP or Supplementary Conditions — ASSUMPTION: threshold to be agreed between Owner and Design-Builder at contract execution or defined in the O&M Manual maintenance schedule (see CON-01 in Guidance.md Conflict Table) |
| **Warranty work scheduling** | All corrective, remedial, or replacement work carried out at times convenient for Owner; may entail overtime; costs borne by Design-Builder | Appendix J SC54 (GC 12.5.4.3), page 71 |
| **Warranty reset mechanism** | When a defect is corrected under the "excessive servicing" provision, the warranty (or free maintenance period) is "deemed to take effect from the time that the defect has been corrected" | Appendix J SC55 (GC 12.5.9), page 72 |
| **Interim responsibility gap** | If occupancy permit is delayed after commissioning completion, interim maintenance responsibility is not explicitly defined — ASSUMPTION: Design-Builder remains responsible until permit issuance triggers warranty (see CON-02 in Guidance.md Conflict Table) | RFP §7.6 and §7.5 combined; location TBD in CCDC 14-2013 |
