## Construction

| Element | Content | Source |
|---|---|---|
| **Design QC** | Design discipline inspections and consultant certifications at each design submission milestone (Building Design Development, 60% DD, Foundation IFC, 100% DD, 100% IFC); corrective action for design non-conformances; cross-discipline coordination check at each gate | RFP §7.1.8, p. 18; ASSUMPTION: QC gates aligned to design submission milestones |
| **Construction QC — routine inspections** | Architect: minimum monthly inspections with written reports; Other disciplines: inspections per approved schedule and at critical milestones with written reports; all written reports provided with each progress draw | RFP §7.3, p. 21 |
| **Construction QC — substantial performance** | Each discipline consultant performs site visit and issues conformance certification letter; Work not considered fit for intended use until five closeout documents received by Owner | RFP §7.3, p. 21; §7.5, p. 24 |
| **Deficiency management** | Trade punch lists established before formal deficiency review; formal Deficiency Meeting held; comprehensive deficiency list with monetary values prepared; Owner 2.5x holdback right; 12-month warranty review; Owner 6-week post-occupancy addition right | RFP §7.3.7, p. 23 |
| **Shop drawing and submittal control** | Review against intent of approved working drawings, specifications, and original design per OSR; document review outcomes and corrective actions | RFP §7.3, p. 20 |
| **Documentation QC** | NMS format; document numbering/version control agreed with Owner and PM; hard copy and digital formats; professional seals on applicable documents | RFP §7.2, p. 20 |
| **On-site records management** | Continuous maintenance of on-site document files: RFIs, change notices, change orders, field reports, monthly progress/control reports, H&S Plan, testing/commissioning records (concrete cylinder tests, air barrier tests, MEP functional tests), and any other documents applicable to construction; access provided to Owner, PM, and Owner-retained consultants | RFP §7.3, p. 20; Guidance EX-002 (concrete testing); Guidance EX-004 (commissioning testing) |
| **Corrective action process** | Non-conformance identification, classification, assignment, timeline, verification, and escalation; expeditious correction committed; ASSUMPTION: three-tier severity classification (critical/major/minor) per standard construction QC practice | RFP §7.3, p. 20 (expeditious correction); ASSUMPTION: classification detail |
