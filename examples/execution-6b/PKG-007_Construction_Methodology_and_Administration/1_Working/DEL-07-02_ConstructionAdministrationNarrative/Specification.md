# Specification — Construction Administration Narrative (DEL-07-02)

## Scope

### What This Deliverable Covers

This deliverable is a **Construction Phase Administration narrative** (Section 7.3 of the proposal) that describes the Design-Builder's approach to on-site management, oversight, and administrative practices during construction of the Penhold Public Services Building.

**In-Scope Elements (per RFP §7.3, §7.3.1–§7.3.3 and _CONTEXT.md):**
- On-site superintendent role, qualifications, and scope of authority
- Quality control practices and on-site oversight structure
- Document management systems: RFIs, site instructions, contemplated change notices, change orders, clarifications, field reports, monthly progress reports, monthly control reports, site-specific H&S Plan
- Access provision: drawings, specifications, and design documents made available to Owner, Project Manager, and Owner-retained consultants
- Shop drawing, sample, and submittal review and approval process
- Assembly of warranties, O&M manuals, and as-built drawings
- Construction per approved IFC drawings and specifications
- Deficiency correction process
- Schedule maintenance and milestone adherence
- H&S policy maintenance and enforcement
- Consultant inspection protocol (including Architect monthly; all disciplines at critical milestones and substantial performance with conformance letters)
- Daily site cleaning and security (barricades, fencing)
- Final pre-occupancy cleaning
- Transportation and storage logistics for materials and equipment
- Site services and utilities coordination with Utility Provider(s)

### What This Deliverable Excludes

- Broader construction methodology strategy (covered in DEL-07-01: H&S plan, staging, permits, budget/schedule control)
- Project meetings and reporting structure (covered in DEL-07-04)
- Quality management standards and documentation formats (covered in DEL-07-05)
- Subconsultant coordination approach (covered in DEL-07-03)
- Commissioning and closeout procedures (covered in PKG-008)
- Deficiencies management process with monetary holdback (covered in DEL-08-02)
