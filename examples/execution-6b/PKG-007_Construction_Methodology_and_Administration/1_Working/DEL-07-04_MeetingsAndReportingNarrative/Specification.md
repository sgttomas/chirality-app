## Scope

### Inclusions

This deliverable is the proposal narrative section addressing RFP Section 7.3.5 -- Project Meetings and Reporting. It covers the Design-Builder's proposed approach to:

- Meeting structure: format (in-person vs. tele/videoconference), participants, chairing and minuting responsibilities
- Proposed meeting frequency and duration (proponent-proposed, based on project schedule)
- Standard agenda development and consistent application to all meetings
- Agenda issuance protocol (3 business days advance notice)
- Meeting minutes issuance and review protocol (4 business days post-meeting; Project Manager review)
- Progress reporting content at meetings: schedule, milestones, challenges and issues, proposed solutions
- Schedule update protocol: updated project schedule accompanying each agenda (milestone progress, slippage with explanations, plans for schedule recovery)
- Close-out meetings: approach to meeting structure for completion of all close-out activities
- Monthly progress reports: content, format, and distribution approach

### Exclusions

- Actual meeting records, minutes, or reports produced during construction (post-award operational documents)
- Construction administration document management, RFIs, change notices, and field reports (covered in DEL-07-02)
- Commissioning and closeout procedures (covered in DEL-08-01)
- Deficiencies management meetings (covered in DEL-08-02)
- Detailed project schedule (covered in DEL-09-01)
- Subconsultant management approach (covered in DEL-07-03)
- Construction methodology, health and safety, and budget/change management (covered in DEL-07-01)
