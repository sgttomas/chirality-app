# Deliverable Context

**Deliverable ID:** DEL-05-01
**Name:** Architectural Durability and Maintenance
**Package:** PKG-005 -- Building Durability & Maintenance
**Type:** Durability / Narrative

## Description

Architectural durability and maintenance strategy for 50-year main building and 20-year Cold Storage design life: exterior cladding selection rationale (weather resistance, impact resistance, maintenance frequency), roofing system selection and lifecycle, interior finishes strategy for operations environment (mud, dust, salt, equipment wear, chemical exposure in apparatus bays), flooring systems by zone (apparatus bays, offices, residential quarters), door and hardware durability (including 16 ft overhead doors), moisture management at transitions and penetrations.
