## Scope

### In Scope
This deliverable covers:

1. **Proponent self-inspection** of all construction work — confirming every item of the Work is complete, the site is in a clean and tidy condition, and the Works are ready for Owner acceptance. (Source: RFP SS2.14.1)
2. **Written request to Owner** for final inspection. (Source: RFP SS2.14.2)
3. **Owner final inspection** — facilitated by the Proponent; County project representative must be present. (Source: RFP SS2.14.3, SS3.3.2)
4. **Deficiency identification and documentation** — all remaining deficiencies or defects identified during inspection recorded in writing. (Source: RFP SS2.10.4, SS2.11)
5. **Deficiency remediation** — Proponent corrects identified deficiencies to Owner satisfaction. (Source: RFP SS2.10.3, SS2.10.6, SS2.11.2)
6. **Re-inspection of remediated deficiencies** where required by Owner, including documentation of re-inspection outcomes. (Source: RFP SS2.11.2; ASSUMPTION: re-inspection implied by Owner approval requirement; Enrichment: B-002)
7. **Issuance of Construction Completion Certificate (CCC)** by the Owner after final inspection confirms full compliance with Contract documents. (Source: RFP SS2.14.3; SS2.13.2.1)
8. **Submission of documentation** required for lien holdback release (CCC, claims letter, WCB clearance, Statutory Declaration — these are DEL-020-03 scope but the signed CCC originates here). (Source: RFP SS2.13.2)
9. **Coordination** of inspection activities through County project manager. (Source: RFP SS3.1)
10. **Consolidated inspection outcome summary** — a traceable record aggregating self-inspection results, Owner inspection findings, deficiency status, and CCC decision for downstream closeout documentation (DEL-020-03). (Enrichment: X-003; ASSUMPTION: required for traceability)

### Out of Scope
- Building systems commissioning (DEL-020-01, PKG-020)
- Closeout documentation beyond the CCC (DEL-020-03, PKG-020): claims letter, WCB Clearance, Statutory Declaration
- Bonding, insurance, and warranty period obligations (PKG-021)
- Development permit, building permit, and Safety Code permit inspections during construction (PKG-009)
- Design and construction trade work (PKG-001 through PKG-018)

## Requirements

### REQ-020-02-001: Proponent Self-Inspection
The Proponent shall carefully review all portions of the Work upon completion of construction. The Proponent shall satisfy themselves that every item has been completed and that the whole Works are in a clean and tidy condition and ready in all respects for acceptance by the Owner. Acceptance criteria: a signed self-inspection checklist (per REQ-020-02-011) with all items marked complete or with identified deficiencies logged and resolved prior to requesting Owner inspection. (Enrichment: A-002)
**Source:** RFP SS2.14.1
**Priority:** Mandatory

### REQ-020-02-002: Written Request for Final Inspection
The Proponent shall, by writing to the Owner, request that a final inspection of the Works be carried out.
**Source:** RFP SS2.14.2
**Priority:** Mandatory

### REQ-020-02-003: Final Inspection by Owner
The Owner shall carry out a final inspection of the Works. The Proponent shall facilitate access and coordination for this inspection. The County project representative must be present at all inspections and provided with a copy of all complete inspection reports.
**Source:** RFP SS2.14.3, SS3.3.2
**Priority:** Mandatory

### REQ-020-02-004: CCC Issuance
The Owner shall issue a Construction Completion Certificate (CCC) after completing the final inspection and confirming that the Proponent has complied fully with the Contract documents.
**Source:** RFP SS2.14.3
**Priority:** Mandatory (Owner obligation; Proponent must achieve compliance to trigger issuance)

### REQ-020-02-005: Compliance with Contract Documents
The Proponent must demonstrate full compliance with the Contract documents before the Owner will issue the CCC. Compliance encompasses all design, construction, and commissioning requirements set out in the RFP and Contract. Independent verification of compliance shall be evidenced by: (a) completed self-inspection checklist (REQ-020-02-001), (b) commissioning completion confirmation from DEL-020-01, (c) resolution or documented plan for all deficiency items, and (d) Owner inspection report confirming no outstanding non-compliance. The signed CCC is the outcome of demonstrated compliance, not the sole proof thereof. (Enrichment: F-001)
**Source:** RFP SS2.14.3
**Priority:** Mandatory

### REQ-020-02-006: Deficiency Documentation
Any deficiency or defect identified during inspection shall be documented in writing. The Owner shall notify the Proponent of any defect or deficiency by whatever means available and confirm notification in writing.
**Source:** RFP SS2.10.4
**Priority:** Mandatory

### REQ-020-02-007: Deficiency Remediation Timeline
Work required under the guarantee/deficiency period shall be carried out by the Proponent or their representative within ten (10) days of the Owner's written instruction to perform the Work, except for emergencies.
**Source:** RFP SS2.10.6; Decomposition SOW-0121 (PKG-021 scope note)
**Priority:** Mandatory

### REQ-020-02-008: Deficiency Holdback
The Owner is entitled to retain from any payment an amount equal to the estimated value of outstanding deficiencies or defects ("Deficiency Holdback"). The Deficiency Holdback shall be retained until deficiencies have been corrected and approved by the Owner.
**Source:** RFP SS2.11.1-2.11.2
**Priority:** Mandatory (contractual right of Owner)

### REQ-020-02-009: Completion Deadline
All Work, including final inspection readiness and CCC documentation, must be delivered on or before December 31, 2026.
**Source:** RFP SS3.1; Decomposition SOW-0091, OBJ-002
**Priority:** Hard deadline (contractual)

### REQ-020-02-010: Inspection Coordination Through County Project Manager
The Proponent shall coordinate inspections through the County project manager.
**Source:** RFP SS3.1
**Priority:** Mandatory

### REQ-020-02-011: Inspection Checklist
ASSUMPTION: The Proponent should develop a comprehensive inspection checklist covering all systems, finishes, site conditions, and contractual requirements to support the self-inspection (REQ-020-02-001) and facilitate the Owner final inspection. The RFP does not specify a checklist format; this requirement is inferred from standard construction closeout practice and the self-inspection requirement in SS2.14.1. Minimum checklist content categories should include: structural, architectural, mechanical, electrical, plumbing, civil/site disciplines, Alberta Safety Code compliance confirmation, and IFC drawing verification. (Enrichment: A-001)
**Source:** RFP SS2.14.1 (basis); checklist specifics are ASSUMPTION
**Priority:** Strongly recommended (ASSUMPTION)

### REQ-020-02-012: Safety Code Inspection Prerequisite (ASSUMPTION — pending confirmation)
ASSUMPTION: All Alberta Safety Code permit inspections required during construction shall be completed and approved by the applicable Safety Codes officer before the Proponent requests Owner final inspection. This is inferred from RFP SS3.3.2 ("The proposed building must adhere to all Alberta Safety Codes") and from the logical requirement that Safety Code compliance is a component of "full compliance with the Contract documents" (SS2.14.3). If Safety Code inspections are incomplete at the time of Owner final inspection, the CCC could be withheld. This requirement should be elevated from ASSUMPTION to confirmed status once validated with the Safety Codes officer schedule. (Enrichment: A-004)
**Source:** RFP SS3.3.2 (basis); ASSUMPTION
**Priority:** Mandatory (ASSUMPTION — pending confirmation)

### REQ-020-02-013: Re-Inspection Records
When deficiencies identified during inspection are remediated and re-inspected, the re-inspection outcomes shall be documented including: deficiency reference, re-inspection date, finding (pass/fail), and approving party. (Enrichment: B-002)
**Source:** RFP SS2.11.2 (basis — Owner approval of corrections implies re-inspection); ASSUMPTION
**Priority:** Strongly recommended (ASSUMPTION)

### REQ-020-02-014: Consolidated Inspection Outcome Summary
A consolidated inspection outcome summary shall be produced that aggregates: (a) self-inspection results, (b) Owner inspection findings, (c) deficiency status (identified, remediated, re-inspected, Owner-approved), and (d) CCC decision. This summary provides a single traceable record for downstream deliverables (DEL-020-03). (Enrichment: X-003)
**Source:** ASSUMPTION: required for traceability to DEL-020-03; not explicitly stated in RFP
**Priority:** Strongly recommended (ASSUMPTION)
