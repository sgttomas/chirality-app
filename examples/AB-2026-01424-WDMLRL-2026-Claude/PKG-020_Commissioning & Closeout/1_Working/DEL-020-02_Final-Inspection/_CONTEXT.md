# DEL-020-02: Final Inspection & CCC

## Deliverable Overview
**ID:** DEL-020-02_Final-Inspection
**Package:** PKG-020 — Commissioning & Closeout
**Title:** Final Inspection & Certificate of Completion and Compliance
**Category:** Closeout
**Responsible Party:** Project Manager

## Scope Definition
Comprehensive final inspection of all construction work including deficiency identification and remediation verification, culminating in issuance of Certificate of Completion and Compliance (CCC) signifying substantial completion and project readiness.

## Regulatory Context
Based on Main RFP §2.14 (Final Inspection and CCC) and §3.1 (Coordination Requirements)

## Key Roles
- **Project Manager:** Inspection coordination, deficiency documentation, CCC issuance

## Success Criteria
- All work inspected and verified complete
- Remaining deficiencies identified and documented
- Deficiencies remediated and re-inspected
- CCC issued and executed
- Substantial completion declared
