# DEL-020-01: Building Systems Commissioning

## Deliverable Overview
**ID:** DEL-020-01_Commissioning
**Package:** PKG-020 — Commissioning & Closeout
**Title:** Building Systems Commissioning
**Category:** Commissioning
**Responsible Party:** Commissioning Agent / Project Manager

## Scope Definition
Comprehensive commissioning of all building systems including functional testing, performance verification, operator training, documentation compilation, and system optimization to ensure facility readiness for occupancy.

## Linked SOW Items
- SOW-0090

## Linked Objectives
- OBJ-001 (Design & Specification Compliance)
- OBJ-002 (Project Coordination & Communication)
- OBJ-004 (Building Systems Performance)
- OBJ-005 (Operational Readiness)

## Regulatory Context
Based on Main RFP §1.0 (General Requirements) and §3.1 (Coordination Requirements)

## Key Roles
- **Commissioning Agent:** Direct commissioning activities and performance verification
- **Project Manager:** Overall coordination and contractor liaison

## Success Criteria
- All systems commissioned and tested per specifications
- Performance criteria met or exceeded
- Operator training completed
- Commissioning reports documented
- Facility ready for occupancy
