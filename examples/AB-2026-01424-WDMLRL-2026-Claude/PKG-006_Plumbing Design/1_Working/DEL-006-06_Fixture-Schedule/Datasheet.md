## Attributes

### Scope of Fixture Schedule

The Plumbing Fixture Schedule is a tabular engineering document listing all plumbing fixtures, fittings, and appurtenances specified for the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop Addition (New North Shop) and associated renovations to the Old North Shop.

### Building Context

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|

| **Approximate Gross Area** | ~13,000 sq ft (Source: RFP §3.1) |
| **Ceiling Height** | 35 ft concrete structure (Source: RFP §3.4, App B-Plumbing) |
| **Structure** | Concrete (Source: RFP §3.4) |

## Construction

### Document Character

The Fixture Schedule is a **design deliverable** (Schedule artifact type) within PKG-006 — Plumbing Design. It is produced by the Plumbing Engineer as part of the Issued For Construction (IFC) drawing package.

### Coordination Requirements

| System | Coordination Need |
|---|---|
| Drainage (DEL-006-03) | Floor drain locations and drain/vent routing must align |
| Water Distribution (DEL-006-02) | Water tap, emergency shower, and industrial wash sink supply connections |
| Cistern & Pump (DEL-006-04) | Demand calculations inform fixture scheduling |
| Septic System (DEL-006-05) | Fixture drainage loads inform septic system design |
| Architectural (PKG-001) | Fixture locations cross-referenced to architectural floor plans |
| Structural (PKG-002) | Floor drain sleeve and penetration coordination |
| Mechanical (PKG-003) | Washroom ventilation coordination |
| Electrical (PKG-004) | Pump motor electrical requirements; emergency shower lighting |

### Anticipated Artifacts (per _CONTEXT.md)

- Fixture schedule table (primary artifact)
- Equipment specifications
- Manufacturer selections and cut sheets
- Water demand and drainage calculations
- Accessibility compliance notes
- Code compliance references
- Fixture locations cross-referenced to drawings
