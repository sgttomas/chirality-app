## Description

Plumbing Fixture Schedule deliverable provides a comprehensive tabular listing of all plumbing fixtures, fittings, and appurtenances. This includes fixture types, quantities, models, manufacturers, specifications, and coordination with other systems (drainage, venting, water supply).

## Anticipated Artifacts

- Fixture schedule table
- Equipment specifications
- Manufacturer selections and cut sheets
- Water demand and drainage calculations
- Accessibility compliance notes
- Code compliance references
- Fixture locations cross-referenced to drawings
