## Attributes

### Drawing Set Scope

The Water Distribution Plans drawing set covers the potable and service water supply systems for the New North Shop Addition. Based on the RFP (§3.4) and Appendix B (Plumbing), the following systems are within scope of this deliverable:

| System | Description | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Cistern supply distribution | Internal piped water distribution from the 50,000 L cistern | RFP §3.4, App B-Plumb |
| Service water supply piping | Supply lines from cistern/pump to fixtures and taps throughout the shop | App B-Plumb |
| Water taps | Taps at designated locations per plumbing drawing | App B-Plumb |
| Pressure washer reel supply | Piped supply to water tap and pressure washer reel location | App B-Plumb |
| Emergency shower supply | Piped cold-water supply to emergency shower | App B-Plumb |
| Washroom water supply | Supply to washroom fixtures (wash sink, urinal — ASSUMPTION: includes hot and cold) | App B-Plumb, RFP §3.4 |
| Wash station supply | Supply to separate industrial wash sink (wash station) | RFP §3.1, App B |

**Note:** Cistern storage vessel and pump details are a separate deliverable (DEL-006-04_Cistern-Pump-Details). This drawing set covers the distribution network downstream of the cistern/pump system.

**Unit Convention Note [B-003]:** The RFP uses mixed imperial and metric units. The cistern volume is stated in litres (metric) while the septic tank capacity is stated in US Gallons (imperial). This Datasheet preserves the original RFP values and adds metric equivalents for consistency. The project team should establish a consistent unit convention for design documents. **ASSUMPTION: metric primary units are standard practice for Alberta plumbing design; dual-unit notation recommended where RFP values are imperial.**
