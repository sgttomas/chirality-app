## Description

Drain & Vent Plans deliverable provides detailed drawing sets for all sanitary drainage and venting systems. This includes drain lines, vent stacks, cleanouts, traps, and all associated fittings for the building's waste disposal and venting network.

## Anticipated Artifacts

- Drain system drawings (multiple sheets)
- Vent system drawings
- Isometric diagrams
- Detail sheets for connections and fittings
- Slope diagrams and sizing calculations
- Material and equipment schedules
- Construction notes and specifications
