# Specification — DEL-006-03: Drain & Vent Plans

---

## Scope

### What This Deliverable Covers

DEL-006-03 is the Drain & Vent Plans drawing set for the New North Shop Addition (West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop Addition). It provides the plumbing engineer's engineered design documentation for all sanitary drainage and venting systems in the addition, including:

- All floor drains in main shop repair bays (minimum three drains shown on conceptual drawing, App B-Plumb)
- Wash bay floor drain with connection to the exterior mud sump
- Emergency shower floor drain
- Industrial wash station drain
- Washroom drain(s) (addition and/or renovated Old North Shop washroom)
- Sump drains in repair bays (SOW-0045)
- Sanitary drain piping network connecting all fixtures and floor drains to the new septic holding tank
- Vent stack(s) and vent piping network
- Cleanouts (locations and types per plumbing code)
- All associated fittings, traps, and transition details
- Slope diagrams and pipe sizing calculations (as drawings or accompanying calculation package)
- Isometric diagrams for drain/vent systems
- Material and equipment schedules for drain/vent components

### What This Deliverable Excludes

- Water supply / distribution piping (covered by DEL-006-02)
- Cistern and pump details (covered by DEL-006-04)
- Septic system structural and site details (covered by DEL-006-05)
- Mechanical/HVAC systems (PKG-003)
- Civil drainage and site grading (PKG-005)
- Mud sump structural/civil construction details (PKG-018)
- Construction of the drain and vent systems (PKG-014)
