# Specification — DEL-006-08 Plumbing Specification

---

## Scope

### What This Specification Covers

DEL-006-08 is the written Plumbing Specification for the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop Addition, Camrose County, Alberta. It governs all plumbing materials, equipment, workmanship, testing, and installation methods for the following systems within the new building and associated Old North Shop renovations:

- Potable/service water supply (cistern-based)
- Domestic water distribution (pumps, piping, taps, pressure washer reels)
- Drainage systems (floor drains, sump drains, wash bay drainage, mud sump connection)
- Sanitary/sewage (septic holding tank, washroom fixtures, wash station, laundry)
- Emergency safety plumbing (emergency shower)
- Bulk water fill system (high-volume external fill pump)
- Plumbing vent system for all drainage installations [Enrichment: C-002]
- Hot water supply (source, capacity, distribution) [Enrichment: A-002]

This specification establishes quality standards, performance requirements, and acceptance criteria for all plumbing work. It serves as the governing document for PKG-014 (Plumbing & Water Systems Installation) to execute against.

### What This Specification Excludes

- Mechanical/HVAC systems (governed by PKG-003 / DEL-003 scope)
- Electrical systems and controls (governed by PKG-004 / DEL-004 scope)
- Structural design of cistern supports, sumps, or pits (governed by PKG-002 / DEL-002 scope)
- Civil/site drainage and stormwater management (governed by PKG-005 / DEL-005 scope)
- Construction execution (governed by PKG-014)
- Permit fee payment (Owner — Camrose County — responsibility per RFP §3.3.1 (R-01))
