# Specification — DEL-011-05 Wash Bay Enclosure

**Deliverable ID:** DEL-011-05
**Deliverable Name:** Wash Bay Enclosure
**Package:** PKG-011 Building Structure & Envelope
**SOW Reference:** SOW-0027a
**Revision:** Draft P3 — 2026-02-26

---

## Scope

### In Scope

This deliverable covers the physical construction of the enclosed wash bay structure within the New North Shop addition, including:

- Walls enclosing the wash bay on all sides, integrated with the building envelope
- Roof integration for the wash bay as part of the overall building structure
- One overhead door providing vehicle access to the wash bay
- Embedded steel plate(s) in the concrete floor within the wash bay
- Structural provisions for the mezzanine above the wash bay (mezzanine framing and stair details are in DEL-011-07)
- All structural and envelope elements required to form a weathertight, enclosed bay per IFC drawings

SOW-0027a is the controlling scope item for this deliverable.

### Out of Scope

The following are explicitly excluded from this deliverable:

| Excluded Item | Responsible Deliverable |
|---|---|
| Wash bay floor drains and connection to mud sump (SOW-0027b) | DEL-018-05 |
| Exterior mud sump construction (SOW-0027b) | DEL-018-05 |
| Electrical installation (lighting, overhead door power) | DEL-015-02, DEL-015-04 |
| Mezzanine framing details and stairs | DEL-011-07 |
| Foundation construction | DEL-010-01 |
| Plumbing connections within wash bay | DEL-014-04, DEL-014-06 |
| Mechanical/ventilation installations | DEL-013 package |

## Requirements

### REQ-011-05-001 — Enclosure Configuration
**Statement:** The wash bay shall be a single enclosed bay, dedicated for washing large equipment up to and including motor scraper class.
**Source:** RFP §3.4 — "Single enclosed bay dedicated for washing large equipment such as a motor scraper."
**Priority:** Mandatory

### REQ-011-05-002 — Bay Width
**Statement:** The wash bay clear opening width shall be 24 ft.
**Source:** RFP §3.1, App B (R-04) — floor plan dimension annotation.
**Priority:** Mandatory
**Verification note:** Dimensional tolerance for bay width measurement is TBD — to be determined from IFC drawings or applicable structural tolerances. [A-005]

### REQ-011-05-003 — Overhead Door
**Statement:** The wash bay shall be provided with one overhead door sized to accommodate motor scraper-class equipment access.
**Source:** RFP SOW-0027a ("including overhead door").
**Note:** Door width is shown as 24 ft on App B (R-04). **Door height is TBD** — not specified in RFP or App B; to be confirmed on IFC drawings. **ASSUMPTION**: typical door height for motor scraper-class equipment is 16–20 ft; this must be confirmed by Architect/Structural Engineer. Door height is a tracked open item requiring resolution before procurement and wall framing proceed. [A-001]
**Note:** Door is motorized — **ASSUMPTION** (consistent with all other bay overhead doors in the building). This assumption drives procurement (motorized operator specification) and electrical coordination (power feed to door operator). If motorized operation is not required, rework of electrical rough-in and procurement may result. Confirmation required from Owner or IFC drawing set. [A-002]
**Priority:** Mandatory

### REQ-011-05-004 — Wall and Roof Integration
**Statement:** The wash bay walls and roof shall be fully enclosed and integrated with the New North Shop building envelope, providing a weathertight enclosure.
**Source:** RFP §3.1, SOW-0027a ("walls, roof integration").
**Verification note:** Weathertightness acceptance criteria (e.g., visual inspection protocol, water test method, or reference to applicable building envelope standard) are TBD — to be confirmed by building envelope consultant or per Alberta Building Code applicable provisions. [C-002]
**Priority:** Mandatory

### REQ-011-05-005 — Steel Plate Floor Embedment
**Statement:** Embedded steel plate(s) shall be installed in the concrete floor within the wash bay to accommodate tracked and packer-type heavy equipment.
**Source:** RFP §3.4, App B (R-04) — steel plate shown in wash bay floor on floor plan.
**Note:** Specific plate dimensions, thickness, material grade, and embedment specifications are TBD — to be determined by Structural Engineer on IFC drawings and coordinated with DEL-002-08 embedment plan. [B-001]
**Priority:** Mandatory

### REQ-011-05-005a — Floor Drainage Slope (Coordination)
**Statement:** The wash bay floor shall be constructed with a drainage slope (direction and minimum gradient) sufficient to direct wash water to floor drain locations, as required by the IFC plumbing and civil drawings.
**Source:** RFP §3.4 (drainage referenced); App B (R-04) — floor drains shown; Procedure Step 2.2 references drainage slope coordination.
**Note:** This is a coordination requirement. The floor slope direction and minimum gradient are TBD — to be determined by plumbing design (PKG-006) and civil design (PKG-005). The drainage slope affects steel plate embedment elevation, formwork layout, and must be confirmed before the floor pour proceeds. [F-002]
**Priority:** Mandatory (coordination)
**ASSUMPTION**: A drainage slope is required for the wash bay floor to function as intended; the RFP does not explicitly state a slope value, but floor drains are shown on App B requiring positive drainage.

### REQ-011-05-006 — Mezzanine Structural Provision
**Statement:** The wash bay enclosure structure shall accommodate the overhead mezzanine above the wash bay, which is load-bearing (capable of handling heavy items such as oil totes and pumping equipment).
**Source:** RFP §3.4, App B (R-04) — "Overhead Mezzanine over Parts Room/Utility Room/Wash Bay"; "Load Bearing Over Parts Room + Utility For Storage."
**Note:** Mezzanine framing details are governed by DEL-011-07; this requirement covers only that the wash bay enclosure structure provides the necessary structural support as determined by the Structural Engineer.
**Priority:** Mandatory

### REQ-011-05-006a — Wash Bay Clear Ceiling Height
**Statement:** The wash bay enclosure shall provide adequate clear ceiling height within the bay to accommodate motor scraper-class equipment access and wash-down operations.
**Source:** RFP §3.4 (motor scraper-class equipment); App B (R-04) — building ceiling height annotated as 35 ft; wash bay has mezzanine above, reducing clear height.
**Note:** The building ceiling height is 35 ft (concrete structure), but the wash bay clear ceiling height — accounting for the mezzanine floor structure above — is TBD. This is a critical dimension for equipment access and must be confirmed by Architect/Structural Engineer on IFC drawings. [C-001]
**Priority:** Mandatory
**ASSUMPTION**: Clear ceiling height within wash bay is less than 35 ft due to mezzanine above; exact value must be determined from structural design.

### REQ-011-05-007 — Code Compliance
**Statement:** The wash bay enclosure shall adhere to all applicable Alberta building codes, regulations, and Alberta Safety Codes.
**Source:** RFP §3.3.2, §3.4.
**Note:** Specific Alberta Building Code parts/divisions applicable to the wash bay enclosure (e.g., Part 3 vs. Part 9 thresholds, occupancy classification for a wash bay within an industrial building) are TBD — to be identified by the design team or code consultant. [A-003]
**Priority:** Mandatory

### REQ-011-05-007a — OH&S Compliance
**Statement:** Construction activities for the wash bay enclosure shall comply with all applicable provisions of the Alberta OH&S Code, with the Contractor fulfilling Prime Contractor obligations per RFP §2.15.
**Source:** RFP §2.15, §3.3.2; Alberta OH&S Code (ASSUMPTION: applicable — location TBD).
**Note:** Specific OH&S obligations triggered by wash bay construction activities (e.g., confined space entry requirements if the enclosure is enclosed during construction, fall protection requirements for mezzanine-level work, hot work permits for welding) are TBD — to be identified by the safety coordinator or OH&S consultant and documented in the project safety plan. Cross-reference to project safety plan (SOW-0083) required. [F-001]
**Priority:** Mandatory

### REQ-011-05-008 — IFC Drawings
**Statement:** Construction shall be executed in accordance with IFC drawings signed and stamped by a P.Eng. licensed in the Province of Alberta.
**Source:** RFP §3.3.2.
**Priority:** Mandatory

### REQ-011-05-009 — Drainage Interface Provision
**Statement:** The wash bay floor and enclosure structure shall provide the necessary openings, sleeves, or penetrations for floor drains and connection to the exterior mud sump, as required by the IFC plumbing and civil drawings.
**Source:** RFP §3.4, SOW-0027b (drainage scope in DEL-018-05), App B (R-04) — mud sump shown exterior to building on east wall.
**Note:** This is a coordination requirement — the actual drain and sump construction is in DEL-018-05. The enclosure must be built to accommodate these penetrations.
**Priority:** Mandatory (coordination)

### REQ-011-05-010 — Guarantee Period
**Statement:** All materials and workmanship for this deliverable shall be guaranteed for the construction period plus 2 years following the Construction Completion Certificate (CCC).
**Source:** RFP §2.10.
**Note:** Per RFP §2.10.1, neither final progress payment nor the CCC relieves the Contractor of responsibility for faulty materials or defective workmanship. Per RFP §2.10.3, faulty materials shall be replaced and defects rectified during the guarantee period. Per RFP §2.10.7, all costs resulting from guarantee work are borne by the Contractor.
**Priority:** Mandatory (contractual)
