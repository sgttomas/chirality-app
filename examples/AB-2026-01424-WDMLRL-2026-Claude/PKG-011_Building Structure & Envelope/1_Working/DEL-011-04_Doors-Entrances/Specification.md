# Specification — DEL-011-04 Entrance/Exit Doors

---

## Scope

### In Scope
This deliverable covers the supply and installation of separate pedestrian entrance/exit doors, frames, and hardware for the New North Shop addition (West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop Addition, SW 14–44–21–W4, Camrose County, Alberta), as required by SOW-0026 (RFP §3.1, R-01).

In scope:
- All entrance/exit door assemblies (door leaf, frame, hardware) as specified in the IFC Architectural Door & Window Schedule (DEL-001-07)
- Frames and anchorage into structural openings provided by DEL-011-01 (Concrete Superstructure)
- Door hardware including hinges, closers, latches, locksets, weather stripping, and thresholds
- Caulking and sealing at door frame perimeters
- Adjustment and operational commissioning of all installed door assemblies

### Out of Scope
- 24'-wide drive-through overhead doors for repair bays (DEL-011-03, SOW-0025)
- 6'-wide roll-up door for parts storage room (DEL-012-01, SOW-0029)
- Overhead door for wash bay (DEL-011-05, SOW-0027a)
- Structural rough openings in the concrete superstructure (DEL-011-01, SOW-0022)
- Architectural design and door scheduling (DEL-001-07, DEL-001-11 — PKG-001 Architectural Design)
- Electronic access control wiring or security system integration (PKG-015 Electrical scope, if applicable)
- Interior door hardware finish selection beyond what is specified in DEL-001-07 (TBD)

## Requirements

### REQ-011-04-01 — Separate Entrance/Exit Doors Provided
The building addition shall be provided with separate entrance/exit doors distinct from the overhead vehicle access doors.

**Source:** RFP §3.1: "The proposed building shall have separate entrance/exit doors." (R-01, location: p. 11, §3.1)

**Priority:** Mandatory

**Note (Egress):** Entrance/exit doors serving as means of egress shall comply with NBC/ABC means-of-egress provisions, including minimum egress door width, swing direction (egress doors typically required to swing in the direction of travel when serving an occupant load above the threshold), and panic hardware where required by the applicable occupancy classification. Specific egress provisions TBD pending confirmation of occupancy classification (see REQ-011-04-03). [Enriched per C-001; source: Alberta Building Code / NBC Part 3, **location TBD**]

### REQ-011-04-04 — Accessibility Compliance
Where required by the Alberta Building Code and applicable accessibility standards, door assemblies shall meet barrier-free (accessible) requirements including clear opening widths, hardware operability, and threshold heights.

**Source:** RFP §3.3.2 (Alberta Safety Codes requirement); Alberta Building Code / NBC accessibility provisions — specific clause numbers TBD at design stage.

**Priority:** Mandatory (code-driven)

**Note:** ASSUMPTION: At least one pedestrian entrance/exit door will require accessible configuration given industrial occupancy and applicable Alberta code requirements. Exact requirements to be confirmed by Architect in DEL-001-07/DEL-001-11.

### REQ-011-04-05 — Weather Performance (Alberta Climate)
Door assemblies shall be suitable for the Alberta climate, including adequate weather stripping, thresholds, and insulation values appropriate for the cold-climate location at SW 14–44–21–W4, near Ferintosh, Alberta.

**Source:** ASSUMPTION: standard cold-climate construction requirement for Alberta; no explicit door thermal specification found in RFP. Specific thermal/weather performance values TBD in Architectural Specification (DEL-001-11).

**Priority:** Mandatory (ASSUMPTION — to be confirmed in design documents)

**Note (Quantified thresholds):** The phrase "suitable for the Alberta climate" does not constitute a measurable compliance threshold. Specific performance values (e.g., air infiltration rate per ASTM E283, thermal transmittance U-value) should be obtained from DEL-001-11 or NECB requirements when available. TBD — explicit deferral to DEL-001-11 for numeric values. Proposed authority: DEL-001-11 / NECB. [Enriched per F-001; source: Specification.md REQ-011-04-05]
