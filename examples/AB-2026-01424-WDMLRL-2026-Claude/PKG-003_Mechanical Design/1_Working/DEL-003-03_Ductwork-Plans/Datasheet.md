### Ductwork System Coverage

This drawing set addresses supply air distribution ductwork and general ventilation ductwork for the following mechanical systems that must be designed under SOW-0013. Dedicated exhaust systems (equipment bay exhaust, welding station exhaust) are governed by DEL-003-04 (Exhaust System Plans) — see Scope boundary in Specification.md and CON-003-03-01 in Guidance.md.

| System | Governed by DEL-003-03? | RFP Reference | Appendix B (Shop) Reference |
|---|---|---|---|
| High-Recovery Heating System — distribution ductwork | Yes | §3.4 | — |
| High-Volume Air Exchanger — distribution ductwork | Yes | §3.4 | — |
| Forced-Air Makeup Air (MUA) Unit — supply ductwork | Yes | Appendix B (Shop) note | Appendix B (Shop) |
| Wash Bay ventilation — supply/general ventilation distribution | Yes (ASSUMPTION: boundary TBD per CON-003-03-01; dedicated local exhaust governed by DEL-003-04) | RFP §3.1, §3.4 | Appendix B (Shop) |
| Service Pit ventilation ductwork — general ventilation distribution | Yes (ASSUMPTION: boundary TBD per CON-003-03-01; dedicated local exhaust governed by DEL-003-04) | §3.4 | — |
| Equipment Exhaust (repair bays) — dedicated exhaust | No — governed by DEL-003-04 | §3.4 | — |
| Welding Station Exhaust/Ventilation — dedicated exhaust | No — governed by DEL-003-04 | §3.4, Appendix B (Shop) | Appendix B (Shop) — Welding Station shown at north wall |
| Ceiling Fans (6 in main shop) | Coordination reference only (not duct-connected) | Appendix B (Electrical) | — |

> **Note:** Ceiling fan layouts are shown on the Appendix B (Electrical) conceptual drawing. The coordination interface between DEL-003-03 (ductwork) and electrical fan power is shared with DEL-004-04 (Electrical Lighting Plans) and DEL-015-04 (Equipment Power Circuits). ASSUMPTION: Ceiling fans serve air circulation, not active duct distribution; they are noted here for spatial coordination only. See CON-003-03-02 in Guidance.md.
