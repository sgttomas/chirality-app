# Specification — DEL-003-02 HVAC Layout Plans

---

## Scope

### What This Deliverable Covers

DEL-003-02 (HVAC Layout Plans) is a drawing set produced by the Mechanical Engineer under PKG-003 (Mechanical Design). It covers the graphical layout documentation for all HVAC and ventilation systems associated with the WDMLRL Maintenance Shop Addition, as required by SOW-0013 (complete final mechanical design — HVAC, ventilation, exhaust systems).

The drawing set shall depict the spatial arrangement, equipment locations, and high-level routing of:

- High-recovery heating system (SOW-0035)
- High-volume air exchanger (SOW-0036)
- Forced-air makeup air (MUA) unit (SOW-0037)
- Equipment exhaust hoses and fans in repair bays (SOW-0038)
- Welding station ventilation/exhaust system (SOW-0039)
- Ceiling fans in main shop area (SOW-0040)
- Service pit/trench ventilation (SOW-0028)

### What This Deliverable Excludes

| Excluded Item | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Ductwork sizing and distribution detail | DEL-003-03 (Ductwork & Distribution Plans) |
| Exhaust system routing detail | DEL-003-04 (Exhaust System Plans) |
| Equipment capacity calculations and load calculations | DEL-003-06 (Mechanical Calculation Package) |
| Equipment specifications and performance requirements | DEL-003-07 (Mechanical Specification) |
| Electrical power wiring to HVAC equipment | PKG-004 (Electrical Design) |
| Gas supply piping design | PKG-006 (Plumbing Design) — ASSUMPTION: gas supply by plumbing discipline |
| Structural supports for equipment | PKG-002 (Structural Design) |
| Plumbing systems (cistern, drains, septic) | PKG-006 (Plumbing Design) |

## Requirements

### REQ-001 — System Coverage: Heating

**Source:** R-01 §3.4 (SOW-0035)

The HVAC Layout Plans shall show the location(s) of the high-recovery heating system units serving the addition. The layout shall indicate coverage of the main shop area (including both repair bays), wash bay, and all ancillary occupied spaces (office, parts room, utility room).

**Verification:** Layout confirms heating units cover all occupied areas of the addition. TBD: define measurable spatial or thermal coverage criterion (e.g., zone-by-zone heating unit assignment confirmed against room schedule, or BTU/sq ft coverage threshold per DEL-003-06 calculations) [E-003].

---

### REQ-002 — System Coverage: Air Exchanger

**Source:** R-01 §3.4 (SOW-0036)

The HVAC Layout Plans shall show the location(s) of the high-volume air exchanger unit(s) and indicate supply/exhaust connections. The air exchanger serves the main shop volume.

**Verification:** Air exchanger unit shown on layout with supply and exhaust connections indicated.

---

### REQ-003 — System Coverage: Makeup Air Unit (MUA)

**Source:** R-04 App B (SOW-0037)

The HVAC Layout Plans shall show the location of the forced-air makeup air (MUA) unit. The MUA system shall replace air exhausted from the building. Location shall be consistent with the conceptual floor plan shown in Appendix B of the RFP.

**Verification:** MUA unit shown on layout; position consistent with App B conceptual.

---

### REQ-004 — System Coverage: Equipment Exhaust (Repair Bays)

**Source:** R-01 §3.4 (SOW-0038)

The HVAC Layout Plans shall show the location of exhaust hose drops/reels and exhaust fans serving each of the two repair bays, to capture and exhaust heavy equipment engine fumes. A minimum of one exhaust drop location per repair bay is required.

**Verification:** Exhaust drops shown at each repair bay; fan locations indicated.

---

### REQ-005 — System Coverage: Welding Station Exhaust

**Source:** R-01 §3.4; R-04 App B (SOW-0039)

The HVAC Layout Plans shall show the location of the welding station exhaust/ventilation system at the welding station in the main shop. The welding station is shown at the north-east area of the building in Appendix B. The system shall be of a type suitable for capturing welding fumes at source.

**Verification:** Welding station exhaust shown on layout at the welding station location.

---

### REQ-006 — System Coverage: Ceiling Fans

**Source:** R-04 App B-Elec (SOW-0040)

The HVAC Layout Plans shall show the location of 6 ceiling fans in the main shop area. Fan positions shall be distributed across the main shop area to promote air circulation.

**Verification:** Exactly 6 ceiling fans shown on layout within main shop area.

---

### REQ-007 — System Coverage: Service Pit Ventilation

**Source:** R-01 §3.4 (SOW-0028)

The HVAC Layout Plans shall indicate the ventilation provision for the below-grade service pit/trench. The service pit requires ventilation to address accumulation of heavier-than-air vehicle exhaust and vapors.

**Verification:** Ventilation supply and/or exhaust shown for service pit area.
