## Scope

### What This Deliverable Covers

DEL-021-01 covers the preparation, execution, and submission of bid security documentation that must accompany the proposal submitted to Camrose County for the 2026 Design Build of the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop Addition. This deliverable satisfies SOW-0097 (bid bond preparation) and SOW-0098 (bid security package submission).

The bid security package consists of:
1. A bid bond or certified cheque in the amount of 10% of the bid amount, and
2. A Consent of Surety issued by an Alberta-licensed surety company.

### What This Deliverable Does Not Cover

- Performance bonds and payment bonds (governed by DEL-021-02 and RFP §2.12)
- Insurance requirements (governed by DEL-021-03 and RFP §4.2)
- CCDC 14–2013 contract execution (governed by DEL-021-04)
- Guarantee period obligations (governed by DEL-021-05)
- Bid security for any other project or procurement

## Requirements

> Requirements are derived from Addendum 1 §4.11 (primary governing source) and RFP §1.3.1, §2.4, §2.7, §2.16. Requirements inferred beyond explicit text are labeled ASSUMPTION.

### REQ-021-01-001: Bid Security Amount
**Statement:** The proponent shall provide bid security equal to ten percent (10%) of the Bid Amount.
**Source:** Add. 1 §4.11
**Priority:** Mandatory — omission invalidates the bid.

### REQ-021-01-002: Acceptable Bid Security Form
**Statement:** Bid security shall be in the form of either (a) a bid bond or (b) a certified cheque payable to the Camrose County of the Province of Alberta.
**Source:** Add. 1 §4.11
**Priority:** Mandatory.

### REQ-021-01-003: Consent of Surety Required
**Statement:** Each bid must be accompanied by a Consent of Surety as required by the Contract Documents, in addition to the bid bond or certified cheque.
**Source:** Add. 1 §4.11
**Priority:** Mandatory.

### REQ-021-01-004: Surety Licensing
**Statement:** The Consent of Surety must be issued by a surety company licensed to do business in the Province of Alberta.
**Source:** Add. 1 §4.11
**Priority:** Mandatory.

### REQ-021-01-005: Surety Address on Document
**Statement:** The Consent of Surety document must include the surety company's Alberta address or the address of its representative agent in the Province.
**Source:** Add. 1 §4.11
**Priority:** Mandatory.

### REQ-021-01-006: Submission with Proposal
**Statement:** Bid security (bid bond or certified cheque and Consent of Surety) must be enclosed with the sealed proposal envelope and submitted on or before the RFP closing time of April 2, 2026, at 2:00:00 p.m. MST.
**Source:** Add. 1 §4.11; RFP §1.3.1
**Priority:** Mandatory — late or missing submission invalidates the bid.

### REQ-021-01-007: Physical Submission Only
**Statement:** Proposals and enclosed bid security must be submitted in a sealed physical envelope. No faxed or electronically submitted proposals will be accepted.
**Source:** RFP §1.3.1
**Priority:** Mandatory.

### REQ-021-01-008: Addendum Acknowledgment
**Statement:** The proponent must acknowledge receipt of Addendum No. 1 (dated February 19, 2026, which introduced the §4.11 bid security requirement) using the form in Appendix A.
**Source:** RFP §4.1; RFP §4.9
**Priority:** Mandatory — failure to acknowledge may result in disqualification.

### REQ-021-01-009: Forfeiture Obligation — Contract Execution
**Statement:** If, within twenty-one (21) days after the contract is presented to the proponent for signature (by hand delivery, registered mail, or courier), the proponent refuses or fails to (a) sign and return the Contract, or (b) provide the required security and insurance, the bid bond or deposit is subject to forfeiture to Camrose County.
**Source:** Add. 1 §4.11
**Priority:** Normative (obligation upon award).

### REQ-021-01-010: Forfeiture Liability Cap
**Statement:** If the bid bond or deposit is forfeited and the County enters a contract with another party for a greater amount, the proponent is liable to the County for the difference between their bid and that contract amount, to a maximum not exceeding the amount of the security required.
**Source:** Add. 1 §4.11
**Priority:** Normative (obligation upon award).

### REQ-021-01-011: Internal Approvals (ASSUMPTION)
**Statement:** The bid security instrument (bid bond or certified cheque) must be reviewed and approved by the Proponent's authorized executive or officer prior to submission.
**Source:** ASSUMPTION — inferred from standard commercial practice and the _CONTEXT.md success criterion "Obtain executive sign-off on bid bond." No explicit RFP requirement for internal approval process.
**Priority:** Internal requirement.

### REQ-021-01-012: Bid Withdrawal and Security Handling (Enrichment: F-002)
**Statement:** If the proponent elects to withdraw or modify the bid during the 30-minute window before closing time (RFP §2.4), the handling of the enclosed bid security (return of certified cheque or lapse of bid bond) must be anticipated. The proponent should confirm with the surety whether the bid bond is automatically voided upon withdrawal, and should verify with the Owner the process for physical retrieval of a certified cheque if applicable.
**Source:** RFP §2.4 (withdrawal window); Add. 1 §4.11 (bid security terms). TBD: The RFP does not explicitly state the mechanics of bid security return upon withdrawal. [Enrichment: F-002]
**Priority:** Informational — anticipatory planning for potential withdrawal scenario.

### REQ-021-01-013: OBJ-008 Acceptance Criteria (Enrichment: D-003)
**Statement:** This deliverable satisfies OBJ-008 (Satisfy all bonding, insurance, and warranty obligations) for the bid security component when: (a) bid security conforming to all mandatory requirements (REQ-021-01-001 through REQ-021-01-008) is submitted with the proposal before the closing deadline, and (b) all verification checks in the Verification table pass. OBJ-008 satisfaction for the full bonding, insurance, and warranty scope additionally requires completion of DEL-021-02 through DEL-021-05.
**Source:** _CONTEXT.md (Linked Objectives: OBJ-008); Decomposition — OBJ-008 definition: "Satisfy all bonding, insurance, and warranty obligations through the 2-year guarantee period post-CCC." [Enrichment: D-003]
**Priority:** Evaluative — links this deliverable's acceptance to its objective-level success condition. ASSUMPTION: This decomposition of OBJ-008 into a per-deliverable acceptance criterion is inferred; the human should confirm that the listed criteria constitute full satisfaction of the bid security portion of OBJ-008.
