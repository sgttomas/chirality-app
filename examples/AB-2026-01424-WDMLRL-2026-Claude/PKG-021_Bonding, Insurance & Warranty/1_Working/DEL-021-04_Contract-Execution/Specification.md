# Specification — DEL-021-04: CCDC 14-2013 Contract Execution

---

## Scope

### What This Deliverable Covers

DEL-021-04 covers the complete execution of the CCDC 14-2013 Design-Build Stipulated Price Contract between Camrose County (Owner) and the successful Proponent (Design-Builder) for the 2026 Design Build of the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop Addition.

In scope:
- Finalization of all contract terms and conditions
- Confirmation of signature authority and execution by authorized representatives of both parties
- Assembly of the full contract execution package (contract form, supplementary conditions, bonds, insurance evidence, response forms, designation forms)
- Establishment of contract administration procedures
- Distribution of fully executed contract copies to all required parties
- Authorization of project execution and scheduling of site mobilization

### What This Deliverable Excludes

- The content of the design and construction work itself (covered by PKG-001 through PKG-018)
- Bidding activities and proposal preparation (pre-contract; excluded per Decomposition D-003)
- Ongoing construction management and administration beyond initial setup (PKG-019)
- Commissioning and closeout activities (PKG-020)
- Warranty and guarantee period obligations after contract execution (DEL-021-05). Note: The guarantee period terms are established in this contract (REQ-021-04-21, REQ-021-04-22) but ongoing administration and defect rectification during the guarantee period are managed under DEL-021-05. See Guidance Considerations for handoff details.
- Payment of permit fees (Owner responsibility per RFP §3.3.1)

## Requirements

### REQ-021-04-01: Contract Form
The Design-Builder shall execute a CCDC 14-2013 Design-Build Stipulated Price Contract prior to commencing any work on the project.
- **Source:** RFP §2.7
- **Note:** The full text of CCDC 14-2013 is not directly accessible; clause-level requirements within the standard form are not derived here and are marked TBD pending access to the standard form document. Specific general conditions expected to be operative (change orders, dispute resolution, termination provisions) require the standard form text for normative derivation. See TBD Tracking item A-002.

### REQ-021-04-02: Execution Prior to Commencement
The contract must be fully executed (signed by both parties) before any work commences.
- **Source:** RFP §2.7 ("Prior to commencing work, the successful Proponent must sign...")
- **Verification note:** The relationship between contract execution date and any Notice to Proceed (NTP) should be confirmed. See Guidance Considerations for NTP discussion.

### REQ-021-04-03: Authorized Signatures
The contract must be signed by an authorized representative of the Design-Builder (Proponent). If the Proponent is a corporation, signatures of duly authorized officers are required with the full corporate name printed above signatures.
- **Source:** RFP §4.3.1, §4.3.2

### REQ-021-04-04: Stipulated Price — Firm Scope
The contract price for all scope items except foundation design/construction must be a firm (stipulated) price, comprehensive and inclusive of all tasks identified in RFP §4.7.3.
- **Source:** RFP §4.8.1

### REQ-021-04-05: Foundation Pricing — Variable
The contract must accommodate a variable-price element for foundation design/construction. Foundation pricing is negotiated with the successful Proponent after the complete geotechnical report is available.
- **Source:** RFP §4.8.2; Decomposition SOW-0304, OBJ-006
- **Note (ASSUMPTION):** The variable foundation scope will be incorporated as a contract amendment or schedule addendum post-geotech. The specific mechanism within CCDC 14-2013 for this is TBD pending access to the standard form.

### REQ-021-04-06: Performance Bond
The Design-Builder shall provide a Performance Bond in the amount of fifty percent (50%) of the Contract price at time of contract execution.
- **Source:** RFP §2.12.1.1; Decomposition SOW-0099

### REQ-021-04-07: Labour and Material Payment Bond
The Design-Builder shall provide a Labour and Material Payment Bond in the amount of fifty percent (50%) of the Contract price at time of contract execution.
- **Source:** RFP §2.12.1.2; Decomposition SOW-0100

### REQ-021-04-08: Bond Timing
Both bonds must be provided with the executed Contract Agreement, within ten (10) days of being notified of contract award.
- **Source:** RFP §2.12.3; Decomposition SOW-0101
- **Note:** This requirement contains a timing ambiguity — see Conflict Table C-021-04-01 in Guidance.md. RFP §2.12.1 states "at the time of his execution" while §2.12.3 states "within the 10 days." Conservative interpretation: bonds at or before signing. Confirm with County at award.

### REQ-021-04-09: Bond Issuer
Bonds must be issued by a surety company licensed to conduct business in the Province of Alberta, on forms acceptable to the Owner and compatible with the Contract documents.
- **Source:** RFP §2.12.2

### REQ-021-04-10: Insurance — Automobile / CGL
The Design-Builder shall provide Standard Automobile, Bodily Injury, and Property Damage Insurance with coverage of at least $5,000,000 inclusive, including non-owned automobiles, independent subcontractors, contractual liability, broad form property damage endorsement, environmental liability, and product/completed operations coverage.
- **Source:** RFP §4.2.1 and subsections

### REQ-021-04-11: Insurance — Workers Compensation
Workers' Compensation coverage for all employees engaged by the Proponent, in accordance with the laws of the Province of Alberta.
- **Source:** RFP §4.2.2; Decomposition SOW-0102

### REQ-021-04-12: Insurance — Professional Liability (E&O)
Professional Liability Errors and Omissions insurance of at least $5,000,000.
- **Source:** RFP §4.2.3; Decomposition SOW-0102

### REQ-021-04-13: Insurance — Employer's Liability
Employer's Liability insurance with limits of not less than $5,000,000 per employee per accident.
- **Source:** RFP §4.2.4; Decomposition SOW-0102

### REQ-021-04-14: Insurance — Additional Insured
All insurance policies (except E&O) must name Camrose County as additional insured and include a severability of interests or cross-liability clause.
- **Source:** RFP §4.2.6; Decomposition SOW-0103

### REQ-021-04-15: Insurance — Cancellation Notice
No insurance policy may be cancelled without the insurer providing at least thirty (30) days written notice of cancellation to the County.
- **Source:** RFP §4.2.6

### REQ-021-04-16: Insurance — Evidence
Upon the County's request, the Design-Builder must furnish written documentation satisfactory to the County evidencing required insurance coverage.
- **Source:** RFP §4.2.6

### REQ-021-04-17: Prime Contractor Designation
The Design-Builder shall complete a Prime Contractor Designation form issued by the County, confirming Prime Contractor status under Health and Safety agency requirements for the full project duration.
- **Source:** RFP §2.15
- **Note (A-004):** The specific Alberta OH&S legislation governing Prime Contractor obligations is not cited in the RFP. **ASSUMPTION:** The governing legislation is likely the Alberta Occupational Health and Safety Act, RSA 2000, c O-2.1, and the Occupational Health and Safety Regulation, AR 62/2003 (or successor instruments). Specific citation TBD — text not directly accessible.

### REQ-021-04-18: Payment — Invoice Cycle
Invoices shall be submitted at least every thirty-one (31) days.
- **Source:** RFP §2.13.2

### REQ-021-04-19: Payment — Terms
The Owner shall make payment within twenty-eight (28) days of receiving a proper invoice.
- **Source:** RFP §2.13.2

### REQ-021-04-20: Lien Holdback
Ten percent (10%) of the value of Work performed shall be withheld from each payment in accordance with the Prompt Payment and Construction Lien Act (Alberta), released upon project completion and satisfaction of conditions per RFP §2.13.2.
- **Source:** RFP §2.13.2
- **Note (X-004):** "Confirm legislative compliance" requires verification of holdback administration in accordance with the Act's requirements (e.g., proper trust account provisions if applicable, timely release conditions, adjudication provisions). Specific compliance checklist TBD pending access to the Act's full text.

### REQ-021-04-21: Guarantee Period
The Proponent guarantees all materials and Work for the construction period plus two (2) years following the construction completion date noted on the Construction Completion Certificate (CCC).
- **Source:** RFP §2.10.2
- **Note:** Guarantee period terms are established in this contract but ongoing administration transitions to DEL-021-05. See Guidance Considerations for handoff.

### REQ-021-04-22: Deficiency Rectification
Defects or deficiencies identified during the Guarantee Period shall be rectified within ten (10) days of the Owner's written instruction. All costs are borne by the Design-Builder.
- **Source:** RFP §2.10.6, §2.10.7

### REQ-021-04-23: Contract Administration Setup
Prior to or immediately upon contract execution, contract administration procedures shall be established, including coordination of inspections, weekly meetings, and billing through the County project manager.
- **Source:** _CONTEXT.md (Success Criteria); RFP §3.1
- **ASSUMPTION:** Specific contract administration procedures and formats are to be agreed upon with the County project manager at contract award. County project manager identity is confirmed at project award (Decomposition SOW-0303).
- **Minimum content requirements (ASSUMPTION — D-003):** The contract administration procedures document shall address at minimum: (a) weekly meeting schedule and format, (b) invoice submission process and cycle, (c) inspection request and reporting process, (d) change order process (per CCDC 14-2013 general conditions — TBD pending standard form access), (e) deficiency notification and rectification process, (f) deficiency holdback administration, (g) lien holdback administration, (h) document control and records management system, and (i) County project manager communication protocols. Items for which specific procedures cannot be defined at contract execution shall be noted as TBD with a resolution timeline.

### REQ-021-04-24: Contract Distribution
Fully executed contract copies shall be distributed to all required parties.
- **Source:** _CONTEXT.md (Success Criteria)
- **ASSUMPTION:** The distribution list and number of copies required are TBD pending contract award and County instruction. Minimum recipients to be confirmed — see Procedure Step 8.1 for candidate list.

### REQ-021-04-25: Completion Deadline
All Work must be completed on or before December 31, 2026.
- **Source:** RFP §3.1 (emphasized in bold); Decomposition header

### REQ-021-04-26: Supplementary Conditions
Any project-specific modifications to CCDC 14-2013 general conditions shall be documented as Supplementary Conditions and included in the contract execution package. If no modifications are required, this shall be documented.
- **Source:** Procedure Step 3.6; Guidance Principle 2
- **ASSUMPTION (C-002):** Supplementary Conditions are a standard component of CCDC contract execution. Whether modifications are needed is TBD pending contract negotiation. The requirement to document the outcome (modified or unmodified) ensures completeness of the contract record.
