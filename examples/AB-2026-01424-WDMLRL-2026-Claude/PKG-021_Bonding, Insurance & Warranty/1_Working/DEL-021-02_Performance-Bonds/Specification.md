## Scope

### What This Deliverable Covers

DEL-021-02 covers the arrangement, execution, and delivery of the Performance Bond and Labour & Material Payment Bond required by the Owner (Camrose County) as conditions of contract execution under the CCDC 14–2013 Design-Build Stipulated Price Contract.

This deliverable encompasses:
- Procurement and execution of a Performance Bond (50% of Contract price)
- Procurement and execution of a Labour & Material Payment Bond (50% of Contract price)
- Delivery of both executed bonds to the Owner within the required timeline
- Surety company selection and qualification confirmation

### What This Deliverable Excludes

- Bid Security (10% bid bond / certified cheque and Consent of Surety) — covered by DEL-021-01
- Insurance coverage (CGL, auto, E&O, employer's liability, WCB) — covered by DEL-021-03
- Contract execution itself (CCDC 14–2013 signing) — covered by DEL-021-04
- Warranty / guarantee period obligations — covered by DEL-021-05

## Requirements

### REQ-021-02-001: Performance Bond Required
**Statement:** The Design-Builder shall provide a Performance Bond in the amount of fifty percent (50%) of the Contract price.
**Purpose:** Ensures faithful performance of the Contract.
**Source:** RFP §2.12.1.1
**SOW Reference:** SOW-0099

### REQ-021-02-002: Labour & Material Payment Bond Required
**Statement:** The Design-Builder shall provide a Labour and Material Payment Bond in the amount of fifty percent (50%) of the Contract price.
**Purpose:** Ensures payment of all labour and material used or reasonably required in the performance of the Contract.
**Source:** RFP §2.12.1.2
**SOW Reference:** SOW-0100

### REQ-021-02-003: Bond Delivery Timeline
**Statement:** Both bonds must be provided with the executed Contract Agreement within ten (10) days of being notified of the award of Contract.
**Source:** RFP §2.12.3
**SOW Reference:** SOW-0101

### REQ-021-02-004: Surety Company Licensing
**Statement:** Bonds must be issued by a surety company licensed to conduct business in the province or territory wherein the Work is located (Alberta).
**Source:** RFP §2.12.2

### REQ-021-02-005: Bond Form Acceptability
**Statement:** Bonds must be provided on forms acceptable to the Owner and compatible with the forms contained within the Contract documents (CCDC 14–2013).
**Source:** RFP §2.12.2
**Enrichment note (Lensing Item A-002):** The specific bond form standard is not mandated in accessible RFP text. CCDC A312 forms are marked ASSUMPTION in the Standards table and in Datasheet Construction. The Owner must confirm form acceptability in writing before bond execution to avoid form rejection. This confirmation should be obtained during Procedure Step 3.

### REQ-021-02-006: Bond Amounts Based on Contract Price
**Statement:** Bond amounts (50% each for Performance and Payment) are calculated based on the final negotiated Contract price under CCDC 14–2013.
**Note:** Contract price is TBD at time of drafting; bonds cannot be finalized until contract award.
**Source:** Derived from RFP §2.12.1.1, §2.12.1.2, and §2.7

### REQ-021-02-007: Bond Concurrence with Contract Execution
**Statement:** ASSUMPTION — Both bonds are to be executed concurrently with or immediately prior to the execution of the CCDC 14–2013 Contract Agreement. The explicit sequencing requirement (bonds "at the time of" contract execution, delivered within 10 days of award) is stated in RFP §2.12.1 and §2.12.3. Treat as a hard requirement.
**Source:** RFP §2.12.1 ("at the time of his execution of the Contract Agreement"), §2.12.3

### REQ-021-02-008: Bond Duration (Enrichment — Lensing Item A-001)
**Statement:** TBD — Bond duration/expiry period must be defined. Bonds are assumed to remain in force for the Contract duration (construction period), but the exact term length, expiry conditions, and renewal obligations are not explicitly stated in accessible sources.
**Action required:** Confirm bond duration requirements with the Owner per RFP full text or CCDC 14-2013 contract terms.
**Source:** ASSUMPTION based on RFP §2.12 intent; Datasheet Conditions (Duration row). Lensing Item A-001.
