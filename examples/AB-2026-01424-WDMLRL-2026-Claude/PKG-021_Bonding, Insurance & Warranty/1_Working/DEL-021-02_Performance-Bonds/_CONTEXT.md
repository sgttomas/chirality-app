# DEL-021-02: Performance & Payment Bonds

## Deliverable Overview
**ID:** DEL-021-02_Performance-Bonds
**Package:** PKG-021 — Bonding, Insurance & Warranty
**Title:** Performance & Payment Bonds
**Category:** Commercial
**Responsible Party:** Project Manager / Finance

## Scope Definition
Arrangement and execution of performance and payment bonds required for contract execution including surety selection, bond documentation, and bonding company authorization to ensure project completion and payment of suppliers and workers.

## Linked SOW Items
- SOW-0099
- SOW-0100
- SOW-0101

## Linked Objectives
- OBJ-008 (Commercial & Legal Compliance)

## Regulatory Context
Based on Main RFP §2.10 (Performance and Payment Bonds)

## Key Roles
- **Project Manager:** Bond coordination and contract integration
- **Finance:** Surety arrangement and bond execution

## Success Criteria
- Performance bond obtained and properly executed
- Payment bond obtained and properly executed
- Surety authorized and accepted
- Bond documents attached to contract
- Bonding maintained throughout contract period
