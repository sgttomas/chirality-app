### Bond Coverage Description

**Performance Bond (SOW-0099):**
Covers the faithful performance of the Contract. Required to ensure the Design-Builder completes the Work per contract terms.
Source: RFP §2.12.1.1

**Labour & Material Payment Bond (SOW-0100):**
Covers payment of all labour and material used or reasonably required in the performance of the Contract. Protects subcontractors and material suppliers.
Source: RFP §2.12.1.2

**Bonding Execution Timeline (SOW-0101):**
Both bonds must be provided with the executed Contract Agreement within 10 days of receiving the award notification.
Source: RFP §2.12.3
