# DEL-021-03: Insurance Package

## Deliverable Overview
**ID:** DEL-021-03_Insurance
**Package:** PKG-021 — Bonding, Insurance & Warranty
**Title:** Insurance Package
**Category:** Commercial
**Responsible Party:** Project Manager / Finance

## Scope Definition
Arrangement of required insurance coverages including general liability, workers compensation, equipment insurance, and project-specific insurance requirements with proper certificates of insurance, endorsements, and owner protection.

- SOW-0102
- SOW-0103

## Regulatory Context
Based on Main RFP §2.12 (Insurance Requirements)

## Key Roles
- **Project Manager:** Insurance coordination and compliance verification
- **Finance:** Insurance procurement and documentation

## Success Criteria
- All required insurance policies obtained
- Coverage limits meet or exceed RFP requirements
- Certificates of insurance provided
- Owner named as additional insured where required
- Insurance maintained throughout project duration
