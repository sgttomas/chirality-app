# DEL-021-05: Guarantee Period Obligations

## Deliverable Overview
**ID:** DEL-021-05_Warranty
**Package:** PKG-021 — Bonding, Insurance & Warranty
**Title:** Guarantee Period Obligations

Management and documentation of warranty and guarantee period obligations including deficiency tracking, remediation scheduling, contractor performance monitoring, and warranty expiration management throughout the guarantee period.
