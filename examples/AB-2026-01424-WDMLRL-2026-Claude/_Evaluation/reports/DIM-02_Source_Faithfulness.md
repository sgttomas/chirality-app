## Overall Dimension Assessment

### Score: EXEMPLARY

**Justification:**

All six checks in Dimension 2 pass. The content digests demonstrate source faithfulness that exceeds minimum conformance in three specific areas:

**1. Completeness of building program capture (S-2.1):** All 22 distinct §3.4 design requirements and all §3.1 spatial elements are represented in the deliverable set. The decomposition covers the full building program systematically rather than selectively.

**2. Addenda propagation discipline (S-2.4):** Addendum 4 changes are not merely noted — they are operationalized. The SCA-001 (2026-03-26) dependency refresh cycle provides a structured mechanism for addenda incorporation, and DEL-002-07 demonstrates the highest quality of addenda handling by converting a prior TBD (corbel vs. free-standing) directly into a resolved specification requirement, with the Addendum 4 source cited explicitly.

**3. Epistemological transparency (S-2.5, S-2.6):** The consistent use of TBD labels, ASSUMPTION flags with rationale, and escalation to County authority (rather than silent resolution) for ambiguous items reflects a mature evidence-first epistemology. In no case across the 12-package sample did a deliverable invent a mandatory requirement not traceable to RFP or addenda text.

**Observations (not failures):**

- The "2–5 tonne" crane language in RFP §3.4 is interpreted uniformly as "two cranes each of 5-tonne capacity." This interpretation is consistent with Appendix B floor plan geometry showing two crane bays and is reasonable, but the RFP text is technically ambiguous (it could be read as a range 2 to 5 tonnes for a single crane). This is flagged as an OBSERVATION: the interpretation is defensible and consistently applied, and merits confirmation from County at design approval.

- The mezzanine railing change from Addendum 4 (no walls, standard steel safety railing with 10-ft sliding gate) is captured at the architectural/decomposition level in DEL-001-01 but is not yet propagated to the specific mezzanine construction or fit-out deliverables. This is appropriate for the current project stage (preliminary design) and does not constitute a failure, as mezzanine railing detail belongs to DEL-002-05 and interior fit-out packages which are downstream of the current design gate.

- DEL-002-01 flags CON-001 (mezzanine extent — Appendix B shows parts room + utility room, while RFP/SOW references include wash bay) as unresolved. This is a legitimate source conflict that is correctly escalated to County ruling rather than resolved by the agent. This is not a source faithfulness failure; it is an appropriate handling of a genuine ambiguity in the source material.
