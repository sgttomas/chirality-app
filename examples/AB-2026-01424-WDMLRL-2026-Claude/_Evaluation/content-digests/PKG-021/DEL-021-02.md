### Scope
DEL-021-02 covers the arrangement, execution, and delivery of:
- **Performance Bond:** 50% of Contract price (ensures faithful contract performance)
- **Labour & Material Payment Bond:** 50% of Contract price (ensures subcontractor and supplier payment)
- Both bonds must be delivered to Owner within 10 days of contract award notification (RFP §2.12.3)
- Surety company selection, qualification, and bond form coordination

**Excludes:** Bid security (DEL-021-01), Insurance (DEL-021-03), Contract execution itself (DEL-021-04), Warranty obligations (DEL-021-05)
