### Scope
Complete renovation of the existing 250 sq ft kitchenette area within the Old North Shop building. Work includes:
- Demolition and removal of existing fixtures, cabinetry, and finishes
- Plumbing and electrical rough-in updates/extensions
- Installation of new cabinetry and appliances
- Application of interior finishes per approved finish schedule
