### Scope Summary
The deliverable covers comprehensive renovation of the existing mezzanine within the Old North Shop (105 ft × 40 ft building). The mezzanine is located at the 2nd level above the washroom and is currently used as a coffee room (break room). Renovation scope includes structural assessment and repairs, flooring replacement, stairs/railing safety upgrades, electrical updates, and finishing work. Full scope detail is pending structural assessment (STRUCT-ASSESS-001), existing documentation (EXISTING-DOCS-001), and approved design (DESIGN-001).
