### Scope Summary
DEL-018-05 covers the complete design-build construction of wash bay drainage infrastructure:
- Floor drain(s) installation within the 24'-wide enclosed wash bay
- Drainage piping connecting wash bay floor drain(s) to exterior mud sump
- Excavation and construction of exterior mud sump pit (east side of building, adjacent to wash bay)
- System designed for cleanout by excavator
