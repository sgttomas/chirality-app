### Scope

- Receipt and placement of Owner-supplied gravel aggregate
- Spreading and compaction to design specifications
- All labour, equipment, and supervision for surface preparation
- Coordination with civil design (PKG-005) and handoff to operations (PKG-016, PKG-017)
