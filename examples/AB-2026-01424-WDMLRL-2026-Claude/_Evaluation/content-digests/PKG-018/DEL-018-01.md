### Scope
DEL-018-01 covers mechanical removal and temporary on-site stockpiling of existing topsoil from all driving surfaces at the project site to expose competent subgrade for subsequent grading and construction activities. This is a foundational, safety-critical site preparation activity that is first in sequence within PKG-018 and prerequisite to all subsequent sitework deliverables (DEL-018-02 through DEL-018-06).

**In Scope:**
- Mechanical stripping and excavation of topsoil layer from driving surfaces
- Temporary on-site stockpiling for future landscaping reuse
- Subgrade exposure for grading and pad construction

**Excluded (County/Landfill Responsibility):**
- Brushing and clearing (SOW-0200)
- Bulk cut and fill (SOW-0201)
- Aggregate/gravel supply (SOW-0203)
