### Identity

### Scope
- **Description:** Architectural drawing set documenting the renovation of the existing Old North Shop building covering five distinct scope items: kitchenette renovation (250 sq ft), mezzanine renovation, washroom renovation and expansion, new locker/change room with laundry services.
- **Acceptance Criteria:**
