### Scope
DEL-006-04 covers the detailed engineering drawing set for:
- Cistern sizing, structural integration, and plumbing connections (minimum 50,000 L capacity, TBD material/installation type)
- Internal service distribution pump (100 LPM minimum, 2.5" filling connection)
- Bulk water fill system with external high-volume pump
- Pressure-side distribution piping from cistern to building distribution
- Control system provisions for cistern level and pump operation
- Electrical interface information for pump power
- Installation and maintenance access provisions

**Out of scope:** water distribution throughout building, drain/vent plans, septic system, fixture schedule, plumbing calculations, specification document, electrical design, structural design, civil sitework.
