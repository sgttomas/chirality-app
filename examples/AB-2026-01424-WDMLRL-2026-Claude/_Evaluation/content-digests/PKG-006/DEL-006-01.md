- System layout and design parameters for water supply (cistern-fed), drainage, and septic systems
- Preliminary specification outlines for materials, equipment, and fixture selections
- Design intent statements and documentation sufficient for County approval
- Coordination baseline for structural, civil, and mechanical disciplines
