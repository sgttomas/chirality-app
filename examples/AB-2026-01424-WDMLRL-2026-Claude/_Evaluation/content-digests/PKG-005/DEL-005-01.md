### Scope
DEL-005-01 covers preliminary-phase civil engineering for a landfill expansion site (SW 14–44–21–W4 near Ferintosh, Alberta):
- Preliminary site grading approach and drainage strategy
- Preliminary driving surface layout (gravel surface for motor scraper class equipment)
- Cement pad and gravel pad layout consistent with Appendix B conceptual drawings
- Topsoil stripping strategy
- Stormwater/drainage approach ensuring positive site drainage without altering neighboring property conditions
- Exterior wash bay mud sump design approach
- Design narrative and presentation materials for County approval

**Exclusions:** Final IFC drawings, structural design (except mud sump coordination), landscape planting, utility routing, County-scope items (brushing/clearing, bulk cut/fill, aggregate supply), and geotechnical investigation.
