1. **Design Gate (Critical):** Installation cannot commence until IFC plumbing drawings (DEL-006-05) are approved. Conceptual location confirmed (north side of New North Shop), but precise invert elevations, connection details, and bedding specification are TBD.

2. **Environmental/Regulatory Compliance:** Existing tank removal is subject to Alberta Environmental Protection and Enhancement Act (EPEA) and Alberta Waste Control Regulation. Specific disposal requirements and Authority Having Jurisdiction (AHJ) identity TBD pending confirmation by environmental consultant or County.

3. **Service Continuity Risk (Procedure Step 1.7):** If the existing septic system currently has active service connections, a temporary wastewater management plan is required before removal to avoid service interruption. This must be confirmed prior to design completion.

4. **Frost Protection (Central Alberta Context):** Winterization and frost protection are essential for this remote site. Minimum burial depth, tank insulation, and access riser protection must be specified by Plumbing Engineer of Record in coordination with geotechnical report. Specification includes detailed requirements (REQ-014-02-10, not fully shown in excerpt) and Guidance § Winterization.

5. **Electrical Conditional (TBD):** High-level alarm may be code-required for holding tanks under Alberta Safety Codes. If required, scope assignment and coordination with PKG-015 must be resolved. Current confidence: LOW; flagged for code confirmation.

6. **Tank Material Selection (TBD):** Material (fiberglass, polyethylene, or concrete) affects installation equipment, bedding requirements, and lifecycle longevity (service life 20–30 years for PE/FRP; longer for concrete but prone to hydrogen sulfide corrosion). Material submittal approval required before installation (Procedure P-06).

7. **Pump-Out Frequency & Lifecycle Cost (Guidance § Pump-Out Access and Frequency [A-005]):** Expected pump-out frequency depends on daily wastewater generation (tied to fixture count and usage in DEL-014-06). PROPOSAL noted for Plumbing Engineer or Owner operations staff to estimate frequency and annual cost prior to final tank sizing.

8. **Inspection Hold Point (Procedure Step 3.8 [F-003]):** County project representative must inspect tank placement, orientation, invert elevations, and connections in the open excavation before backfill; photographed as concealed-work documentation.
