**SOW Reference:** SOW-0017

### Scope
DEL-007-03 comprises the complete planting and restoration plans drawing set for the WDMLRL Maintenance Shop Addition expansion area. The deliverable covers:

- Plant palette development with species selection appropriate to central Alberta climate and landfill site conditions
- Planting zone drawings with plant locations, quantities, and callouts
- Restoration strategy for disturbed areas
- Native vegetation establishment phasing plan
- Plant schedule (botanical and common names, grades, quantities)
- Planting details and standard specifications

The deliverable **excludes** civil grading (PKG-005), construction execution (PKG-018), brushing/clearing, and bulk earthwork (County responsibility).
