- **Location**: SW 14–44–21–W4, Camrose County, Alberta

### Scope
DEL-007-02 establishes comprehensive graphic representation of the landscape and site layout at design development and construction document (IFC) levels. The deliverable covers:
- Site layout and spatial organization around the New North Shop addition (130' × 100') and existing Old North Shop (105' × 40')
- Site grading framework coordinated with Civil design (positive drainage, off-site drainage constraint compliance)
- Hardscape elements: gravel driving surface (designed for motor scraper-class equipment), cement pads, gravel pad, and site circulation
- Wash Bay Mud Sump location (exterior, east of New North Shop per conceptual baseline)
- Topsoil stripping indication for all driving surfaces
- Establishment of spatial framework for downstream DEL-007-03 (Planting & Restoration Plans) and DEL-007-04 (Landscape Specification)

**Exclusions**: Civil grading engineering calculations, construction execution (PKG-018), detailed planting palette, material specifications, brushing/clearing, bulk cut/fill, and aggregate supply (all out of Proponent scope per RFP §3.3.1).
