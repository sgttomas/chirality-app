### Scope
The deliverable covers the full lifecycle of obtaining a building permit from Camrose County for the WDMLRL Maintenance Shop Addition:
- Preparation and assembly of building permit application package
- Coordination with Camrose County building authority throughout review and approval
- Receipt and documentation of issued permit
- Recording and tracking of all permit conditions and requirements
- Coordination of inspections with County representative presence
- Provision of completed inspection report copies to County

Out of scope: permit fee payment (County responsibility), development permit (DEL-009-01), safety code permits (DEL-009-03), detailed compliance tracking (DEL-009-04).
