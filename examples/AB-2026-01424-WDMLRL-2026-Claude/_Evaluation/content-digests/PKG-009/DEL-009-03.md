### Scope
This deliverable covers all activities required to obtain and manage Safety Code permits for the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop Addition, including:
- Identification of all applicable Alberta Safety Code permit requirements
- Identification of Safety Codes Council accredited agencies responsible for each permit category
- Preparation and submission of all Safety Code permit applications
- Coordination with Fire Marshal, Electrical Inspector, OH&S regulatory body, Building Inspector, and other authorities
- Coordination of all required inspections (County project representative must be present at all)
- Provision of completed inspection report copies to the County
- Prime Contractor designation assumption and fulfillment for the full project duration
- Maintenance of all Safety Code certifications and compliance documentation

**Explicitly Excluded:** Permit fee payment (County responsibility); compliance register tracking (DEL-009-04); building code compliance design (PKG-001 through PKG-007); OH&S program management during construction (DEL-019-01).
