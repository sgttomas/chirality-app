### Scope
The deliverable provides centralized tracking of all regulatory compliance requirements, permit conditions, inspections, and corrective actions throughout the project lifecycle. It consolidates conditions from three permit types (Development, Building, and Safety Code permits) and maintains a living operational log through construction and the guarantee period.

**Key scope elements:**
- Centralized register of all permits and conditions from DEL-009-01, DEL-009-02, DEL-009-03
- Inspection scheduling, request submission, and outcome tracking
- County project representative notification and attendance confirmation (mandatory per RFP §3.3.2)
- Deficiency documentation and corrective action workflow
- Compliance evidence documentation and audit trail
- 10-day deficiency rectification tracking (guarantee period per RFP §2.10.6)
