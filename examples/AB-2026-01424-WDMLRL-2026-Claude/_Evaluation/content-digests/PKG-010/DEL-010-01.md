### Key Quality Observations

1. **Variable-Price Gate:** This is a variable-price scope item. Final cost negotiated post-geotechnical report. Preliminary estimate assumes normal ground conditions — must be flagged as preliminary with significant uncertainty range.

2. **Geotechnical Criticality:** All foundation design parameters (type, depth, reinforcement) are gated by PKG-008. No construction commitments should bypass geotech findings.

3. **Embedded Items Risk:** Missed embedments (anchor bolts, conduit sleeves) in concrete are disproportionately costly to remediate. Structural drawings must be reviewed thoroughly before formwork is set.

4. **Schedule Exposure:** Foundation is on critical path to Dec 31, 2026 completion deadline. Delayed geotech mobilization compounds schedule compression and reduces negotiation time for variable-price scope.

5. **County Interface:** Site handoff condition (after County's bulk cut/fill and clearing) must be documented at mobilization. Any discrepancies should be raised with County project manager.

6. **Service Pit Scope Ambiguity:** Service pit scope boundary (PKG-010 vs PKG-011) is TBD per Conflict Table CF-010-01 in Guidance.md. Scope ownership unresolved.
