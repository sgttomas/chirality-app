### Scope

**In Scope:** Complete supply and installation of LED lighting fixtures, branch circuit wiring, switching/control devices, and supporting hardware across five zones:
- Z-1 (Main Shop): 20 high-bay LED fixtures (5 rows x 4)
- Z-2 (Wash Bay): 6 high-bay LED fixtures (2 rows x 3)
- Z-3 (Office): 1 x 8-foot LED linear fixture
- Z-4 (Utility Room): 2 x 8-foot LED linear fixtures
- Z-5 (Parts/Tool Room): 6 x 8-foot LED linear fixtures
- **Total: 35 fixtures**

**Out of Scope:** Three-phase power service (DEL-015-01), receptacles (DEL-015-03), equipment power circuits (DEL-015-04), low-voltage systems (DEL-015-05), ceiling fans, Old North Shop lighting, and emergency/exit lighting (unresolved scope).
