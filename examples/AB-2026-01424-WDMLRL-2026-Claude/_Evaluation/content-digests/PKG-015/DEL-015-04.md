#### Scope
The deliverable encompasses dedicated power circuits for:
- Two (2) 5-tonne overhead cranes (circuits C-01, C-02)
- Overhead doors (circuit C-03)
- Air compressor, 40 A (circuit C-04)
- Fire hose pump, 70 A (circuit C-05)
- Pressure washer, 70 A (circuit C-06)
- Exhaust fan(s) (circuit C-07)

All work includes conductors, conduit/raceway, overcurrent protection, disconnecting means, and panel connections per IFC electrical design.
