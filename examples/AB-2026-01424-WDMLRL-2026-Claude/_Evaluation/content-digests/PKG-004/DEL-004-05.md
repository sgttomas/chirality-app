### Scope
**Covers:** Graphical layout and annotation of all general-purpose and specialized electrical receptacle outlets (15 A/120 V through 50 A/240 V) in the new addition, including legend, dimensional coordination with architectural floor plans, and special installation notes (GFCI, wet-location ratings, mounting heights).

**Excludes:** Lighting fixture layout (DEL-004-04), single-line diagram and main service (DEL-004-02), hardwired equipment power circuits (DEL-004-03), panel schedules (DEL-004-06), low-voltage/communications (DEL-004-07), and electrical work in Old North Shop renovation beyond explicit scope (TBD).

**Key Scope Boundary:** Receptacles are general-purpose or designated outlets into which portable equipment plugs; hardwired equipment circuits (compressor 40 A, pumps 70 A) fall under DEL-004-03. High-amperage receptacles serving portable equipment (e.g., 50 A/240 V for welding) are within DEL-004-05 scope.
