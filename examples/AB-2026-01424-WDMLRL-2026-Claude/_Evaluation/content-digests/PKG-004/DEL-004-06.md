### Scope
Panel Schedules encompasses complete, signed, stamped electrical panel schedule document(s) for the New North Shop Addition. Deliverable covers all panels/distribution boards serving the addition, enumerating each circuit breaker position with load description, circuit designation, breaker rating, conductor sizing, voltage, and connected/demand load. Must be consistent with and traceable to upstream deliverables (DEL-004-02 Single-Line Diagram, DEL-004-03 Power Distribution Plans, DEL-004-04 Lighting Plans, DEL-004-05 Receptacle Layout Plans, DEL-004-08 Electrical Calculation Package). Excludes electrical calculations (DEL-004-08), single-line diagram (DEL-004-02), and physical installation (PKG-015).
