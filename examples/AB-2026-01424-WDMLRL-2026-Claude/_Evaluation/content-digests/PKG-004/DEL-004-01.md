### Scope

**What This Deliverable Covers:**
- Preliminary-level electrical design documentation for the New North Shop Addition (~13,000 sq ft, 35' ceiling height)
- Design coverage: three-phase power distribution, lighting layout, receptacle layout, dedicated equipment circuits (cranes, overhead doors, compressor, fire hose pump, pressure washer), exhaust fans, ceiling fans, and low-voltage systems
- Conceptual coordination with architectural, structural, and mechanical disciplines
- Compliance with Alberta Safety Codes and Alberta Building Code at preliminary level
- Sufficient for County project team review and approval (RFP §3.3.2)

**What This Deliverable Does NOT Cover:**
- Final Issued-for-Construction (IFC) electrical drawings (DEL-004-02 through DEL-004-09)
- Electrical load calculations (DEL-004-08)
- Panel schedules (DEL-004-06)
- Mechanical equipment selection (PKG-003)
- Site electrical service route design beyond building entry (PKG-018)
- Electrical Safety Code permit application (PKG-009)
