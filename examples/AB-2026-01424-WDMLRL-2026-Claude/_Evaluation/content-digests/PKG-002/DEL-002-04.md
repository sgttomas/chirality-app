### Scope - Description, Acceptance Criteria, SOW IDs, OBJ IDs
**Description:** Cross-sectional views and construction-level detail drawings translating structural framing plans (DEL-002-03) and design calculations (DEL-002-10) into final IFC drawings. Covers 35 ft clear ceiling concrete structure, including sections through repair bays, wash bay, service pit/trench, mezzanine framing connections, crane runway support, steel plate embedments, and stairs.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- All structural elements identified in SOW-0012 are fully detailed across PKG-002 drawing set.
- All drawings signed/stamped by P.Eng. licensed in Alberta.
- Details traceable to DEL-002-10 (Structural Calculation Package).
- Section cut schedule coordinated with DEL-002-03 (Framing Plans).
- Interface details completed with companion deliverables and external inputs (crane supplier, County confirmations).

**SOW IDs:** SOW-0012 (Complete final structural design — concrete structure, 35 ft ceiling)
