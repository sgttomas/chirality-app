### Scope — Description, Acceptance Criteria, SOW IDs, OBJ IDs
**Description:** Engineering justification record for all structural subsystems of a 130 ft × 100 ft, 35 ft clear-height concrete industrial maintenance facility with heavy equipment loading, crane runway support, mezzanine, service pit, and variable-price foundation design.

**Structural subsystems covered:**
- Concrete superstructure (gravity and lateral systems)
- Foundation system (normal ground conditions basis; post-geotech reconciliation)
- Mezzanine structure (load-bearing over parts room, utility room, wash bay)
- Crane support structure (two 5-tonne overhead cranes on trolley)
- Steel plate embedments in floor slab (tracked/packer equipment)
- Below-grade service pit/trench
- Wash bay enclosure framing
- Stair structure

**Acceptance criteria:**
- All structural subsystems per RFP §3.4 and App B addressed in calculations
- Concrete superstructure designed for 35 ft clear height (REQ-002)
- Foundation design based on normal ground conditions with post-geotech reconciliation trigger defined (REQ-004, REQ-005)
- Mezzanine designed for heavy stored items (oil totes, pumping equipment) (REQ-006)
- Crane runway calculations include manufacturer data (or conservative 5-tonne assumptions flagged) (REQ-007)
- Steel plate embedments verified with limit-state checks (punching shear, bearing, flexure) (REQ-008)
- Service pit design includes lateral earth pressure and conditional hydrostatic loading (REQ-009)
- All calculations performed per NBC, CSA A23.3, CSA S16, Alberta Safety Codes (REQ-010)
- Code edition specificity confirmed and documented (REQ-010)

**SOW IDs:** SOW-0012 (Complete final structural design), SOW-0019 (Design foundation — variable-price post-geotech)

**OBJ IDs:** OBJ-001 (Code-compliant functional facility), OBJ-003 (Complete P.Eng.-stamped IFC documentation), OBJ-006 (Foundation cost reconciled post-geotech)
