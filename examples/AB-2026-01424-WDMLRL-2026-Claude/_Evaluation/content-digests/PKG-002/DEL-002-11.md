### Scope - Description, Acceptance Criteria, SOW IDs, OBJ IDs
**Description:** Foundation design for the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop Addition (130 ft × 100 ft, 35 ft ceiling, concrete structure). Covers the complete foundation system design with dual-phase delivery: (1) proposal-phase variable-price cost estimate based on assumed normal geotechnical conditions, (2) post-award IFC-ready design following geotechnical report receipt.

**SOW ID:** SOW-0019 — "Design foundation (pricing negotiated post-geotech; estimate based on normal ground conditions)"

**OBJ IDs:** 
- OBJ-003: Produce complete P.Eng.-stamped IFC design documentation
- OBJ-006: Variable-price foundation cost reconciliation

**Scope Boundaries:** Excludes foundation construction (DEL-010-01), superstructure design (DEL-002-03 through DEL-002-09), geotechnical investigation (DEL-008-01), civil grading (PKG-005), and Old North Shop renovation (PKG-017).
