### Scope
The deliverable covers physical construction of an enclosed wash bay within the New North Shop addition, including:
- Walls enclosing the wash bay on all sides, integrated with building envelope
- Roof integration as part of overall building structure
- One overhead door (24 ft wide) for vehicle access
- Embedded steel plate(s) in concrete floor
- Structural provisions for mezzanine above wash bay
- All elements required to form weathertight, enclosed bay per IFC drawings

**Excluded items:** floor drains/mud sump (DEL-018-05), electrical installation (DEL-015-02/04), mezzanine framing/stairs (DEL-011-07), foundation (DEL-010-01), plumbing connections (DEL-014-04/06), mechanical/ventilation (DEL-013 package)
