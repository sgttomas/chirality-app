## Report: DEL-011-02 Steel Plate Floor Embedments

### Identity
- **Deliverable ID:** DEL-011-02
- **Name:** Steel Plate Floor Embedments
- **Package:** PKG-011 — Building Structure & Envelope
- **Discipline:** General Contractor (construction)
- **Type:** Construction deliverable
- **Covers SOW:** SOW-0024 (Supply and installation of steel plate embedments in repair bay floors)
- **Supports:** OBJ-001
- **Project:** West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop Addition (Camrose County, Alberta)

### Scope
**Included:** Supply and installation of structural steel plates cast into concrete floor slabs of repair bays and wash bay to provide bearing surfaces for tracked and packer-type heavy equipment; coordination with concrete formwork and slab pour; inspection and load rating certification.

**Excluded:** Structural design (DEL-002-08/10/12), concrete slab design (DEL-011-01), equipment installation (PKG-012/016), wash bay drainage (DEL-018-05).

**Key Boundary Issue:** Wash bay plate scope boundary with wash bay enclosure (SOW-0027a) and drainage (DEL-018-05) requires resolution via CON-011-02-03.
