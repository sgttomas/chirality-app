### Scope
This drawing set documents the complete exhaust and ventilation systems for all identified exhaust sources within the building, including:
- Heavy equipment exhaust hose and fan systems in two drive-through repair bays (24' wide each)
- Welding station exhaust/ventilation system
- Service pit ventilation (below-grade)
- Wash bay exhaust/ventilation system
- General building exhaust fans
- Ceiling fan interaction assessment with exhaust capture systems
- Coordination with high-volume air exchanger and forced-air makeup air system
