### Scope
This drawing set documents the routing, sizing, and layout of ductwork and air distribution systems for the ~13,000 sq ft, 35 ft ceiling maintenance shop addition, including:
- Supply air distribution ductwork from forced-air makeup air (MUA) unit, high-volume air exchanger, and high-recovery heating system
- Air distribution to all served spaces: main repair bays, wash bay, service pit, office, parts room, utility room, and mezzanine (extent TBD)
- Duct sizing schedules and coordination interfaces with structural and architectural systems

**Excludes:** Mechanical equipment selection (DEL-003-02), exhaust systems (DEL-003-04), calculations (DEL-003-06), equipment schedule (DEL-003-05), and mechanical specification (DEL-003-07).
