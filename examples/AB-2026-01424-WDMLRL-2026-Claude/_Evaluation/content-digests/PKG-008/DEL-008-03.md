### Scope
The construction survey establishes horizontal and vertical control, positioning, grades, and alignment for the WDMLRL Maintenance Shop Addition. It translates final Issued for Construction (IFC) design drawings to physical field control and guides construction operations. Scope includes:
- Layout and alignment survey for building footprint, structural elements, pads, access routes, utility corridors
- Grade establishment for slab-on-grade, site grading, drainage, cistern installation
- Construction staking for all major building elements
- Field verification and documentation confirming constructed elements match design positions
- Progress survey updates throughout construction
