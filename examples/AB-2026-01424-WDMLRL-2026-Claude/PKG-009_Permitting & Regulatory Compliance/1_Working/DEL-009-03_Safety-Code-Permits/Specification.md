# Specification
## DEL-009-03 | Safety Code Permits

---

## Scope

### Included
This deliverable covers all activities required to obtain and manage Safety Code Permits for the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop Addition project, specifically:

- Identification of all applicable Alberta Safety Code permit requirements for the project (SOW-0007; RFP §3.3.2).
- Identification of the Safety Codes Council accredited agency (or agencies) responsible for issuing each permit category (see REQ-009-03-12).
- Preparation and submission of all applicable Safety Code permit applications to the relevant regulatory authorities.
- Coordination with all relevant Safety Code authorities (Fire Marshal, Electrical Inspector, OH&S regulatory body, Building Inspector, and any others identified by the safety code audit — anticipated additional categories include gas, plumbing, and elevator/crane per RFP §3.4).
- Coordination of all required inspections, with the County project representative present at all inspections (RFP §3.3.2).
- Provision of copies of all completed inspection reports to the County (RFP §3.3.2).
- Assumption and fulfillment of Prime Contractor designation as defined by OH&S agencies, for the full project duration (RFP §2.15; Decomposition SOW-0083).
- Completion and submission of the Prime Contractor Designation form issued by Camrose County (RFP §2.15).
- Maintenance of all Safety Code certifications and compliance documentation.
- Ensuring the building design and construction adhere to all applicable Alberta building codes, regulations, and Alberta Safety Codes (RFP §3.3.2).

### Excluded
- Payment of Safety Code permit fees — this is the County's responsibility (RFP §3.3.1; Decomposition SOW-0202).
- Tracking of permits and inspection records in the Code Compliance Register — that is the scope of DEL-009-04.
- Building code compliance design — that is the responsibility of the design disciplines (PKG-001 through PKG-007 as applicable).
- OH&S program management during construction operations — covered under DEL-019-01 (Prime Contractor Designation & OH&S Program), though Prime Contractor designation initiation is part of this deliverable. See Conflict Table C-009-03-02 in Guidance for the boundary clarification needed between DEL-009-03 and DEL-019-01.
