# Specification — DEL-009-01 | Development Permit Application & Approval

---

## Scope

### What This Deliverable Covers

DEL-009-01 covers the full lifecycle of the Development Permit for the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop Addition, from application preparation through to receipt of the approved permit. This includes:

- Preparation and assembly of the development permit application package
- Submission of the application to Camrose County
- Coordination with the regulatory authority through the review and approval process
- Receipt and documentation of the approved development permit
- Tracking and recording of any conditions attached to the permit approval
- Communication of permit conditions to downstream deliverables (DEL-009-04, construction packages)

### What This Deliverable Excludes

| Exclusion | Basis |
|---|---|
| Development permit fee payment | County responsibility — explicitly OUT of Proponent scope (R-01 §3.3.1; Decomp SOW-0202) |
| Building permit application (DEL-009-02) | Separate deliverable; sequential dependency on this deliverable |
| Safety Code permits (DEL-009-03) | Separate deliverable; parallel coordination |
| Code compliance register and inspection log (DEL-009-04) | Separate deliverable within PKG-009 |
| Brushing, clearing, bulk cut and fill | County responsibility (R-01 §3.3.1) |
| Aggregate supply | County responsibility (R-01 §3.3.1) |

## Requirements

### REQ-009-01-001 — Obligation to Obtain Development Permit
The Proponent shall obtain a development permit from Camrose County for the Maintenance Shop Addition project.

- **Basis:** R-01 §3.3.2 ("Obtaining a development permit and building permit from the County"); Decomp SOW-0005
- **Type:** Contractual obligation

### REQ-009-01-002 — Permit Application Completeness
The development permit application shall include all information and supporting documentation required by Camrose County's permitting authority. At a minimum, the application shall include the following baseline content items (regardless of authority checklist availability): site plan, legal land description, building description (footprint, height, use), and access information.

- **Basis:** R-01 §3.3.2; R-01 §3.4 (design requirements including building dimensions); standard regulatory practice
- **Note:** Specific application requirements beyond baseline are TBD — to be confirmed with the issuing authority. The baseline content items are derived from Procedure Step 3.1 and standard Alberta development permit practice.
- **Label:** ASSUMPTION — baseline content requirements inferred from standard practice and Procedure Step 3.1; authority-specific requirements not yet confirmed

### REQ-009-01-003 — Building Code Compliance
The building design submitted with or in support of the development permit application shall adhere to all applicable Alberta building codes and regulations.

- **Basis:** R-01 §3.3.2 ("The building design must adhere to all applicable building codes and regulations")
- **Type:** Regulatory and contractual requirement
- **Specific Code Identifiers:** TBD — not enumerated in available sources; to be confirmed with Camrose County permitting authority or Alberta Municipal Affairs
- **Verification Note:** Verification of code compliance requires identification of specific applicable codes; a preliminary verification step should confirm which codes apply before design review can be performed (see Verification table)

### REQ-009-01-004 — Alberta Safety Code Compliance
The building design shall adhere to all Alberta Safety Codes.

- **Basis:** R-01 §3.3.2 ("The proposed building must adhere to all Alberta Safety Codes")
- **Type:** Regulatory and contractual requirement
- **Specific Code Identifiers:** TBD — not enumerated in available sources; to be confirmed with Camrose County permitting authority or Alberta Municipal Affairs
- **Verification Note:** Verification of Safety Code compliance requires identification of specific applicable codes; a preliminary verification step should confirm which codes apply before design review can be performed (see Verification table)

### REQ-009-01-005 — Inspection Submission
The Proponent shall submit all inspection requests to the regulatory authority as required during the permit process.

- **Basis:** R-01 §3.3.2 ("Submit all inspection requests"); Decomp SOW-0084
- **Type:** Contractual obligation

### REQ-009-01-006 — County Representative at Inspections
The County project representative must be present at all inspections related to the development permit process.

- **Basis:** R-01 §3.3.2 ("The County project representative must be present for all inspections"); Decomp SOW-0084
- **Type:** Contractual obligation / coordination requirement

### REQ-009-01-007 — Inspection Report Distribution
Copies of all completed inspection reports shall be provided to the County.

- **Basis:** R-01 §3.3.2 ("shall be provided with a copy of all complete inspection reports"); Decomp SOW-0085
- **Type:** Contractual obligation

### REQ-009-01-008 — Schedule Constraint
The development permit shall be obtained in sufficient time to support construction start and overall project completion on or before December 31, 2026.

- **Basis:** R-01 §3.1 (project completion deadline)
- **Label:** ASSUMPTION — specific permit submission timeline not stated; derived from overall project deadline

### REQ-009-01-009 — Fee Exclusion from Proposal
The Proponent shall exclude development permit fee costs from its proposal submittal; fees are paid by the County.

- **Basis:** R-01 §3.3.1 ("Pay for development permit, building permit, and Safety Code Permits Fees. Proponents shall exclude the cost of these permits in their Proposal submittal."); Decomp SOW-0202
- **Type:** Commercial / contractual requirement

### REQ-009-01-010 — Governing Law
All permit activities are subject to the law of the Province of Alberta.

- **Basis:** R-01 §2.22
- **Type:** Regulatory constraint

### REQ-009-01-011 — Conditions Tracking
Any conditions attached to the development permit approval shall be documented and tracked to ensure compliance.

- **Basis:** _CONTEXT.md (Scope: "Conditions and requirements tracking")
- **Label:** ASSUMPTION — derived from project context; specific tracking mechanism not prescribed in sources

### REQ-009-01-012 — Permit Conditions Downstream Communication
Permit conditions that affect design or construction shall be formally communicated to downstream deliverables (DEL-009-04 Code Compliance Register & Inspection Log) and to relevant construction packages, with confirmation of receipt.

- **Basis:** Guidance (Permit Conditions as Downstream Constraints); Procedure Step 6.4 (distribution list); _CONTEXT.md (Scope: Conditions and requirements tracking)
- **Label:** ASSUMPTION — the requirement for formal downstream communication with confirmation is inferred from the Guidance directional statement and Procedure operational step; no source explicitly mandates the mechanism or completeness check for condition flow
- **Enrichment:** Added per F-003 from _SEMANTIC_LENSING.md to close gap in downstream condition flow governance

### REQ-009-01-013 — Application Package Internal Review
The completed application package shall undergo a formal internal review for completeness (against authority checklist), consistency, and accuracy before submission to the permitting authority.

- **Basis:** Procedure Step 3.4 (internal review before submission); _CONTEXT.md Success Criteria ("Documentation complete and accessible")
- **Label:** ASSUMPTION — internal review is described in Procedure Step 3.4 as an operational step; elevating to a normative requirement is inferred from the quality assurance gap identified by C-003
- **Enrichment:** Added per C-003 from _SEMANTIC_LENSING.md to formalize internal quality review as a normative requirement

### REQ-009-01-014 — Permit Outcome Success Criteria
The development permit shall be obtained without conditions that require material design changes, or within a schedule window that does not compress the construction timeline beyond the project's risk tolerance. If the permit is issued with conditions requiring design changes, these shall be documented and the schedule impact assessed.

- **Basis:** _CONTEXT.md Success Criteria ("Approval obtained with minimal conditions"); R-01 §3.1 (completion deadline)
- **Label:** ASSUMPTION — "minimal conditions" from _CONTEXT.md is interpreted as conditions not requiring material design changes; specific thresholds for schedule compression are TBD
- **Enrichment:** Added per D-003 from _SEMANTIC_LENSING.md to trace the _CONTEXT.md success criterion into a formal requirement
