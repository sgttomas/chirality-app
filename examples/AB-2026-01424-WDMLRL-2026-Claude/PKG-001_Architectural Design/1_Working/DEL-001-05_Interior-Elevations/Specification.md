# Specification — DEL-001-05 Interior Elevations

---

## Scope

### What This Deliverable Covers

DEL-001-05 Interior Elevations is a drawing set produced by the Architect under PKG-001 Architectural Design. It covers the interior elevation views of all rooms and spaces within the New North Shop addition, sufficient to define:

- Wall heights, finish transitions, and features as seen from interior orthographic views.
- Locations and dimensions of interior doors, windows (if any), openings, and roll-up doors visible in elevation.
- Built-in features visible on interior walls: shelving, work benches, wash station, wash sink, and similar fixed elements shown on the Appendix B (Shop) conceptual floor plan.
- Interior face of mezzanine framing (reference only — structural details in DEL-002-05).
- Washroom and locker/change room interior elevations (plumbing rough-in locations shown for coordination; plumbing design in PKG-006).
- Office interior elevations.
- Parts room interior elevations (including the 6 ft wide roll-up door location and head height).
- Utility room interior elevations (including water storage area and stair-to-mezzanine interface).
- Wash bay interior elevations (including mud sump reference, ventilation penetrations, and crane rail height reference).
- Service trench/pit interior elevations (trench walls, ventilation penetrations; structural detail reference to DEL-002-06).
- Welding station area interior elevations (including exhaust ventilation penetration locations for coordination with mechanical PKG-003).

This deliverable covers the addition (New North Shop) and includes renovation scope for the Old North Shop washroom and locker room area where interior elevations are required (coordination with DEL-001-10 Renovation Plans).

## Requirements

### REQ-001 — Coverage of All Interior Spaces
**Statement:** Interior elevation drawings shall be provided for all principal interior spaces in the addition and for the Old North Shop washroom/locker renovation area, including: main repair bays, wash bay, parts room, utility room, office, washroom/locker/change room, welding station area, service trench walls, and mezzanine-level storage area.
**Source:** R-01 §3.1, §3.4; R-04 (App B); Decomp SOW-0011.
**Verification:** Drawing set includes at least one interior elevation view per principal space.

### REQ-002 — Code-Compliant Design
**Statement:** All interior elevations shall reflect a design that adheres to all applicable Alberta Safety Codes and Alberta building codes and regulations.
**Source:** R-01 §3.3.2, §3.4.
**Verification:** Architect confirmation on drawing set; permit issuance by the County (building permit obtained by Proponent per R-01 §3.3.2).

### REQ-003 — IFC Stamping Requirement
**Statement:** All Issued for Construction drawings, including interior elevation sheets, shall be signed and stamped by a Professional Engineer (P.Eng.) licensed to practice in the Province of Alberta.
**Source:** R-01 §3.3.2.
**Verification:** Each IFC sheet bears a valid Alberta P.Eng. stamp and signature.

### REQ-004 — County Preliminary Approval
**Statement:** Preliminary architectural design shall receive County project team approval before final interior elevation drawings are issued for construction.
**Source:** R-01 §3.3.2; Decomp DEL-001-01 (Preliminary Design).
**Verification:** Documented County approval of preliminary design (DEL-001-01) precedes IFC issuance.

### REQ-005 — Crane Rail Reference Elevation
**Statement:** Interior elevations in the main repair bays shall show the reference elevation (underside of crane rail) for the two 5-tonne overhead cranes on trolley, to confirm adequate clearance within the 35 ft ceiling height.
**Source:** R-01 §3.4 (2 × 5-tonne overhead cranes; 35 ft ceiling); R-04 (App B).
**Verification:** Elevation view in repair bay area includes annotated crane rail underside elevation; cross-referenced to DEL-002-07 (Crane Support Structure Details).

### REQ-006 — Service Trench Wall Elevations
**Statement:** Interior elevation of service trench (service pit) walls shall be provided, showing trench depth, ventilation penetrations, and lighting penetration locations (lighting and ventilation design by others — shown for coordination).
**Source:** R-01 §3.4 (ventilated and lighted service pit); R-04 (App B, service trench notation).
**Verification:** Service trench wall interior elevation included in drawing set; cross-referenced to DEL-002-06.

### REQ-007 — Parts Room Roll-Up Door
**Statement:** Interior elevations of the parts room shall include the 6 ft wide roll-up door: door head height, sill, jamb condition, and clearances.
**Source:** R-01 §3.4 (400 sq ft secure parts storage area with 6 ft wide roll-up door).
**Verification:** Roll-up door shown and dimensioned in parts room interior elevation; door referenced in DEL-001-07 Door & Window Schedule.

### REQ-008 — Mezzanine Interior Elevations
**Statement:** Interior elevations shall show the mezzanine level over the parts room, utility room, and wash bay, including guardrail reference, stair opening, and any fixed storage reference heights.
**Source:** R-01 §3.4 (mezzanine storage); R-04 (App B, mezzanine notation).
**Verification:** Mezzanine-level interior elevation views included; cross-referenced to DEL-002-05.

### REQ-009 — Washroom and Locker Room
**Statement:** Interior elevations for the expanded washroom (urinal, locker change room, laundry) shall show fixture wall heights and layout sufficient for plumbing and millwork coordination.
**Source:** R-01 §3.4 (expand washroom to include urinal and locker change room including laundry services).
**Verification:** Washroom and locker/change room interior elevations present in drawing set; plumbing rough-in references noted for coordination with PKG-006.

### REQ-010 — Welding Station Ventilation Penetrations
**Statement:** Interior elevations of the welding station area shall identify the location(s) of ventilation exhaust penetrations through the wall or ceiling for coordination with mechanical design (PKG-003).
**Source:** R-01 §3.4 (ventilation at welding station; welding station exhaust system); R-04 (App B, welding station location).
**Verification:** Penetration location annotated on interior elevation; mechanical coordination note included.

### REQ-011 — Wash Bay Interior Elevations
**Statement:** Interior elevations of the wash bay shall show the bay walls, the connection to the exterior mud sump (penetration reference), ventilation penetrations, and crane rail reference elevation.
**Source:** R-01 §3.4 (single enclosed wash bay; mud sump on exterior); R-04 (App B).
**Verification:** Wash bay interior elevation present; mud sump and ventilation penetrations identified.

### REQ-012 — Discipline Coordination
**Statement:** Interior elevations shall be coordinated across architectural, structural, mechanical, electrical, civil, and plumbing disciplines before IFC issuance.
**Source:** R-01 §3.3.2; Decomp OBJ-003 (fully coordinated across all disciplines).
**Verification:** Coordination review documented; no unresolved clashes at IFC issuance. A "clash" is defined as a dimensional or spatial conflict between disciplines that would prevent construction or code compliance if built as drawn. Coordination comments that are tracked as TBD with responsible party and expected resolution date are not clashes for the purpose of this gate, provided they do not affect life-safety or structural integrity. Coordination log shall record: date, discipline, item description, resolution status, and responsible party. Source: lensing items X-003, F-004.

### REQ-013 — Accessible Design (Barrier-Free)
**Statement:** Interior elevations for the washroom, locker/change room, and any public-access areas shall reflect barrier-free design requirements as applicable under the Alberta Building Code and NBC.
**Source:** ASSUMPTION — Alberta building code barrier-free requirements are likely applicable given this is a new building with washroom facilities (R-01 §3.3.2 requires adherence to all applicable building codes; R-01 §3.4 specifies washroom with urinal and locker change room). Specific code provisions TBD — Architect / AHJ to confirm.
**Verification:** Accessible design features (door widths, clearances, fixture heights) shown on washroom/locker interior elevations and confirmed compliant by Architect; AHJ review at permit application.
**Status:** TBD — requirement to be confirmed with AHJ. Elevated from Guidance assumption per lensing item C-001.

### REQ-014 — Interior Finish Callouts
**Statement:** Interior elevation drawings shall include finish callouts for all wall surfaces, keyed to the Finish Schedule (DEL-001-08) and consistent with the Architectural Specification (DEL-001-11).
**Source:** R-01 §3.4 (complete final architectural design); Datasheet Construction section ("Interior Finishes: TBD — to be specified by Architect in DEL-001-11"); Procedure Step 3 (interior finish zones). Added per lensing item C-002 to provide normative anchor for finish documentation.
**Verification:** All wall surfaces in interior elevation views have finish keynotes present; keynotes cross-referenced to DEL-001-08 Finish Schedule.

### REQ-015 — Mezzanine Guardrail Height
**Statement:** Interior elevations showing the mezzanine perimeter shall include guardrail height at or above the minimum required by the applicable building code.
**Source:** R-01 §3.4 (mezzanine storage above parts room, utility room, and wash bay); ASSUMPTION: 1,070 mm minimum per NBC (specific edition and clause TBD — Architect / AHJ to confirm). Elevated from Procedure Step 4.3 inline assumption per lensing item F-001.
**Verification:** Guardrail height annotated on mezzanine interior elevation; confirmed compliant with applicable code by Architect; AHJ review at permit application.
**Status:** TBD — minimum height to be confirmed with AHJ upon code edition determination.
