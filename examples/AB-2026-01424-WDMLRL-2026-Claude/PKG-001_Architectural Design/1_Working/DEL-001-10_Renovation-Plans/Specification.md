## Scope

### What This Deliverable Covers

DEL-001-10 comprises the architectural drawing set for the renovation of the existing Old North Shop at the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill (SW 14–44–21–W4, Camrose County, Alberta). The drawing set documents the architectural intent for the following renovation scope items:

- **SOW-0070** — Renovation of the 250 sq ft kitchenette area in the existing Old North Shop
- **SOW-0071** — Renovation of the existing mezzanine (2nd-level coffee room) within the Old North Shop
- **SOW-0072** — Renovation of the existing washroom below the mezzanine in the Old North Shop
- **SOW-0073** — Construction of a new locker/change room with laundry services
- **SOW-0074** — Expansion of washroom facilities to include a urinal

Sources: Decomposition §3-I (SOW definitions), §7 PKG-001 table (DEL-001-10 row); RFP §3.4.

### What This Deliverable Excludes

- New North Shop design drawings (covered by DEL-001-02 through DEL-001-08)
- Demolition drawings for the Old North Shop (covered by DEL-001-09; DEL-001-10 picks up post-demolition conditions)
- Architectural specification document (covered by DEL-001-11)
- Structural drawings for the Old North Shop renovation (PKG-002 deliverables — coordinate for mezzanine structural requirements)
- Mechanical and plumbing drawings for renovated spaces (PKG-003/PKG-004 deliverables — coordinate for kitchenette and washroom services)
- Electrical drawings for renovated spaces (PKG-005 deliverables — coordinate for lighting and power in renovated areas)
- Construction of the renovations (PKG-017 deliverables)

## Requirements

### REQ-001 — Drawing Set Completeness

The renovation drawing set shall include, at minimum, floor plan(s) documenting the post-renovation architectural intent for all five SOW items (SOW-0070, SOW-0071, SOW-0072, SOW-0073, SOW-0074).

Source: RFP §3.3.2 ("Complete final design"); Decomposition DEL-001-10 row.

### REQ-002 — Kitchenette Renovation Area

The drawings shall reflect the renovation of a kitchenette area of 250 square feet within the existing Old North Shop building.

Source: RFP §3.4 ("Renovate 250 square foot kitchenette area located in the existing building").

### REQ-003 — Mezzanine Renovation

The drawings shall reflect the renovation of the existing 2nd-level mezzanine (coffee room) within the Old North Shop. The mezzanine is shown in conceptual form on Appendix B (Shop) at the west section of the Old North Shop adjacent to the junction with the New North Shop.

Source: App B (Shop) — note "Old North Shop Mezzanine to be renovated and the washroom below renovated along with new locker room."

### REQ-004 — Washroom Renovation and Expansion

The drawings shall reflect:
(a) Renovation of the existing washroom located below the mezzanine in the Old North Shop (SOW-0072); and
(b) Expansion of the washroom to include a urinal (SOW-0074).

Source: RFP §3.4 ("Expand washroom facilities to include urinal and locker change room, including laundry services"); App B (Shop).

### REQ-005 — Locker/Change Room with Laundry

The drawings shall reflect the construction of a new locker/change room that includes laundry services facilities (SOW-0073).

Source: RFP §3.4 ("Expand washroom facilities to include urinal and locker change room, including laundry services"); Decomposition SOW-0073 ("Construct new locker/change room with laundry services").

### REQ-006 — Code Compliance

The renovation design documented in the drawing set shall comply with all applicable Alberta Safety Codes and the applicable Alberta Building Code edition.

Source: RFP §3.3.2 ("The building design must adhere to all applicable building codes and regulations"; "The proposed building must adhere to all Alberta Safety Codes").

**ASSUMPTION:** The Alberta Building Code 2019 (NBC 2019 Alberta Edition) is the governing building code edition. The RFP does not specify the edition; confirm with the Authority Having Jurisdiction (Camrose County) prior to design submission.

**Action required (Lensing items A-001, C-001):** The governing code edition must be confirmed with the AHJ (Camrose County) before design proceeds. This ASSUMPTION cannot remain unresolved at the IFC stage because the enforceable compliance threshold for the renovation design depends on the specific code edition. Once the AHJ confirms the applicable edition, this requirement shall be updated to remove the ASSUMPTION label and state the confirmed edition as a mandatory requirement. (Source: Specification.md REQ-006, Standards table.)

### REQ-007 — P.Eng. Stamp Required

All Issued for Construction (IFC) drawings included in or referenced by this drawing set shall be signed and stamped by a professional engineer (P.Eng.) licensed to practice in the Province of Alberta.

Source: RFP §3.3.2 ("All Issued for Construction Drawings must be signed/stamped by a professional engineer licensed to practice in the province of Alberta").

**Note:** For architectural renovation drawings, the stamp requirement may be satisfied by the Architect of Record in coordination with the structural P.Eng. for any structural elements. Confirm with the Authority Having Jurisdiction.

**Ambiguity flag (Lensing item A-002):** The RFP requires "P.Eng." stamp on all IFC drawings (RFP SS3.3.2), but the Note above introduces the possibility that the Architect of Record seal may satisfy this for architectural sheets. This creates ambiguity in what mandatory practice actually requires. The design team should clarify with the AHJ and/or APEGA regulations whether: (a) P.Eng. stamp alone is required on all sheets including architectural, or (b) the Architect of Record seal satisfies the requirement for architectural sheets, with P.Eng. stamp required only for structural/mechanical/electrical sheets. Record the ruling here once confirmed. (Source: Specification.md REQ-007 Note; RFP SS3.3.2.)

### REQ-008 — Coordination with Demolition Plans

The renovation plans shall be coordinated with DEL-001-09 (Old North Shop Demolition Plans) to ensure continuity between the post-demolition conditions and the renovation design intent.

Source: Decomposition §7 PKG-001 — DEL-001-09 is the companion demolition drawing set covering SOW-0070, SOW-0071, SOW-0072.

### REQ-009 — Coordination with Structural, Mechanical, and Electrical Disciplines

The renovation plans shall be coordinated with:
- PKG-002 (Structural Design) for any structural modifications to the mezzanine or load-bearing elements
- PKG-003/PKG-004 (Mechanical/Plumbing Design) for kitchenette and washroom/laundry service rough-ins
- PKG-005 (Electrical Design) for lighting, power, and ventilation rough-ins in renovated spaces

Source: RFP §3.3.2 (final design to be fully coordinated); RFP §1.0 (services include architectural, mechanical, structural, electrical disciplines).

### REQ-010 — Completion Deadline

The drawing set, in IFC form, shall be completed in time to support construction commencement and completion of all Work on or before December 31, 2026.

Source: RFP §3.1 ("All Work pertaining to the Design Build of West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop Addition must be completed on or before December 31, 2026").

### REQ-011 — Preliminary Design Approval

A preliminary design shall be delivered to the County for approval prior to completion of the final IFC drawing set.

Source: RFP §3.3.2 ("Deliver Preliminary Design for the County project team to approve").

**Minimum content requirement (Lensing item D-002):** The RFP does not specify the required contents or level of completion for the Preliminary Design submission. The following minimum content is proposed to ensure validated work delivery at the preliminary design gate:
- Renovation floor plan(s) at a level of detail sufficient to communicate the post-renovation layout for all five SOW items
- Identification of major structural, mechanical, and electrical coordination items requiring design resolution
- Any constraints or conflicts requiring County direction (e.g., expansion direction for locker/change room, extent of mezzanine renovation, accessibility compliance approach)
- Confirmation of field-surveyed existing conditions forming the design basis

ASSUMPTION: The above minimum content list is proposed based on design-build practice; the County Project Manager should confirm the required submission scope. (Source: Specification.md REQ-011; Procedure.md Step 3.)
