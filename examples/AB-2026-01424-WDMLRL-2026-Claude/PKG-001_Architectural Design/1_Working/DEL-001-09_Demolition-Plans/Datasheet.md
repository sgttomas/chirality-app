## Construction (Downstream Use Context)

The Demolition Plans inform the construction team executing DEL-017-01 (Kitchenette Renovation), DEL-017-02 (Mezzanine Renovation), and DEL-017-03 (Washroom Renovation) under PKG-017. The drawings document existing conditions to be removed or modified, and provide the legal/regulatory basis for permit applications under PKG-009. Demolition scope is limited to the three renovation areas; no demolition of the overall Old North Shop structure is indicated.

| Downstream Deliverable | Relationship |
|---|---|
| DEL-001-10 — Old North Shop Renovation Plans | Demolition drawings precede and inform renovation design |
| DEL-017-01 — Kitchenette Renovation (construction) | Demolition drawings define scope of removal |
| DEL-017-02 — Mezzanine Renovation (construction) | Demolition drawings define scope of removal |
| DEL-017-03 — Washroom Renovation & Expansion (construction) | Demolition drawings define scope of removal |
| DEL-009-02 — Building Permit | Demolition drawings support permit application |
