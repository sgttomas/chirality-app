# Specification — DEL-001-11 Architectural Specification

---

## Scope

### What This Deliverable Covers

DEL-001-11 is the **Architectural Specification** for PKG-001 Architectural Design. It establishes the normative requirements for materials, performance, finishes, and architectural standards governing:

1. **New North Shop Addition** — a new concrete structure of approximately 13,000 sq ft useable area at SW 14–44–21–W4, near Ferintosh, Alberta (SOW-0011).
2. **Old North Shop Renovation** — renovations to the existing 105 ft x 40 ft maintenance shop building, covering the kitchenette area, existing mezzanine (2nd-level coffee room), existing washroom, new locker/change room with laundry services, and washroom expansion to include urinal (SOW-0070–SOW-0074).

This specification is a companion document to the architectural drawing set (DEL-001-02 through DEL-001-10) and supports issuance of Issued for Construction (IFC) documentation. It governs the work of the Design-Builder and any subcontractors performing architectural scope.

### What This Deliverable Excludes

- Structural design requirements (governed by DEL-002-12 Structural Specification)
- Mechanical design requirements (governed by DEL-003-07 Mechanical Specification)
- Electrical design requirements (governed by DEL-004-09 Electrical Specification)
- Plumbing design requirements (governed by DEL-006-08 Plumbing Specification)
- Civil/site design requirements (governed by DEL-005-07 Civil Specification)
- Geotechnical investigation scope
- Construction means and methods (Design-Builder's responsibility)
