# Specification — DEL-001-06 Reflected Ceiling Plans

---

## Scope

### What this deliverable covers

DEL-001-06 consists of the complete set of Reflected Ceiling Plan (RCP) drawings for the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop Addition (New North Shop). The RCP drawing set shall graphically depict all ceiling-plane conditions, ceiling-mounted elements, and ceiling heights for all spaces within the new addition and the renovation scope of the Old North Shop, as part of the final architectural design (SOW-0011).

The RCP drawings are part of the Issued For Construction (IFC) drawing package and must be fully coordinated with structural, mechanical, and electrical discipline drawings before issue.

### What this deliverable excludes

- Structural framing design for the ceiling and crane system (DEL-002 series — Structural Design package)
- Electrical wiring design and panel schedules (DEL-004 series — Electrical Design package)
- Mechanical ductwork design and HVAC schedules (DEL-003 series — Mechanical Design package)
- Site plan and civil drawings
- Reflected ceiling plans for any buildings other than the New North Shop addition and the identified Old North Shop renovation areas

---

## Requirements

### REQ-RCP-001 — Complete Space Coverage

The RCP drawing set shall include reflected ceiling plans for all spaces within the new addition footprint (~13,000 sq ft, 130' × 100' plan) and for all Old North Shop renovation areas identified in the conceptual drawings, including:
- Main Shop Bay (two drive-through bays)
- Wash Bay
- Service Trench / Pit Area
- Parts Room (~400 sq ft)
- Utility Room
- Office
- Washroom (new addition)
- Overhead Mezzanine level (over Parts Room/Utility Room/Wash Bay)
- Old North Shop: Washroom renovation area
- Old North Shop: Locker / Change Room (new)
- Old North Shop: 2nd Level Mezzanine / Coffee Room renovation area
- Welding Station area

*Sources: RFP §3.1, §3.4; App B (Shop); App B (Electrical)*

### REQ-RCP-002 — Ceiling Height Annotation

All RCP drawings shall indicate ceiling heights for each space, including explicit ceiling height annotations (or TBD markers with resolution tracking) for all non-main-shop spaces: office, parts room, utility room, washroom, locker room, mezzanine level, wash bay, and work bench areas. The main shop structural ceiling height is 35 feet. Ceiling heights for all other spaces shall be confirmed by the architect in coordination with the structural engineer before IFC.

*Source: RFP §3.4 ("Concrete structure 35' ceiling"); App B (Shop) legend*
*Note: Enriched per A-001 — original requirement stated "indicate ceiling heights for each space" but only specified the 35' main shop height; all other ceiling heights remain TBD and require explicit annotation or resolution before IFC.*

### REQ-RCP-003 — Lighting Layout

The RCP drawings shall show the location, type, and designation of all ceiling-mounted light fixtures, consistent with the electrical design. Minimum fixture requirements from conceptual drawings:
- Main Shop: 5 rows × 4 high bay fixtures (150W LED, 22,000 lumens each)
- Wash Bay: 2 rows × 3 high bay fixtures
- Office: 1 × 8' LED fixture
- Utility Room: 2 × 8' LED fixtures
- Parts/Tool Room: 6 × 8' LED fixtures

The RCP lighting layout shall be consistent with DEL-004 (Electrical) drawings.

*Source: App B (Electrical) — Electrical Notes*

### REQ-RCP-004 — Ceiling Fan Layout

The RCP drawings shall show the location of 6 ceiling fans within the main shop area.

*Source: App B (Electrical) — Electrical Notes ("6 Ceiling Fans for Main Shop")*

### REQ-RCP-005 — Exhaust Fan Locations

The RCP drawings shall show the ceiling-level location of all exhaust fans, including at the welding station and other locations per the electrical and mechanical design. Exhaust fan symbols shall be consistent with the electrical drawing legend.

*Source: App B (Electrical) — legend symbol "E"; RFP §3.4 ("Ventilation at welding station," "Exhaust hoses and fans for heavy equipment")*

### REQ-RCP-006 — Overhead Crane Rail Indication

The RCP drawings shall indicate the position and extent of overhead crane beam/rail systems for the two (2) 5-tonne cranes in the main shop. Crane rail elevations shall be coordinated with the structural drawings.

*Source: RFP §3.4 ("2 – 5-tonne overhead cranes on a trolley"); App B (Shop) — "5 TON CRANE" annotations*

### REQ-RCP-007 — Mechanical Unit Locations

The RCP drawings shall indicate the ceiling-mounted locations of forced air makeup units and any other ceiling-suspended mechanical equipment. Quantity and exact configuration to be confirmed by mechanical engineer.

*Source: RFP §3.4 ("High recovery heating system," "High volume air exchanger"); App B (Shop) legend ("Forced Air Makeup")*

*ASSUMPTION: Forced air makeup units are ceiling-mounted or high-wall mounted and will appear on RCP; confirmation required from mechanical discipline.*

### REQ-RCP-008 — Equipment Exhaust Rough-In Indication

The RCP drawings shall indicate ceiling drop locations for exhaust hoses serving heavy equipment in the repair bays.

*Source: RFP §3.4 ("Exhaust hoses and fans for heavy equipment in the repair bays")*

### REQ-RCP-009 — Mezzanine Soffit / Level Differentiation

The RCP drawings shall clearly differentiate ceiling planes at different levels, including:
- Main structural ceiling at 35'
- Mezzanine soffit/underside visible at the main floor level where mezzanine exists (over Parts Room, Utility Room, Wash Bay)
- Ceiling height at mezzanine level (TBD — to be set by architect in coordination with structural)

*Source: App B (Shop) — "Overhead Mezzanine over Parts Room/Utility Room/Wash Bay" legend; RFP §3.4*

### REQ-RCP-010 — Overhead Door Header Indication

The RCP drawings shall indicate overhead door headers and associated ceiling conditions at all overhead door openings.

*Source: RFP §3.1 ("two drive-through bays"); App B (Shop) — Roll Door notation; App B (Electrical) — "Will need power for crane and Overhead Doors"*

### REQ-RCP-011 — Service Pit / Trench Indication

The RCP drawings shall indicate the ceiling/structural conditions above the service trench (pit) area, including lighting and ventilation provisions.

*Source: RFP §3.4 ("Ventilated and lighted service pit area for mechanics to perform servicing under equipment"); App B (Shop) — "SERVICE TRENCH" annotation*

### REQ-RCP-012 — Coordination with Electrical Drawings

All RCP lighting and electrical ceiling elements shall be coordinated with and cross-referenced to the electrical drawing set (DEL-004). No conflicts shall exist between the RCP and electrical plans regarding fixture location, circuit designation references, or ceiling penetrations.

*Source: OBJ-003; RFP §3.3.2*

### REQ-RCP-013 — Coordination with Structural Drawings

Crane beam/rail locations, mezzanine framing, and all ceiling structural elements shown on the RCP shall be consistent with the structural drawing set (DEL-002). Conflicts shall be resolved before IFC issue.

*Source: OBJ-003; RFP §3.3.2*

### REQ-RCP-014 — Coordination with Mechanical Drawings

Mechanical unit locations, exhaust fan positions, and ventilation penetrations shown on the RCP shall be consistent with the mechanical drawing set (DEL-003).

*Source: OBJ-003; RFP §3.3.2*

### REQ-RCP-015 — P.Eng. Stamp (IFC)

All IFC drawings, including the RCP set, shall be signed and stamped by a professional engineer licensed in the Province of Alberta.

*Source: RFP §3.3.2 ("All Issued For Construction Drawings must be signed/stamped by a professional engineer licensed to practice in the province of Alberta")*

### REQ-RCP-016 — Code Compliance

The building design and all drawings, including RCPs, shall adhere to all applicable Alberta Safety Codes (building, electrical, fire).

*Source: RFP §3.3.2 ("The proposed building must adhere to all Alberta Safety Codes")*

### REQ-RCP-018 — Drawing Scale *(added per C-002)*

The RCP drawing set shall be produced at a scale that ensures all ceiling-mounted elements and annotations are legible at printed size. Required drawing scale(s): TBD — to be confirmed by architect. Procedure assumes 1:100 for main shop and 1:50 for smaller rooms (ASSUMPTION); this requirement shall be updated once the architect confirms the scale convention.

*Source: Specification Documentation — Anticipated Artifacts (scale listed as TBD); Procedure Step C-01 (ASSUMPTION); _SEMANTIC_LENSING C-002*

### REQ-RCP-019 — Fire Separation Indication at Ceiling *(added per F-001)*

The RCP drawings shall indicate fire separation lines and fire-rated assembly boundaries at the ceiling plane where applicable, including but not limited to separations between the main shop and ancillary rooms and at the mezzanine level. Fire separation locations shall be consistent with NBC requirements for the building occupancy classification. Specific fire-rated assemblies and their ceiling-plane implications: TBD pending code review (see REQ-RCP-016).

*Source: RFP §3.3.2 (Alberta Safety Codes including fire); REQ-RCP-016 (code compliance); _SEMANTIC_LENSING F-001*
*ASSUMPTION: Fire separation indications on the ceiling plane are required for an industrial building of this type per NBC; specific locations TBD from code review.*

### REQ-RCP-020 — Version Control and Revision Tracking *(added per F-005)*

The RCP drawing set shall include a revision block on each title block and a revision log per drawing sheet, tracking all revisions from preliminary issue through IFC. Revision numbering and tracking format: TBD per project drawing standards.

*Source: Standard drawing practice; _SEMANTIC_LENSING F-005*
*ASSUMPTION: Revision control is standard practice for IFC drawing sets; format TBD.*

### REQ-RCP-017 — Renovation Areas

RCP drawings shall cover all Old North Shop renovation areas (washroom below 2nd-level mezzanine, new locker/change room, and 2nd-level mezzanine/coffee room). Existing ceiling conditions in the Old North Shop shall be field-verified before RCP completion.

*Source: App B (Shop) legend ("Old North Shop Mezzanine to be renovated and the washroom below renovated along with new locker room"); RFP §3.1*
