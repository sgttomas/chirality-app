## Package-Level Cost Ownership Hints (Draft)

- Treat each `PKG-XXX` as the primary cost ownership boundary; avoid double-counting cross-cutting compliance and coordination work.
- Foundation (PKG-010) is variable-price pending geotechnical report; keep foundation quantities and unit rates as allowances until geotech confirms soil/frost conditions.

## SOW Multi-Mapping / Double-Count Warnings (Best-Effort)

- Some high-level SOW items map to ranges of deliverables (e.g., "DEL-xxx-02 thru DEL-xxx-08"). When estimating, ensure hours/allowances are not applied redundantly across each sub-deliverable if they represent a single integrated drawing package.
- Cross-discipline IFC coordination is a quality attribute across PKG-001–007; do not treat it as a separate standalone cost line unless explicitly scoped.
