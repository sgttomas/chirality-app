## Scope

### In Scope

This specification governs the supply, installation, and commissioning of the 50,000L water storage cistern and associated internal service water distribution system for the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill (WDMLRL) New North Shop building.

Scope includes:
- Supply and installation of a minimum 50,000L cistern vessel with all necessary plumbing connections (SOW-0041; RFP §3.4)
- Supply and installation of a pump capable of 100 LPM with a 2.5-inch cistern filling connection for internal service water distribution (SOW-0042; RFP §3.4)
- All distribution piping from the cistern to service points within the building as shown on the plumbing drawing (R-06): water taps, pressure washer reel, and other points as required by the IFC plumbing design (ASSUMPTION: full piping scope to be confirmed in IFC drawings, PKG-006/DEL-006-02)
- All hangers, supports, connections, valves, and appurtenances required for a complete and operational system
- Backflow prevention and cross-connection control devices as required by applicable Alberta plumbing code provisions (see REQ-014-01-011)
- Cistern overflow and high-level management provisions (see REQ-014-01-012)

### Out of Scope

- Plumbing design (PKG-006; DEL-006-02 through DEL-006-08) — design is a prerequisite
- Bulk water fill system and its external fill pump (DEL-014-03)
- Septic system (DEL-014-02)
- Floor drains and sump drains (DEL-014-04)
- Electrical supply to pump motor (PKG-015; DEL-015-01) — electrical is a separate deliverable requiring coordination
- Emergency shower plumbing (DEL-014-05)
- Civil/structural work for cistern pad or containment (PKG-018 / PKG-011 as applicable)

## Requirements

### REQ-014-01-001: Cistern Volume
- **Requirement:** The cistern shall have a minimum storage volume of 50,000 litres.
- **Source:** RFP §3.4 ("Minimum 50,000 L Cistern complete with the necessary plumbing.")
- **Type:** Mandatory

### REQ-014-01-002: Pump Flow Rate
- **Requirement:** The distribution pump shall be capable of a minimum flow rate of 100 litres per minute (LPM).
- **Source:** RFP §3.4 ("Pump capable of 100 LPM complete with a 2.5 inch cistern filling connection for internal service water distribution.")
- **Type:** Mandatory

### REQ-014-01-003: Cistern Filling Connection
- **Requirement:** The cistern filling connection shall be 2.5 inches in diameter.
- **Source:** RFP §3.4
- **Type:** Mandatory
- **Clarification needed (Lensing Item E-002):** The RFP text "Pump capable of 100 LPM complete with a 2.5 inch cistern filling connection" is ambiguous: it is unclear whether the 2.5-inch connection refers to the external bulk-fill inlet to the cistern (from DEL-014-03), or to a pump connection. This specification interprets REQ-014-01-003 as the external cistern fill inlet diameter pending Owner / P.Eng. clarification of the RFP intent. See Conflict Table CONF-014-01-04 in Guidance.

### REQ-014-01-004: Complete Plumbing
- **Requirement:** The cistern shall be installed "complete with the necessary plumbing." All plumbing accessories, connections, and distribution lines required for a fully operational system shall be included.
- **Source:** RFP §3.4; SOW-0041
- **Type:** Mandatory

### REQ-014-01-005: Internal Service Water Distribution
- **Requirement:** The pump and cistern system shall provide internal service water distribution to all service points within the building as shown on the IFC plumbing drawings (to be produced under PKG-006). Distribution points shown on the conceptual plumbing drawing (R-06) include water taps and a pressure washer reel.
- **Source:** RFP §3.4; Appendix B Plumbing Drawing (R-06)
- **Type:** Mandatory; exact service point locations subject to IFC drawings
- **Terminology note (Lensing Item B-004):** This system is described as "internal service water distribution" per the RFP. Whether the system classification is potable or non-potable service water is unresolved (see Conflict Table CONF-014-01-01 and CONF-014-01-02 in Guidance). Consistent terminology: use "service water" until the Owner declares the water quality classification.

### REQ-014-01-006: Code Compliance
- **Requirement:** All plumbing work shall conform to all applicable Alberta Safety Codes and building code requirements in effect at the time of construction. All IFC construction drawings shall be signed/stamped by a Professional Engineer (P.Eng.) licensed in the Province of Alberta.
- **Source:** RFP §3.3.2; RFP §3.4 ("The proposed building must adhere to all Alberta Safety Codes.")
- **Type:** Mandatory

### REQ-014-01-007: Completion Deadline
- **Requirement:** Installation and commissioning shall be complete on or before December 31, 2026.
- **Source:** RFP §3.1 ("All Work pertaining to the Design Build of West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop Addition must be completed on or before December 31, 2026.")
