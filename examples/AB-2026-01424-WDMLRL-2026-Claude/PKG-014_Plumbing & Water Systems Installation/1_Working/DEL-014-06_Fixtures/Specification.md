# Specification — DEL-014-06: Plumbing Fixtures

---

## Scope

### Covered by This Deliverable

DEL-014-06 covers the supply and installation of the following plumbing fixtures within the New North Shop addition at the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop (SW 14–44–21–W4, Ferintosh, Alberta):

- Water tap(s) as shown on the approved plumbing drawings (SOW-0049)
- Pressure washer reel assembly (SOW-0049)
- Industrial wash station (wash sink) (SOW-0050)

All work shall be performed by the Plumbing Contractor as part of PKG-014 — Plumbing & Water Systems Installation, under the Design-Build contract (CCDC 14–2013).

### Excluded from This Deliverable

| Excluded Item | Responsible Deliverable |
|---|---|
| Cistern, distribution piping, and supply pump | DEL-014-01 |
| Septic holding tank and main sanitary connections | DEL-014-02 |
| Floor drains and sump drain rough-in | DEL-014-04 |
| Emergency shower installation | DEL-014-05 |
| Pressure washer electrical circuit (70A) | DEL-015-04 (PKG-015) |
| Plumbing design drawings and fixture schedule | PKG-006 (DEL-006-02, DEL-006-03, DEL-006-06) |
| Building structure and wall framing for fixture mounting | PKG-011, PKG-012 |

## Requirements

> **Note on ASSUMPTION-typed requirements (C-001):** Several requirements below are typed as "ASSUMPTION." These are requirements inferred from project context (industrial maintenance shop use, Alberta plumbing code expectations, standard practice) that have not yet been confirmed by the Plumbing Engineer or design documents. They are treated as operative pending design confirmation via DEL-006-06 and IFC drawings. If design confirmation overturns an assumption, the affected requirement should be revised or removed. The distinction between mandatory-by-code requirements and assumed-pending-design requirements is important: ASSUMPTION-typed requirements should not be treated as optional but rather as provisionally binding until the authoritative design source (DEL-006-06) confirms or overrides them. See Guidance for rationale (F-001).
