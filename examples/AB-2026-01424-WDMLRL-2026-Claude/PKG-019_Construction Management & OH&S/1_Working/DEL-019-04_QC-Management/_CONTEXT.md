# DEL-019-04: Construction Quality Control

## Deliverable Overview
**ID:** DEL-019-04_QC-Management
**Package:** PKG-019 — Construction Management & OH&S
**Title:** Construction Quality Control
**Category:** Management
**Responsible Party:** Project Manager / QC Manager

## Scope Definition
Implementation and oversight of comprehensive quality control program including inspection protocols, testing procedures, deficiency tracking, remediation verification, and quality assurance throughout construction phases.

## Linked SOW Items
- SOW-0089

## Linked Objectives
- OBJ-001 (Design & Specification Compliance)
- OBJ-007 (OH&S Program Implementation)

## Regulatory Context
Based on Main RFP §2.15 (OH&S Requirements) and §3.1 (Coordination Requirements)

## Key Roles
- **Project Manager:** Overall QC oversight and coordination
- **QC Manager:** Daily inspections, testing coordination, deficiency documentation

## Success Criteria
- Quality control plan documented and approved
- All inspections completed per schedule
- Deficiencies identified and corrected
- Testing protocols executed and results documented
- Final work meets specification requirements
