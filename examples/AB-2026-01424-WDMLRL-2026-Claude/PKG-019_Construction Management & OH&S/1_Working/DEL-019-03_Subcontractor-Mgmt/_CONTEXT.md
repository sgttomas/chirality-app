# DEL-019-03: Subcontractor Management

## Deliverable Overview
**ID:** DEL-019-03_Subcontractor-Mgmt
**Package:** PKG-019 — Construction Management & OH&S
**Title:** Subcontractor Management
**Category:** Management
**Responsible Party:** Project Manager

## Scope Definition
Comprehensive management of subcontractors including qualification, onboarding, performance monitoring, compliance verification, and administrative coordination throughout the construction phase.

## Linked SOW Items
- SOW-0088

## Linked Objectives
- OBJ-002 (Project Coordination & Communication)
- OBJ-007 (OH&S Program Implementation)

## Regulatory Context
Based on Main RFP §3.1 (Meeting and Coordination Requirements) and §2.15 (OH&S Requirements)

## Key Roles
- **Project Manager:** Subcontractor administration, compliance verification, performance monitoring

## Success Criteria
- All subcontractors qualified and approved per project requirements
- Performance standards established and monitored
- Compliance with OH&S and contractual requirements maintained
- Administrative coordination seamless
