# Specification — DEL-004-01 Preliminary Electrical Design

---

## Scope

### What This Deliverable Covers

DEL-004-01 is the **Preliminary Electrical Design** deliverable for the New North Shop Addition at the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill. It covers:

- Preliminary-level electrical design documentation for the New North Shop Addition (~13,000 sq ft, 35' ceiling) sufficient for County project team review and approval.
- Design coverage of: three-phase power distribution, lighting layout, receptacle layout, dedicated equipment circuits (cranes, overhead doors, compressor, fire hose pump, pressure washer), exhaust fans, ceiling fans, and low-voltage systems (security camera wiring, 2-way radio antenna wiring).
- Conceptual coordination with other disciplines (architectural, structural, mechanical) to confirm space allocations, equipment locations, and service entry points.
- Compliance with Alberta Safety Codes and applicable Alberta Building Code requirements at a preliminary level.

This deliverable corresponds to SOW-0010d: "Deliver preliminary electrical design for County approval." (Source: RFP §3.3.2; Decomposition §3C)

### What This Deliverable Does Not Cover

- Final Issued-for-Construction (IFC) electrical drawings — covered by DEL-004-02 through DEL-004-09.
- Electrical load calculations (DEL-004-08).
- Panel schedules (DEL-004-06).
- Mechanical equipment selection (outside electrical scope; mechanical discipline is PKG-003).
- Plumbing or structural design.
- Site electrical service route design beyond the building entry point (coordinated via PKG-018).
- Electrical Safety Code permit application (coordinated via PKG-009).
