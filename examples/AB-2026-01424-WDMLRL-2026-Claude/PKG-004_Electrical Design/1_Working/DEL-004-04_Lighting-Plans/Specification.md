# Specification — DEL-004-04 Lighting Plans

---

## Scope

### What This Deliverable Covers

DEL-004-04 Lighting Plans is the drawing set component of the PKG-004 Electrical Design package that documents the interior lighting system for the New North Shop addition at the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop. It covers:

- Lighting layout drawings for all zones of the New North Shop addition: main shop, wash bay, office, utility room, parts/tool room, and service pit/trench.
- Fixture schedule (type, wattage, lumen output, quantity per zone).
- Lighting circuit identification sufficient to coordinate with Panel Schedules (DEL-004-06) and Power Distribution Plans (DEL-004-03).
- IFC-quality drawings suitable for P.Eng. stamping per RFP §3.3.2.

### What This Deliverable Does Not Cover

- Receptacle layout (DEL-004-05).
- Power distribution and single-line diagram (DEL-004-02, DEL-004-03).
- Low-voltage systems: security camera wiring, antenna wiring (DEL-004-07).
- Mechanical systems: ceiling fans, exhaust fans (covered by PKG-003 and coordinated in electrical panel schedules).
- Lighting for the Old North Shop renovation (scope of renovation is architectural/electrical for existing building — not part of this new-addition lighting plan set). **TBD [E-003]:** Project Manager to determine whether coordination of Old North Shop lighting with this deliverable is required; if so, add as coordination TBD in REQ-LT-011. If not, record as explicitly excluded with no coordination requirement.
- Exterior lighting (e.g., building-mounted security lighting, parking area lighting). **TBD [X-001]:** Confirm with Owner / RFP review whether any exterior lighting is expected under this deliverable. If exterior lighting is in scope, add requirements. If not, this exclusion is confirmed.
- Construction of the lighting system (PKG-015, DEL-015-02).

## Requirements

### REQ-LT-001 — Main Shop High Bay Lighting

The lighting plan shall show 5 rows of 4 high bay LED fixtures in the main shop area (20 fixtures total).
- Wattage: 150W per fixture.
- Lumen output: 22,000 lumens per fixture.
- Source: App B-Elec (Electrical Notes); SOW-0052.

### REQ-LT-002 — Wash Bay High Bay Lighting

The lighting plan shall show 2 rows of 3 high bay LED fixtures in the wash bay area (6 fixtures total).
- Fixture type: high bay (LED assumed consistent with main shop specification — ASSUMPTION: same technology family; wattage/lumens TBD, not explicitly stated for wash bay in source).
- **TBD [A-002]:** Wash bay high bay fixture wattage and lumen output to be determined by Electrical Engineer and confirmed at preliminary design review. See CONF-LT-001 in Guidance.md.
- Source: App B-Elec (Electrical Notes); SOW-0053.

### REQ-LT-003 — Office Lighting

The lighting plan shall show 1 × 8-foot LED strip fixture in the office.
- Source: App B-Elec (Electrical Notes — source term: "8' LED"); SOW-0054.

### REQ-LT-004 — Utility Room Lighting

The lighting plan shall show 2 × 8-foot LED strip fixtures in the utility room.
- Source: App B-Elec (Electrical Notes — source term: "8' LED"); SOW-0055.

### REQ-LT-005 — Parts/Tool Room Lighting

The lighting plan shall show 6 × 8-foot LED strip fixtures in the parts/tool room.
- Source: App B-Elec (Electrical Notes — source term: "8' LED"); SOW-0056.

### REQ-LT-006 — Service Pit/Trench Lighting

The service pit/trench shall be lighted (and ventilated, per mechanical).
- Fixture type, count, and circuit: TBD (RFP §3.4 states requirement; App B-Elec does not specify pit lighting detail beyond the "L" symbol appearing near the service trench on the conceptual layout).

### REQ-LT-007 — IFC Drawing Quality and P.Eng. Stamp

All lighting plan drawings produced as IFC (Issued for Construction) documents shall be signed and stamped by a professional engineer licensed to practice in the Province of Alberta.
- Source: RFP §3.3.2; SOW-0018.

The lighting design shall comply with all applicable Alberta Safety Codes and the Alberta Building Code.
- Source: RFP §3.3.2; §3.4; SOW-0008; SOW-0009.

### REQ-LT-009 — Three-Phase Power Supply

The building electrical system, including lighting circuits, shall be served by three-phase power.
- Source: RFP §3.4; SOW-0051.
- Note: Specific lighting circuit voltage/phase configuration TBD in final design; to be coordinated with Panel Schedules (DEL-004-06).

Preliminary electrical design (DEL-004-01), which encompasses the approach to lighting layout, shall receive County approval before final lighting plans are issued.
- Source: RFP §3.3.2; SOW-0010d.
