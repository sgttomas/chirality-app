# Specification: DEL-004-07 Low-Voltage System Plans

---

## Scope

### What This Deliverable Covers

DEL-004-07 produces the IFC Drawing Set for all low-voltage systems within the New North Shop Addition. The scope may also extend to applicable renovation areas of the Old North Shop; however, the low-voltage scope for Old North Shop renovation areas is TBD and must be confirmed with the County at preliminary design (see Datasheet §Facility Context, where Old North Shop low-voltage scope is recorded as TBD). Based on available sources, the confirmed low-voltage scope encompasses:

- Security camera wiring layout (SOW-0064; App B-Elec)
- 2-way radio antenna wire routing (SOW-0065; App B-Elec)

The drawing set is part of the broader final electrical design (SOW-0014) within PKG-004 and must be coordinated with the other PKG-004 drawing sets (single-line diagram, power distribution, lighting, receptacle plans, panel schedules, and electrical specification).

### What This Deliverable Excludes

- High-voltage power distribution (covered by DEL-004-02 and DEL-004-03)
- Lighting design (covered by DEL-004-04)
- Receptacle layout (covered by DEL-004-05)
- Panel schedules (covered by DEL-004-06)
- Electrical calculations (covered by DEL-004-08)
- Electrical specification (covered by DEL-004-09)
- Physical procurement and installation of low-voltage equipment (covered by PKG-015, DEL-015-05)
- Mechanical exhaust/ventilation systems (covered by PKG-003/PKG-013)

## Requirements

### REQ-007-01 — Security Camera Wiring Shown on Plan
**Statement:** The drawing set shall show the routing for security camera wiring throughout the facility, including conduit/cable pathways and termination point locations.
**Basis:** App B-Elec (Electrical Notes: "Wiring for Security Cameras"); SOW-0064.
**Verification:** Drawing review — confirm camera wiring routes are depicted on plan view.

### REQ-007-02 — 2-Way Radio Antenna Wiring Shown on Plan
**Statement:** The drawing set shall show the routing for 2-way radio antenna wire, including conduit/cable pathways and antenna termination locations.
**Basis:** App B-Elec (Electrical Notes: "Antenna Wire for 2 Way Radios"); SOW-0065.
**Verification:** Drawing review — confirm antenna wire routes are depicted on plan view.

### REQ-007-03 — IFC Stamp
**Statement:** All low-voltage system plans forming the IFC set shall be signed and stamped by a Professional Engineer (P.Eng.) licensed in the Province of Alberta.
**Basis:** RFP §3.3.2; SOW-0018.
**Verification:** Confirm P.Eng. stamp and signature on all issued sheets prior to release for construction.

### REQ-007-04 — Alberta Safety Code Compliance
**Statement:** The low-voltage system design shall comply with all applicable Alberta Safety Codes, including the specific adopted edition of the Canadian Electrical Code Part I (CSA C22.1) in force in Alberta at the time of design. The Electrical Engineer shall identify and record the applicable code edition(s) prior to commencing detailed design.
**Basis:** RFP §3.3.2; SOW-0008, SOW-0009.
**Verification:** (1) Confirm applicable code edition(s) identified and recorded; (2) Design compliance review against each applicable code edition; (3) Safety Code permit obtained (DEL-009-03).
**Note:** The specific adopted CEC edition is TBD -- confirm with AHJ (lensing item A-001; source: Specification §Standards, Procedure Step 2).

### REQ-007-05 — Preliminary Design County Approval
**Statement:** The preliminary electrical design (DEL-004-01) shall receive County approval before the final low-voltage plans are issued for construction.
**Basis:** RFP §3.3.2; SOW-0010d.
**Verification:** County approval confirmation on record prior to IFC release.

### REQ-007-06 — Coordination with Power and Architectural Plans
**Statement:** Low-voltage wiring routes shall be coordinated with structural elements, wall/partition layouts (per architectural floor plans DEL-001-02), and power distribution runs (DEL-004-03) to avoid conflicts.
**Basis:** ASSUMPTION — standard multi-discipline IFC coordination requirement consistent with RFP §3.3.2 (complete final design, coordinated across disciplines) and SOW-0018.
**Verification:** Cross-discipline coordination review; no unresolved routing conflicts on issued drawings.

### REQ-007-07 — Inspection Coordination
**Statement:** The Contractor shall submit all inspection requests related to low-voltage rough-in and final inspection. The County representative shall be present at all applicable inspections, and copies of completed inspection reports shall be provided to the County.
**Basis:** RFP §3.3.2; SOW-0084, SOW-0085.
**Verification:** Inspection request records and County-countersigned inspection reports.

### REQ-007-08 — Additional Low-Voltage Systems
**Statement:** The Electrical Engineer shall explicitly confirm or exclude each of the following candidate low-voltage systems during preliminary design review: (a) fire alarm system, (b) data/network cabling, (c) access control, (d) intercom/PA system. If any of these systems are required by applicable Alberta Safety Codes or are requested by the County, they shall be incorporated into the drawing set. If excluded, the exclusion rationale shall be documented.
**Basis:** ASSUMPTION — RFP §3.4 ("The County only has a concept in mind; therefore, it will be a design/build project"); specific additional low-voltage subsystems not enumerated in available sources. Fire alarm is ASSUMPTION: likely code-required for this occupancy (see Guidance §Scope Uncertainty).
**Verification:** Written record of confirmed/excluded systems at preliminary design stage, with exclusion rationale where applicable.

### REQ-007-09 — Fire Alarm System Determination
**Statement:** The Electrical Engineer shall determine whether a fire alarm system is required by the Alberta Building Code and/or Alberta Fire Code for this occupancy type and building size. If required, the fire alarm system design shall be included in this drawing set or explicitly assigned to a separate deliverable with documented handoff.
**Basis:** ASSUMPTION — fire alarm is typically code-required for industrial occupancies; not stated in available sources. See Guidance §Scope Uncertainty.
**Verification:** AHJ determination documented; if required, fire alarm shown on plans or exclusion/handoff documented.
