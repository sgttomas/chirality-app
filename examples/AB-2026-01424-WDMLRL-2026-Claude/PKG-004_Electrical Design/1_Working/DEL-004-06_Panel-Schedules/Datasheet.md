## Construction

### Relationship to Other PKG-004 Deliverables

The Panel Schedules deliverable is one of nine deliverables within PKG-004 (Electrical Design). Panel Schedules are typically produced after or concurrently with:

- DEL-004-02 Single-Line Diagram (establishes overall distribution hierarchy)
- DEL-004-03 Power Distribution Plans (establishes panel locations and feeder routing)
- DEL-004-04 Lighting Plans (establishes lighting circuit counts)
- DEL-004-05 Receptacle Layout Plans (establishes receptacle circuit counts)
- DEL-004-08 Electrical Calculation Package (provides load calculations that inform panel ratings)

The Panel Schedules feed into:
- DEL-004-09 Electrical Specification (references panel types and ratings)
- PKG-015 Electrical & Low-Voltage Installation (used for procurement and installation)

**ASSUMPTION:** The sequencing above is inferred from standard electrical design workflow conventions. The decomposition does not explicitly state inter-deliverable sequencing within PKG-004.

### Schedule Format (Typical)

ASSUMPTION: A panel schedule for this project type typically includes, per panel:
- Panel designation and location
- Main breaker/disconnect rating
- Bus rating
- Voltage and phase configuration
- For each circuit: circuit number, load description, breaker size, wire size/type, load (amps and/or VA), notes
- Totals: connected load, demand load, spare capacity

Exact format to be established by the Electrical Engineer per CEC Part I conventions and the Electrical Specification (DEL-004-09).
