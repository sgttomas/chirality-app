## Scope

### Included

This specification covers the construction of a new locker/change room facility within the existing Old North Shop building at the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill site. The scope includes:

- Construction of new interior partitions/walls to create an enclosed locker and change room space within the Old North Shop (105' × 40' existing building).
- Supply and installation of a locker system.
- Installation of laundry services (washer and dryer connections and appliances; specifics TBD at design phase).
- Plumbing rough-in and fixture installation for the locker/change room (sink(s), drain(s), and laundry connections).
- Electrical rough-in, lighting, receptacles, and exhaust fan installation serving the locker/change room.
- Ventilation/exhaust for the change room occupancy.
- Interior finishes (flooring, wall, ceiling) — specifications TBD; minimum performance criteria (e.g., slip resistance rating for flooring, moisture resistance for walls) to be defined at design phase [D-003].
- Doors and hardware — specifications TBD; fire rating to be determined based on fire separation analysis (see REQ-017-04-12) [F-001].
- Coordination with DEL-017-03 (Washroom Renovation & Expansion) for shared utility infrastructure.

**Source:** SOW-0073 (WDMLRL_Decomposition_Claude.md); RFP §3.4

### Excluded

- Renovation of the Old North Shop mezzanine (DEL-017-02).
- Washroom renovation and urinal addition (DEL-017-03), though coordination is required.
- Kitchenette renovation (DEL-017-01).
- New North Shop building construction.
- Site works, grading, and civil (PKG-018).
- Supply of welding equipment or any County-supplied items.
- Permit fees (paid by County per RFP §3.3.1).

## Requirements

### REQ-017-04-01 — Locker/Change Room Construction
**Statement:** The General Contractor shall construct a new locker and change room within the Old North Shop building.
**Basis:** SOW-0073 (WDMLRL_Decomposition_Claude.md); RFP §3.4 — "Expand washroom facilities to include urinal and locker change room."
**Status:** Mandatory.

### REQ-017-04-02 — Laundry Services
**Statement:** The locker/change room shall include laundry services. Laundry equipment specifications (washer, dryer, hookups) are TBD pending design.
**Basis:** SOW-0073 (WDMLRL_Decomposition_Claude.md) — "Construct new locker/change room with laundry services"; RFP §3.4 — "including laundry services."
**Status:** Mandatory.
**TBD [C-001]:** Clarify whether "laundry services" scope includes supply and installation of appliances (washer, dryer) or only rough-in (plumbing hookups, electrical circuits, dryer exhaust duct). Current wording is ambiguous on procurement responsibility. Procedure Step 2.12 assumes both supply and installation. Design team / County to confirm scope boundary.

### REQ-017-04-03 — Code Compliance
**Statement:** All construction, plumbing, and electrical work shall comply with applicable Alberta Safety Codes and all local, provincial, and national codes and regulations applicable to the locker/change room occupancy type.
**Basis:** RFP §3.3.2 — "The proposed building must adhere to all Alberta Safety Codes."
**Status:** Mandatory.
**TBD [A-001]:** The specific NBC occupancy classification for the locker/change room (e.g., Group D, Group E) has not been stated. The design team / building code consultant must confirm the occupancy classification, as it directly affects ventilation rates, egress width, plumbing fixture counts, and fire separation requirements.

### REQ-017-04-04 — P.Eng.-Stamped IFC Drawings
**Statement:** Issued-for-Construction drawings for the locker/change room shall be signed and stamped by a Professional Engineer licensed to practice in the Province of Alberta.
**Basis:** RFP §3.3.2 — "All Issued For Construction Drawings must be signed/stamped by a professional engineer licensed to practice in the province of Alberta."
**Status:** Mandatory.
**Note:** Architectural design coordination falls under PKG-001 (DEL-001-10_Renovation-Plans covers Old North Shop renovation plans including SOW-0073).
**TBD [F-002]:** Clarify whether P.Eng. stamp requirement extends to mechanical/ventilation drawings in addition to architectural, structural, electrical, and plumbing. The Documentation table lists architectural, plumbing, and electrical IFC drawings but does not list mechanical/ventilation separately. If mechanical drawings are required for the exhaust ventilation system, they should be added to the Documentation artifacts.
