## Scope

### What This Specification Covers

This specification governs the renovation of the existing 2nd-level mezzanine (referred to as the "coffee room" on Appendix B conceptual drawings) within the Old North Shop at the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill site, SW 14-44-21-W4, near Ferintosh, Alberta.

The scope of work for DEL-017-02, per SOW-0071, is: **"Renovate Old North Shop mezzanine (2nd-level coffee room)."**

*Source: WDMLRL_Decomposition_Claude.md §8, SOW-0071; App B (Shop) floor plan.*

The renovation includes all labour, materials, equipment, and management required to bring the existing mezzanine to a fully functional, code-compliant condition suitable for its intended use as a coffee room (break room) at the 2nd level.

### What This Specification Excludes

| Exclusion | Notes |
|-----------|-------|
| New mezzanine construction in the New North Shop addition | Covered by DEL-011-07 (Mezzanine Structure & Stairs, SOW-0032/0033/0034) |
| Washroom renovation below the mezzanine | Covered by DEL-017-03 (SOW-0072, SOW-0074) |
| Kitchenette renovation in Old North Shop | Covered by DEL-017-01 (SOW-0070) |
| New locker/change room | Covered by DEL-017-04 (SOW-0073) |
| Civil/sitework | Covered by PKG-018 |
| County-supplied items (aggregate, brushing/clearing) | RFP §3.3.1 |
| Permit fees (development permit, building permit, Safety Code permit fees) | County responsibility per RFP §3.3.1 |

## Requirements

### REQ-017-02-001: Scope Completion
**Statement:** The General Contractor shall complete the full renovation of the existing Old North Shop mezzanine (2nd-level coffee room (break room)) as defined by SOW-0071 and the approved design drawings. "Approved design drawings" means the Issued for Construction (IFC) drawing set that has been (a) stamped and signed by the design-build team's P.Eng., and (b) reviewed and accepted by the County project representative, with approval documented in writing (e.g., approval transmittal or sign-off sheet). The County review/acceptance process is described in Procedure Step 3.5-3.6.
**Source:** SOW-0071 (WDMLRL_Decomposition_Claude.md §8); RFP §3.3.2. ASSUMPTION — County review/acceptance process inferred from RFP design-build context and Procedure Step 3.5-3.6; specific approval artifact not defined in RFP. Enrichment ref: F-001.
**Priority:** Mandatory.

### REQ-017-02-002: Structural Assessment Prior to Construction
**Statement:** A structural engineering assessment of the existing mezzanine shall be completed and approved before renovation design is finalized and before construction begins. The assessment shall be performed by a professional engineer licensed to practice in Alberta (APEGA-registered P.Eng.) with demonstrated structural engineering experience. The assessment report shall address structural adequacy, load capacity, stair condition, and code compliance, and shall provide a clear recommendation (proceed with renovation / remediate specific deficiencies / mezzanine not economically repairable).
**Source:** _DEPENDENCIES.md (STRUCT-ASSESS-001); WDMLRL_Decomposition_Claude.md PKG-017 package description; _CONTEXT.md. **Qualification requirement:** ASSUMPTION — APEGA registration is a legal requirement for engineering practice in Alberta; structural experience requirement is standard practice. Enrichment ref: C-002.
**Priority:** Mandatory — gates design and construction.

### REQ-017-02-003: Existing Documentation
**Statement:** Original mezzanine drawings and a condition survey shall be obtained and reviewed prior to finalizing the renovation design.
**Source:** _DEPENDENCIES.md (EXISTING-DOCS-001).
**Priority:** Mandatory — gates design.

### REQ-017-02-004: Code Compliance
**Statement:** All Work shall comply with all applicable Alberta Safety Codes and the current edition of the Alberta Building Code.
**Source:** RFP §3.3.2 ("The proposed building must adhere to all Alberta Safety Codes"); RFP §3.3.2 ("building design must adhere to all applicable building codes and regulations").
**Priority:** Mandatory.

### REQ-017-02-005: Engineered Drawings

**Source:** RFP §3.3.2.
**Priority:** Mandatory.

### REQ-017-02-006: Permit Acquisition
**Statement:** The General Contractor shall obtain all required building permits and applicable Safety Code permits for the renovation work. (Permit fees are paid by the County — see Exclusions.)
**Source:** RFP §3.3.2.
**Priority:** Mandatory.

### REQ-017-02-007: Inspection Coordination
**Statement:** All inspection requests shall be submitted by the General Contractor. The County project representative must be present for all inspections and shall receive copies of all complete inspection reports.
**Source:** RFP §3.3.2.
**Priority:** Mandatory.

### REQ-017-02-008: Flooring Replacement
**Statement:** Existing mezzanine flooring shall be assessed and replaced or refinished as determined by the approved renovation design. Flooring material, finish, and specification shall be defined in the approved renovation design. If design determines existing flooring is adequate, a written justification shall be included in the design package.
**Source:** ASSUMPTION — "full renovation" context implied by SOW-0071 and typical renovation scope; specific flooring material not stated in RFP. Flagged as ASSUMPTION until design drawings are issued.
**Priority:** Mandatory (design-gated) — flooring scope is mandatory as part of the renovation; the specific approach (replacement vs. refinish) is determined by design. Enrichment ref: A-003.

### REQ-017-02-009: Stair and Railing Condition and Compliance
**Statement:** Existing stairs and railings providing access to the mezzanine shall be assessed and upgraded as necessary to comply with current Alberta Building Code requirements for guard height, stair geometry, and structural adequacy.
**Source:** ASSUMPTION — code compliance requirement (REQ-017-02-004) implies stair/railing upgrade if existing conditions do not meet current code. Specific stair scope not stated in RFP.
**Priority:** Conditional — mandatory if non-compliant; determined by structural/code assessment.

### REQ-017-02-010: Electrical Updates
**Statement:** Electrical service to the mezzanine shall be reviewed and updated as required to support the intended coffee room (break room) use, including lighting and outlet provisions meeting applicable codes.
**Source:** ASSUMPTION — standard renovation scope; no explicit electrical line item in RFP for Old North Shop mezzanine renovation. Electrical coordination with DEL-017-03 and DEL-017-04 may be required.
**Priority:** Expected — subject to design and electrical assessment.

### REQ-017-02-011: Finishing Work
**Statement:** Interior finishing of the mezzanine shall be completed as part of the renovation. Finish specifications (walls, ceiling, paint, etc.) shall be defined in the approved design.
**Source:** ASSUMPTION — full renovation context; specific finishes not defined in RFP.
**Priority:** Expected — subject to design confirmation.
