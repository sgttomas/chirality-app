# DEL-017-03 | Washroom Renovation & Expansion — Specification

---

## Scope

### What this deliverable covers

DEL-017-03 covers the complete renovation and expansion of the existing washroom located below the mezzanine (2nd-level coffee room area) in the Old North Shop at the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill site, Camrose County, Alberta.

Specifically, this deliverable includes:
- Renovation of the existing washroom below the Old North Shop mezzanine (SOW-0072, source: App B)
- Expansion of washroom facilities to include the addition of a urinal (SOW-0074, source: RFP §3.4)
- All associated demolition of existing fixtures and finishes as required to execute the renovation
- All plumbing connections, rough-in, fixtures, and finishes required to produce a fully functional, code-compliant washroom
- Coordination with plumbing design (PKG-006) and plumbing installation (PKG-014) for service connections
- Coordination with mechanical (PKG-013) and electrical (PKG-015) trades for ventilation and lighting as required

## Requirements

### REQ-001 — Washroom Renovation (Mandatory)
**Statement:** The existing washroom below the mezzanine in the Old North Shop shall be renovated.
**Source:** SOW-0072, App B (floor plan note); Decomposition §3-I
**Verification:** Completed renovation confirmed by final inspection and County sign-off.

### REQ-002 — Urinal Addition (Mandatory)
**Statement:** The washroom facilities shall be expanded to include at least one urinal.
**Source:** SOW-0074, RFP §3.4; Decomposition §3-I
**Verification:** Urinal installed and functional; confirmed by plumbing inspection.

### REQ-003 — Code Compliance (Mandatory)
**Statement:** All renovation and expansion work shall comply with all applicable Alberta Safety Codes and applicable building codes and regulations.
**Source:** RFP §3.3.2 ("The proposed building must adhere to all Alberta Safety Codes"); RFP §3.3.2 ("The building design must adhere to all applicable building codes and regulations"); OBJ-001
**Verification:** Passed all required Safety Code inspections; inspection reports provided to County.

### REQ-004 — IFC Documentation (Mandatory)
**Statement:** Issued-For-Construction drawings covering the washroom renovation shall be produced, signed and stamped by a professional engineer licensed to practice in Alberta.
**Source:** RFP §3.3.2
**Verification:** P.Eng.-stamped IFC drawing set issued prior to construction commencement; design covered under PKG-001 (DEL-001-10_Renovation-Plans) and PKG-006.

### REQ-005 — Inspection Coordination (Mandatory)
**Statement:** All inspection requests shall be submitted by the General Contractor. The County project representative must be present for all inspections and shall receive copies of all completed inspection reports.
**Source:** RFP §3.3.2 (SOW-0084, SOW-0085)
**Verification:** Inspection requests submitted; reports delivered to County.

### REQ-006 — Plumbing Functional Completeness (Mandatory)
**Statement:** Upon completion, the washroom shall be fully functional with operational plumbing fixtures including water supply, drainage, and venting connected to the project's water distribution and septic systems.
**Source:** ASSUMPTION — derived from SOW-0072 (renovation), App B (Plumbing) showing floor drain and water tap at washroom, and requirement to pass inspections (OBJ-001). Specific fixture schedule TBD in design.
**Verification:** Functional testing of all fixtures per the following acceptance criteria: (a) no leaks at supply connections, drain connections, or fixture joints during a pressurized hold test (duration and pressure TBD by plumbing designer, PKG-006); (b) drain confirmed clear via water flow test; (c) adequate water pressure at each fixture (minimum pressure TBD by plumbing designer); (d) plumbing inspection passed. [D-001]

### REQ-007 — Ventilation (ASSUMPTION)
**Statement:** The renovated washroom shall be provided with mechanical ventilation meeting applicable Alberta Safety Code requirements.
**Source:** ASSUMPTION — required by Alberta building and safety codes for washroom spaces; specific system TBD in mechanical design (PKG-003/PKG-013). No explicit ventilation specification found in RFP for this specific room.
**Verification:** Ventilation system inspected and confirmed compliant. Commissioning acceptance criteria: minimum exhaust air flow rate TBD by mechanical designer (PKG-003) per applicable Alberta Building Code requirements for washroom exhaust; operational test confirming continuous exhaust during occupancy. [F-003]

### REQ-008 — Accessibility (ASSUMPTION)
**Statement:** The renovated washroom shall meet applicable Alberta Building Code barrier-free accessibility provisions (ASSUMPTION: Alberta Building Code Part 3, Division B, Section 3.8 — Barrier-Free Design; specific edition and applicability to be confirmed by design team during CODE-REVIEW-001 / ACCESS-REVIEW-001). The specific accessibility provisions triggered by this renovation (e.g., extent of upgrade required for existing building renovation) are TBD pending code review.
**Source:** ASSUMPTION — required for code-compliant renovation of existing facilities; specific standard to be confirmed in design. Not explicitly stated in RFP for this room. [A-002]
**Verification:** Accessibility review completed and confirmed during design and inspection phases. Measurable acceptance criteria TBD pending code review — expected to include, at minimum: (a) accessible turning radius per applicable code section; (b) grab bar installation where required; (c) fixture clearances per applicable code section; (d) accessible door hardware and clearance. Design team to define specific pass/fail criteria based on applicable code edition (CODE-REVIEW-001 / ACCESS-REVIEW-001). [A-003]

### REQ-009 — Completion Deadline (Mandatory)
**Statement:** All work, including this washroom renovation, shall be complete on or before December 31, 2026.
**Source:** RFP §3.1; SOW-0091
**Verification:** CCC issued on or before December 31, 2026.

### REQ-010 — Warranty (Mandatory)
**Statement:** All materials and workmanship shall be guaranteed for the construction period plus 2 years following the Construction Completion Certificate (CCC). Defects shall be rectified within 10 days of Owner's written instruction.
**Source:** RFP §2.10.2, §2.10.6
**Verification:** Warranty obligations maintained through guarantee period; defects corrected on instruction.

### REQ-011 — Finish Material Performance (ASSUMPTION)
**Statement:** Finish materials for the renovated washroom (floor, wall, ceiling) shall meet minimum performance criteria appropriate for an industrial maintenance facility washroom, including: (a) moisture resistance; (b) durability suitable for heavy-use commercial/industrial application; (c) slip resistance for floor surfaces (minimum coefficient of friction TBD by design team). ASSUMPTION: specific material specifications and minimum ratings to be established in the Finish Schedule (DEL-001-08).
**Source:** ASSUMPTION — derived from industrial maintenance facility context (landfill operations environment) and standard practice for commercial/industrial washroom finishes. No explicit finish performance criteria found in RFP. See Guidance, Considerations, Lifecycle Maintenance. [D-002]
**Verification:** Finish materials confirmed to meet specified performance criteria prior to installation; design review of Finish Schedule (DEL-001-08).
