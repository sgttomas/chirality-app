# Specification — DEL-007-04 Landscape Specification

---

## Scope

### What This Specification Covers

This specification governs the materials, workmanship, quality standards, and performance requirements for the landscape architectural scope of the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop Addition project (AB-2026-01424). It applies to:

- Site preparation and grading integration for landscape areas
- Gravel driving surface construction on the expansion lot
- Cement pad and gravel pad construction as shown on conceptual drawings
- Topsoil stripping from all driving surface areas
- Exterior mud sump for the wash bay
- Planting and site restoration works (requirements TBD pending DEL-007-03)
- Workmanship and material guarantee obligations for all landscape scope

This specification is produced by the Landscape Architect and is coordinated with:
- Civil Design (PKG-005): site grading plan, drainage features, stormwater management
- Sitework & Civil Construction (PKG-018): execution of grading, landscaping, driving surfaces, and pads

### What This Specification Excludes

- Brushing and clearing of the project area (County responsibility per RFP §3.3.1)
- Bulk cut and fill earthworks (County responsibility per RFP §3.3.1)
- Supply of aggregate (gravel) (County/Landfill responsibility per RFP §3.3.1 and App B)
- Supply of welding equipment specifications (County responsibility per RFP §3.4)
- Relocation of existing septic tank (excluded per D-002)
- Architectural, structural, mechanical, electrical, plumbing disciplines (separate packages)
- Foundation design and construction (PKG-010)
