## Scope

### What This Deliverable Covers

DEL-018-06 covers the coordination and physical installation of all utility service tie-ins required to bring the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop Addition into full operational service. Three utility systems are explicitly in scope:

1. **Natural Gas** (SOW-0080) — coordinate and execute natural gas service tie-in
2. **Electrical Service** (SOW-0081) — coordinate and execute electrical service tie-in (three-phase power)
3. **Communication Lines** (SOW-0082) — coordinate and execute communication lines tie-in

This deliverable encompasses: utility provider coordination, permit acquisition for utility-related work, design coordination, trenching, conduit/pipe installation, service connections, metering, building penetrations, testing, commissioning, and handover.

Sources: RFP SS3.3.2; Decomposition SS3 SOW-0080/0081/0082; Decomposition SS7 DEL-018-06.
