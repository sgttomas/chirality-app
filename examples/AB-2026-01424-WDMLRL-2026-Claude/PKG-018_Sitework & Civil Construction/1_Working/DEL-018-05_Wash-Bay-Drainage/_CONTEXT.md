## Context Summary
This deliverable covers the complete wash bay drainage and treatment system design and installation. Work includes excavation of drainage swales, installation of catch basins, sump pit construction, grading for drainage flow, filter systems if required, and proper discharge arrangements. This system manages stormwater and wash water from the wash bay area, preventing environmental contamination and ensuring operational compliance. The mud sump allows settlement of sediment before water discharge.
