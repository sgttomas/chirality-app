DEL-002-09 is one of twelve structural deliverables; a pro-rata share of ~$3,750 per deliverable is in the same order of magnitude as this LOE-based estimate of $19,230 when recognizing that drawing set deliverables (with full BIM/detail production) are among the more effort-intensive deliverables compared to specifications, calculation packages, and preliminary design. The LOE-based estimate is within a plausible range but on the higher end, consistent with the 84 hours allocated to the Structural Engineer for a detail-heavy drawing set (stair with multiple connection types, guardrail details, and code compliance documentation).
