## Key Warnings and Coverage Gaps

1. **Foundation type TBD.** The geotechnical investigation (PKG-008) has not been completed. All foundation quantities (footing dimensions, excavation depths, concrete volumes) are preliminary assumptions based on normal ground conditions. The actual foundation system may differ significantly (e.g., piled foundation vs. spread footings).

2. **Variable-price scope.** Per RFP §4.8.2, this deliverable is priced as a variable-price item. This estimate is a preliminary parametric basis for proposal purposes; final pricing is negotiated post-geotech.

3. **Service pit excluded.** The scope boundary between PKG-010 (Foundation) and PKG-011 (Superstructure) for the service pit is unresolved (Conflict CF-010-01). The service pit structure is excluded from this estimate pending human ruling.

4. **Concrete slab dominates cost.** The heavy-duty slab-on-grade (DL-009, $253,680 CAD) represents 44.5% of the total estimate. This item is sensitive to slab thickness and reinforcement assumptions, which are TBD per structural design.

5. **County-supplied aggregate.** The granular fill line item (DL-003) includes placement cost only; aggregate material is supplied by the County per RFP §3.3.1. If this assumption changes, costs would increase.

6. **No cold-weather premium included.** If foundation construction extends into winter months (November-March), cold-weather concrete protection costs would be an additional premium not included in this estimate.

7. **Embedded items allowance.** The embedded items line (DL-020, $12,000 CAD) is a low-confidence parametric allowance. Actual quantities depend on structural and MEP drawings not yet available.
