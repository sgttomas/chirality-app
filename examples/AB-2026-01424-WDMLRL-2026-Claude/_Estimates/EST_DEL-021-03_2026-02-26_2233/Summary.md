## Key Warnings and Coverage Gaps

1. **3 of 5 insurance premium lines are TBD.** WCB, E&O, and Employer's Liability premiums cannot be priced without: (a) total project payroll for WCB assessment, (b) Proponent's claims history and insurer quotes for E&O, (c) employer's liability premium details. These represent material cost gaps.

2. **Builder's risk coverage status is uncertain.** RFP §4.2 does not explicitly require it; CCDC 14-2013 provisions (not accessible) may. The $14,000 estimate is included as a parametric allowance but may be eliminated or increased depending on contract review.

3. **Insurance premiums are project-value-dependent.** The Fees_Permits_Insurance.csv ranges (FP-03: $8,000-$22,000; FP-04: $3,500-$9,500) are wide. Actual premiums will depend on the final contract value, which is not yet determined.

4. **No subcontractor insurance costs are included.** Per ASMP-01, subcontractors are assumed responsible for their own insurance. If the Proponent must procure a wrap-up or OCIP policy, this would add significant cost.

5. **E&O tail coverage cost is not included.** Per Guidance note C-002, tail coverage may be needed for the 2-year guarantee period. This is an additional cost not captured in the estimate.
