## Key Warnings and Coverage Gaps

1. **All rates are MEDIUM confidence parametric estimates.** No firm quotes or negotiated rates are included. Actual rates may differ.
2. **Hours are parametric estimates.** The 130-hour total (84 hrs Structural Engineer, 36 hrs BIM Technician, 6 hrs PM, 4 hrs Cost Estimator) is a parametric allocation for a Drawing Set deliverable. Actual effort may vary based on design complexity, number of revision cycles, and coordination intensity.
3. **Service pit is structurally complex relative to typical drawing sets.** The 24 ft x 100 ft below-grade reinforced concrete pit requires wall design for lateral earth pressure, surcharge from heavy equipment, possible hydrostatic pressure, waterproofing, and multi-discipline penetration coordination. The 84 Structural Engineer hours reflect this complexity (compared to 56 hours for the full Structural Calculation Package DEL-002-10 which covers all building subsystems).
4. **Geotechnical dependency is a cost risk.** If DEL-008-01 reveals adverse conditions (high groundwater, poor bearing capacity, deep frost), redesign effort may exceed the parametric hour allocation. This is the primary cost risk.
5. **Cover system type is TBD.** The cover system design (grating vs. steel plate vs. proprietary) is a significant design decision not yet resolved (Guidance T-3). The selected option may affect the BIM Technician drawing effort and Structural Engineer analysis hours.
6. **Multi-discipline coordination is intensive.** The service pit requires coordination with Mechanical (ventilation), Electrical (lighting), Plumbing (drainage), and Architectural (access, floor plan) disciplines. If coordination rounds increase, PM and Structural Engineer hours may exceed the parametric allocation.
7. **No material or equipment costs.** This is a professional engineering design labor deliverable only. The construction cost of the service pit itself is under DEL-011-06 (PKG-011).
8. **Equipment load specification is TBD from County.** If the County's equipment data requires multiple design iterations for the cover system, additional hours may be needed beyond this estimate.
