# Risk Register — EST_DEL-011-07_2026-02-27_0955

| Risk ID | Risk Description | Likelihood | Impact | Affected Lines | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| RSK-001 | Mezzanine floor area significantly exceeds the assumed 93 m2 once IFC drawings are issued. If the mezzanine fully spans the wash bay (CFT-011-07-01), area could be 120-140 m2 instead of 93 m2. | MEDIUM | HIGH — would increase area-scaled costs by 30-50% (~$28,000-$47,000 increase) | DL-001, DL-002, DL-006, DL-007, DL-017, DL-018, DL-019 | Resolve CFT-011-07-01 at preliminary design review. Obtain floor area from IFC drawings (DEL-002-05) before committing procurement. |
| RSK-002 | Structural system changes from assumed steel framing to concrete. Unit rates for concrete mezzanine structures differ materially from steel. | LOW | HIGH — would require re-derivation of framing and material rates | DL-001, DL-017 | Confirm structural system at preliminary design review with Structural Engineer of Record. |
| RSK-003 | Alberta Safety Codes require a second means of egress (second stair) for the mezzanine level, depending on occupant load and area. | LOW | MEDIUM — would add approximately $9,000-$15,000 for a second stair system | DL-003 | Confirm egress requirements at design stage with Safety Codes Officer. |
| RSK-004 | Design live load requirement exceeds 7.2 kPa (top of the Guidance industrial range), requiring heavier steel members and potentially increasing the $/m2 framing rate. | LOW | MEDIUM — could increase DL-001 and DL-017 by 10-20% | DL-001, DL-017 | Confirm design live load with Structural Engineer of Record. |
| RSK-005 | Steel material price escalation between estimate date (Feb 2026) and procurement date. Structural steel prices are volatile. | MEDIUM | MEDIUM — 5-15% price movement possible over 3-6 months | DL-001, DL-017, DL-020 | Include escalation contingency; lock in steel fabricator quote early. |
| RSK-006 | Rural Alberta site location (near Ferintosh) may result in higher delivery charges for fabricated steel and concrete than assumed in parametric rates, which are based on urban/suburban benchmarks. | MEDIUM | LOW — could add $2,000-$5,000 in delivery premiums | DL-017, DL-018 | Obtain site-specific delivery quotes from fabricators/suppliers. |
