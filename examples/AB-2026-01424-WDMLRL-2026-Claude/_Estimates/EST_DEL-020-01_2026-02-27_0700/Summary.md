## Key Warnings and Coverage Gaps

1. **TBD: Crane load testing third-party costs (DL-020-01-011).** The two 5-tonne overhead cranes require load testing per Alberta Safety Code. Third-party inspector fees and/or test weight rental costs are not available in any provided price source. This is a material gap.

2. **TBD: Safety Code inspection fees (DL-020-01-018).** Third-party Safety Code inspection fees for commissioning-phase inspections are not priced. These are typically Owner-borne (RFP S3.3.1 states permit fees are Owner responsibility) but coordination effort is included in PM and Cx Agent hours.

3. **Non-labour costs not modelled.** The parametric model covers professional staff labour only. Non-labour costs (test equipment rental, consumables, printing/binding for O&M manuals, training materials) are assumed negligible for an industrial commissioning scope of this size but are not explicitly priced.

4. **All acceptance criteria TBD.** Specific performance acceptance criteria for most systems depend on IFC design documents not yet available. This does not affect cost estimation but introduces risk of scope change if criteria reveal additional testing requirements.

5. **Commissioning Agent not yet assigned.** The role is priced at the parametric rate ($160/hr) but the actual cost will depend on whether a dedicated Cx Agent is engaged or the PM absorbs the role.
