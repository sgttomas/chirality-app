## Key Warnings and Coverage Gaps

1. **Warranty remediation costs are excluded.** This estimate covers the professional services (administration) cost of managing the warranty period. Actual deficiency rectification costs (materials, trades, subcontractors) are borne by the Contractor per RFP §2.10.7 and are not included in this deliverable's scope.
2. **Inspection travel costs not included.** The facility is in a rural location (near Ferintosh, AB). Travel costs for scheduled inspections during the 2-year warranty period are not explicitly modelled in the Level_of_Effort hours.
3. **Contingency for additional deficiency volume not included.** The parametric model assumes a "standard" level of warranty administration effort. If deficiency volume is materially higher than typical, additional hours may be required.
4. **Items.csv contains 15 priceable items; Detail.csv prices 8 labour lines.** The 15 items in the quantity takeoff are covered by the 8 role-hour allocations from the Level_of_Effort model. The parametric model allocates hours by role (not by activity), so individual activity-level items are mapped to the closest role allocation.
