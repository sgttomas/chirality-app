## Key Warnings and Coverage Gaps

1. **Scope Coverage:** DEL-002-01 scope is fully covered by the 4 priced labour lines. The 7 lump-sum items (ITM-005 through ITM-011) in Items.csv represent scope traceability entries whose effort is embedded within the 4 labour lines — they are not separately priced.

2. **SCC Bundle:** DEL-002-01 is bundled with DEL-002-10 (Structural Calculation Package) for iterative coordination. This estimate prices DEL-002-01 independently as instructed. Coordination effort between the two is assumed to be included in the PM and Structural Engineer hours.

3. **No Material Costs:** This is a professional services deliverable (design presentation). No material, equipment, or construction costs are included — those are scoped to construction packages (PKG-010, PKG-011).

4. **All Rates MEDIUM Confidence:** Both the hour estimates and hourly rates carry MEDIUM confidence per the source files.

5. **Foundation Variable-Price:** The preliminary design includes a foundation narrative but the foundation itself is a variable-price scope element (RFP §4.8.2). Foundation design costs are in DEL-002-11.
