## 6. Warning Analysis

### 6.1 Status Distribution

- **OK (65 deliverables):** All design-services packages with complete LOE models (PKG-001, PKG-003, PKG-005, PKG-006, PKG-007) achieved 100% OK. Following Rev 1, PKG-001 (Architectural Design) upgraded from 10/11 to 11/11 OK with the resolution of DEL-001-11 TBDs. Eight deliverables upgraded from WARNINGS to OK after TBD resolution.
- **WARNINGS (52 deliverables):** All construction packages (PKG-010 through PKG-018) returned WARNINGS. This is expected — construction estimates rely on parametric unit rates with assumptions about quantities, material specifications, and site conditions that are not yet confirmed at this project stage. ### 6.3 Critical Uncertainty Drivers

1. **DEL-011-01 Structural Steel ($2.26M, 31% of total):** Tonnage derived from parametric building-area rates. A +/-15% variation in steel tonnage would shift the project total by +/-$340K. This deliverable alone warrants early structural engineering refinement.

2. **DEL-010-01 Foundation & Concrete ($570K, 8%):** Dependent on geotechnical report (DEL-008-01). Foundation type, depth, and reinforcement are assumptions. Geotech findings could change this by +/-30%.

3. **DEL-016-01 Equipment ($457K, 6%):** Equipment pricing uses parametric allowances from rate tables, including a $25K anti-collision system allowance added in Rev 1. Vendor quotations would materially improve confidence.

4. **PKG-018 Sitework ($590K, 8%):** Grading volumes, drainage infrastructure, and paving areas derive from conceptual site plans. IFC civil drawings will govern.
