### Scope Description

DEL-009-03 covers the Proponent's professional services effort to obtain and manage all Safety Code permits for the WDMLRL Maintenance Shop Addition project. The estimate covers the 5-phase permitting process described in the Procedure document:

1. **Phase 1 — Safety Code Audit:** Identification of all applicable Alberta Safety Code permit categories (anticipated: building, electrical, fire, gas, plumbing, elevator/crane) and associated requirements.
2. **Phase 2 — Prime Contractor Designation:** Completion and submission of the Prime Contractor Designation form per RFP Section 2.15.
3. **Phase 3 — Permit Applications:** Preparation, submission, and tracking of all permit applications.
4. **Phase 4 — Inspection Coordination:** Scheduling, facilitation, report distribution, and deficiency management for all required inspections.
5. **Phase 5 — Permit Receipt and Compliance:** Recording of issued permits, integration of conditions, and handoff to DEL-009-04.

**Excluded from estimate:**
- Permit fees (County responsibility per RFP Section 3.3.1)
- Ongoing OH&S program management (DEL-019-01 scope)
- Code Compliance Register maintenance (DEL-009-04 scope)
- Design discipline effort for IFC drawings (PKG-001 through PKG-007)

### Key Warnings and Coverage Gaps

1. **LOE Data Sparsity:** The Level_of_Effort.csv provides only 10 hours (R-01: 6 hr, R-08: 4 hr) for DEL-009-03. The 5-phase permitting process with 6+ permit categories clearly requires additional specialist effort. Items ITM-009-03-03 through ITM-009-03-11 were priced using PARAMETRIC estimates with assumed hours. This is the primary source of estimate uncertainty.

2. **Permit Category TBD:** The exact number and type of Safety Code permit categories is TBD pending safety code audit. The estimate assumes approximately 6 categories (building, electrical, fire, gas, plumbing, elevator/crane) based on RFP Section 3.4 building systems. Actual count could be higher or lower.

3. **Inspection Volume TBD:** The number of inspections per permit category is unknown. The inspection coordination line item (L-009-03-08, $2,780) is a parametric allowance that may need adjustment once permit conditions are known.

4. **Confidence Level:** 2 of 11 lines are MED confidence (LOE-backed); 9 of 11 lines are LOW confidence (parametric assumptions without LOE backing). Overall estimate confidence is LOW.

5. **No Material Costs:** This is a 100% professional services / labour estimate. No materials, equipment, or third-party fees are included (permit fees are County's responsibility).
