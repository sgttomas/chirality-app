2. **Workforce headcount (TBD):** Not stated in RFP or decomposition. Drives locker count (assumed 8 EA), bench sizing, and plumbing fixture count. Obtaining this from County is a prerequisite per Procedure Step 1.1.

6. **Finish specifications (TBD [D-003]):** All finish selections (flooring, walls, ceiling) are priced at mid-range parametric rates. Actual specifications could move costs materially in either direction.
