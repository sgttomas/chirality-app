# Specification — DEL-008-04 As-Built Survey

---

## Scope

### What This Deliverable Covers

DEL-008-04 covers the as-built survey of the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop Addition following construction completion. The scope includes:

- Final topographic survey of the constructed site, documenting actual finished conditions.
- Facility layout documentation: building addition footprint, position, and elevation.
- As-built coordinate and elevation data for all principal constructed components.
- Construction verification: confirming that constructed elements conform to the Issued for Construction (IFC) design.
- Final record documentation sufficient for operations, maintenance, and future modifications.

The as-built survey is the final survey deliverable in the project lifecycle, following DEL-008-02 (Preliminary Site Survey) and DEL-008-03 (Construction Survey).

Sources: RFP §3.3.2 (SOW-0004); _CONTEXT.md; WDMLRL_Decomposition_Claude.md (PKG-008, DEL-008-04).

### What This Deliverable Excludes

- Geotechnical investigation (DEL-008-01).
- Preliminary site survey (DEL-008-02).
- Construction survey staking and control (DEL-008-03).
- Foundation design or engineering (PKG-002).
- Civil design and grading design documentation (PKG-005).
- Any ongoing operational or environmental monitoring surveys.

## Requirements

### REQ-001 — Survey Mandate
**The Proponent (Surveyor) shall conduct an as-built survey of the completed project.**
Source: RFP §3.3.2 ("Conduct preliminary, construction, and as-built survey"); SOW-0004.
Status: Explicit requirement.

### REQ-002 — Scope of Components to Be Documented
**The as-built survey shall document all principal constructed components, including at minimum:**
- New maintenance shop addition building footprint, finished floor elevation, and building envelope extent.
- Constructed gravel driving surfaces and their extents.
- Grading and drainage features as constructed (positive drainage, drainage swales or features).
- Cistern location and associated plumbing tie-in points (where visible and accessible).
- Septic holding tank location, access lid, and associated infrastructure, as constructed. (Capacity: 2,000 US gallon per RFP ss3.4. Confirm with Owner whether relocation of existing tank is in scope for as-built documentation.)
- Utility tie-in locations: natural gas, electrical service, communications.
- Expansion area extent relative to existing maintenance shop building.
- Existing building location (for reference and as-built coordination).

Source: RFP ss3.3.2, ss3.4 (constructed components list); _CONTEXT.md; Appendix C site aerial (R-07).
Note: The component list shall be formally confirmed with the Owner at project execution and the confirmation documented. Items above are inferred from the design requirements. ASSUMPTION: all major site components listed in ss3.4 shall be documented. The enforceable obligation requires that any additions or modifications to this list resulting from Owner confirmation be recorded and reflected in the as-built survey scope. [Lensing: C-001, B-004]

### REQ-003 — Construction Verification
**The as-built survey shall verify that constructed conditions conform to IFC design drawings.**
Source: _CONTEXT.md ("Construction verification"); RFP ss3.3.2.
Note: Specific tolerance for conformance is TBD — no tolerance stated in the RFP. ASSUMPTION: conformance verification is a qualitative check unless the Owner specifies numerical tolerances. **However, a measurable acceptance threshold is required for objective compliance determination.** The Surveyor and Owner should agree on a numerical conformance tolerance (or accept a qualitative standard) before field work commences. Without a defined threshold, the verification approach (comparison against IFC drawings) cannot produce a definitive pass/fail result. See also REQ-005 (accuracy) and Procedure Step 3.4 (deviation classification). [Lensing: A-003, C-002]

### REQ-004 — Positional and Elevation Control
**As-built survey data shall be referenced to the same coordinate and elevation datum established by DEL-008-03 (Construction Survey) to ensure continuity of survey control throughout the project lifecycle.**
Source: _DEPENDENCIES.md (DEL-008-03 is upstream dependency); _REFERENCES.md.
Status: ASSUMPTION — datum continuity is standard professional practice for as-built surveys; the RFP does not explicitly state this requirement.

### REQ-006 — Final Record Documentation
**The as-built survey shall result in final record documents sufficient to serve as a permanent baseline for operations, maintenance, and future modifications to the facility.**
Source: _CONTEXT.md ("Final record documentation"); _DEPENDENCIES.md ("Results provide lasting record for operations, maintenance, and future work").

**All work associated with DEL-008-04, including field survey and delivery of final as-built documentation, shall be completed on or before December 31, 2026.**
