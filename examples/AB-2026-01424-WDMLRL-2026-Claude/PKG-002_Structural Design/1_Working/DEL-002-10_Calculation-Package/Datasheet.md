## Attributes

### Scope of Calculations

This deliverable covers structural calculations for the following elements of the Addition (New North Shop), as drawn from RFP §3.4 and Appendix B:

| Calculation Area | Description | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Concrete Superstructure | Structural design of the concrete building with 35 ft (10.67 m) clear ceiling height | RFP §3.4, App B |
| Foundation System | Foundation design based on normal ground conditions (variable-price; reconciled post-geotech) | RFP §4.8.2 |
| Mezzanine Structure | Load-bearing mezzanine above parts room, utility room, and wash bay; designed for heavy items (oil totes, oil pumping equipment) | RFP §3.4, App B |
| Crane Support Structure | Structural supports for two 5-tonne overhead cranes on trolley | RFP §3.4, App B |
| Steel Plate Embedments | Embedded steel plates in concrete floor at strategic locations for tracked/packer heavy equipment | RFP §3.4, App B |
| Service Pit / Trench | Below-grade structural trench for under-equipment servicing | RFP §3.4, App B |
| Wash Bay Enclosure | Enclosed single bay structure for motor scraper-class equipment; 24 ft wide per drawing | RFP §3.1, §3.4, App B |
| Stair Structure | Stairs to mezzanine level | App B |
