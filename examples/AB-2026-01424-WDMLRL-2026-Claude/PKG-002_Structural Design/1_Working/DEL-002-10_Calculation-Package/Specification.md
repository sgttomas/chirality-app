# Specification: DEL-002-10 Structural Calculation Package

---

## Scope

### What This Deliverable Covers

DEL-002-10 is the Structural Calculation Package for the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop Addition (New North Shop), produced by the Structural Engineer of Record as part of PKG-002 — Structural Design.

The Calculation Package must address all structural subsystems of the Addition that require engineering justification, including but not limited to:

1. **Concrete superstructure** — lateral and gravity systems for the 130 ft × 100 ft, 35 ft clear-height concrete building (RFP §3.4, App B).
2. **Foundation system** — design based on normal ground conditions for pricing purposes; updated to reflect geotechnical investigation results (DEL-008-01) when available (RFP §4.8.2).
3. **Mezzanine structure** — load-bearing mezzanine above the parts room, utility room, and wash bay; must accommodate heavy stored items including oil totes and oil pumping equipment (RFP §3.4, App B).
4. **Crane support structure** — structural runway beams and supports for two 5-tonne overhead cranes on trolley (RFP §3.4, App B).
5. **Steel plate embedments** — embedded steel plates in the concrete floor at strategic locations for tracked/packer heavy equipment (RFP §3.4, App B).
6. **Service pit / trench** — below-grade concrete structural pit for under-equipment servicing (RFP §3.4, App B).
7. **Wash bay enclosure** — structural framing for the single enclosed 24 ft wide wash bay, including wall framing, roof structure for the wash bay enclosure, and any structural separation from the main building volume. Must accommodate motor scraper-class equipment clearances. (RFP §3.1, §3.4, App B). [Strengthened per lensing item X-002]
8. **Stair structure** — stair framing connecting main floor to mezzanine level, including stringer design, landing framing, connection to mezzanine and main floor structure, and guardrail/handrail post anchorage (structural). (App B). [Strengthened per lensing item X-002]

The Calculation Package also covers the variable-price foundation scope (SOW-0019) at a pricing level consistent with normal ground conditions, with reconciliation following delivery of the geotechnical report.

### What This Deliverable Excludes

- Mechanical, electrical, plumbing, civil, and architectural calculations (covered by respective discipline calculation packages).
- Construction execution — this is a design deliverable.
- Procurement of materials or equipment.
- Renovation structural calculations for the Old North Shop (addressed separately within PKG-002 design scope if required by Architectural drawings).
- Geotechnical investigation (DEL-008-01 — upstream input).

## Requirements

### REQ-001 — Completeness of Structural Subsystems
The Calculation Package shall address all structural subsystems listed in the Scope section above.
> Source: RFP §3.4, App B | SOW: SOW-0012, SOW-0019.

### REQ-002 — Concrete Superstructure
The building structure shall be designed as a concrete structure with a minimum clear ceiling height of 35 ft.
> Source: RFP §3.4, App B | SOW: SOW-0022.

### REQ-003 — Building Dimensions
Structural calculations shall be consistent with a building footprint of 130 ft × 100 ft (approximately 13,000 sq ft useable area), as shown on the conceptual floor plan (App B).
> Source: RFP §3.1, App B | SOW: SOW-0012.

### REQ-004 — Foundation — Normal Ground Conditions Basis
The foundation design shall be based on normal ground conditions for the purpose of initial cost estimation and structural design, per RFP §4.8.2. The foundation pricing and design shall be a separately identifiable (variable-price) scope element.
> Source: RFP §4.8.2 | SOW: SOW-0019.

### REQ-005 — Foundation — Geotech Reconciliation
The foundation calculations shall be updated or confirmed following delivery of the complete geotechnical investigation report (DEL-008-01). Any change in foundation type, depth, or capacity requirements arising from actual site conditions shall be documented as a design change and reflected in updated calculations. The Structural Engineer of Record shall define the threshold for what constitutes a "material difference" between assumed normal ground conditions and actual geotech findings that triggers a calculation update versus a confirmation note (e.g., bearing capacity deviation exceeding a stated percentage, change in recommended foundation type, or discovery of adverse soil/groundwater conditions). [Strengthened per lensing item F-001]
> Source: RFP §3.3.2 | SOW: SOW-0001. **TBD:** Material-difference threshold to be defined by Structural Engineer of Record.

### REQ-006 — Mezzanine Load-Bearing Design
The mezzanine structure shall be designed to be load-bearing, capable of accommodating heavy items including (at minimum) oil totes and oil pumping equipment. The specific design live load shall be established by the Structural Engineer of Record consistent with applicable code requirements and the Owner's stated operational use.
> Source: RFP §3.4, App B | SOW: SOW-0033.

### REQ-007 — Crane Support Structure
The structural runway system shall be designed to support two 5-tonne overhead cranes on trolley. Crane manufacturer's data (end reactions, wheel loads, bridge weight) shall be used in the calculations when available; if not available at calculation stage, conservative assumptions shall be documented and flagged for reconciliation.

> **ASSUMPTION:** Crane manufacturer/model not specified in available sources; design loads to be established by Structural Engineer of Record based on 5-tonne class equipment. **ASSUMPTION (re: Guidance Conflict C-002):** The RFP Appendix B "Gantry" label and "5 TON CRANE" label are treated as referring to the same equipment (two 5-tonne overhead cranes on trolley), per Decomposition D-001 resolution. If the gantry label refers to a separate gantry crane (not overhead crane on runway), the structural support requirements would differ materially. The Structural Engineer of Record shall confirm with the Owner at design kickoff. [Enriched per lensing item F-004]
> Source: RFP §3.4, App B | SOW: SOW-0067.

### REQ-008 — Steel Plate Embedments
The Calculation Package shall verify the structural adequacy of the concrete floor slab at locations of embedded steel plates, accounting for the concentrated loads of tracked and packer-type heavy equipment. Verification shall include specific limit-state checks (e.g., punching shear per CSA A23.3, bearing stress at embedment locations, and local flexure of slab at plate zones) with quantified acceptance thresholds per the applicable code. [Strengthened per lensing item C-001]
> Source: RFP §3.4, App B | SOW: SOW-0024. **TBD:** Specific equipment wheel/track loads — see Datasheet Key Design Parameters.

### REQ-009 — Service Pit / Trench
Structural calculations shall address the below-grade concrete service pit, including lateral earth pressure, hydrostatic conditions (if applicable per geotech), and any surcharge loading from equipment operating at grade adjacent to the pit. The applicability of the hydrostatic load case shall be determined by the geotech report (DEL-008-01) water table findings: if the water table is at or above the pit base elevation, hydrostatic loading shall be included as a mandatory load case; otherwise, the hydrostatic case may be excluded with documented justification. [Strengthened per lensing item X-003]
> Source: RFP §3.4, App B | SOW: SOW-0028.

### REQ-010 — Code Compliance
All structural calculations shall be performed in accordance with:
- The National Building Code of Canada (NBC), current edition as adopted in Alberta.
- Applicable CSA standards (including, but not limited to, CSA A23.3 for concrete and CSA S16 for steel, as applicable to the structural system).
- All applicable Alberta Safety Codes.

Specific code editions shall be confirmed by the Structural Engineer of Record and documented in the calculation cover sheet. Code compliance shall be demonstrated by: (a) verifying all applicable limit states are checked for each structural element, (b) confirming all required load combinations per NBC are evaluated, and (c) documenting that no element fails any applicable limit state check. [Strengthened per lensing item A-003]
> Source: RFP §3.3.2, §3.4 | SOW: SOW-0008, SOW-0009. **ASSUMPTION:** Specific code editions not stated in RFP; governed by applicable Alberta adoptions at time of design.

### REQ-011 — P.Eng. Stamp
The Structural Calculation Package shall be reviewed, signed, and stamped by a Professional Engineer (P.Eng.) licensed to practice structural engineering in the Province of Alberta.
> Source: RFP §3.3.2 | SOW: SOW-0018.

### REQ-012 — Coordination with Other Disciplines
The structural calculations shall be coordinated with the architectural floor plan (PKG-001), mechanical loads (PKG-003), electrical equipment loads (PKG-004), and plumbing system penetrations (PKG-006) as they affect structural members, openings, or embedments.
> Source: Decomposition PKG-002 scope description | SOW: SOW-0012.

### REQ-013 — Preliminary Design Support
Sufficient structural analysis shall be completed to support the Preliminary Structural Design presentation (DEL-002-01) for County approval, prior to completion of final calculations.
> Source: RFP §3.3.2 | SOW: SOW-0010b.

### REQ-014 — Foundation Variable-Price Segregation
The calculation package shall clearly segregate the foundation design scope (SOW-0019) from the superstructure scope (SOW-0012) to facilitate independent pricing and post-geotech reconciliation.
> Source: RFP §4.8.2; Decomposition DEL-002-11 | SOW: SOW-0019.

> **Note on source citation format:** All REQ source lines follow the pattern `Source: [RFP section / document reference] | SOW: [SOW-XXXX]` for consistent traceability. [Normalized per lensing item D-001]

### Minimum Content of Calculation Package

The following minimum content list is derived from standard Alberta structural engineering practice and is consistent with the completeness requirement of REQ-001 (the Calculation Package "shall address all structural subsystems listed in the Scope section"). While the specific section organization is at the Structural Engineer of Record's discretion, the substantive coverage below is normative insofar as it reflects the REQ-001 scope obligation. [Clarified per lensing item E-002 — removed ASSUMPTION label that conflicted with REQ-001 normative force]

The Calculation Package shall include (at minimum):

1. **Cover Sheet** — Project identification, deliverable ID (DEL-002-10), codes of practice, engineer of record, date, revision history, and P.Eng. stamp.
2. **Design Basis / Assumptions** — Site location, climatic loads (snow, wind, seismic), material specifications, design standards, geotechnical assumptions (normal ground conditions), mezzanine design load.
3. **Foundation Calculations** — Foundation type, bearing capacity, settlement, reinforcement design (variable-price scope segregated).
4. **Concrete Superstructure Calculations** — Gravity load path, lateral load resisting system, column, beam, slab/roof design.
5. **Mezzanine Calculations** — Framing design, beam/column sizing, connection details.
6. **Crane Runway Beam Calculations** — Runway beam design, bracket connections, column loading.
7. **Service Pit Calculations** — Lateral pressure, hydrostatic, slab and wall design.
8. **Steel Plate Embedment Calculations** — Floor slab local check at embedment zones.
9. **Wash Bay / Stair Structural Calculations** — As applicable.
10. **Calculation Index / Table of Contents** — Listing all calculation sections with page references.
