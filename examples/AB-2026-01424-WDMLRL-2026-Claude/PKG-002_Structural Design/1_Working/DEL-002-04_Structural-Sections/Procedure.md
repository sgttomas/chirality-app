# Procedure — DEL-002-04 Structural Sections & Details

---

## Purpose

This procedure describes the steps required to produce the Structural Sections & Details drawing set (DEL-002-04) as a component of the final IFC structural drawing package for the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop Addition. The procedure covers production from design development inputs through IFC issue and records.

## Steps

### Phase 1 — Design Development (Pre-Detail)

#### Step 1 — Confirm Section Cut Schedule with Framing Plans Team
- Coordinate with the drafter/engineer producing DEL-002-03 (Structural Framing Plans) to establish section cut lines.
- Document agreed section cut designations (e.g., Section A-A, B-B) and their locations on framing plans.
- Identify any detail call-outs on framing plans that reference this deliverable.
- **Output:** Agreed section cut schedule; notation on framing plan drawings.

**Hold-Point 1 (lensing item A-004):** Before proceeding to Phase 2 detail drawing, confirm that the agreed section cut schedule from Step 1 is documented and signed off by both the DEL-002-03 drafter/engineer and the DEL-002-04 Structural Engineer. An unconfirmed schedule could cause rework if section cuts are relocated or renumbered after detail drawing has commenced. **Record:** Signed section cut schedule agreement (add to Phase 1 records).

**Hold-Point 2 (lensing item F-001):** Before proceeding to Phase 2 detail drawing, confirm that County approval of DEL-002-01 (Preliminary Structural Design) is documented and on file. This is a mandatory prerequisite per R-012. **Verification action:** Structural Engineer or Project Manager confirms County approval documentation is in project records before Phase 2 commences. **Record:** County approval confirmation (date and document reference).

#### Step 2 — Confirm Drawing Sheet Organization
- Determine which structural details will be included in this deliverable (DEL-002-04) versus in dedicated detail deliverables (DEL-002-05 through DEL-002-09).
- Prepare a preliminary sheet list for DEL-002-04.
- **Output:** Sheet list / drawing index for DEL-002-04 (draft).

#### Step 3 — Resolve Open Design Questions (Owner Coordination)
Before detail drawing can be finalized, the following questions must be resolved (see also Conflict Table in Guidance.md):

| Question | Action | Responsible |
|---|---|---|
| Service pit design depth | Confirm with County operations team; validate against geotech report | Structural Engineer + Owner |
| Mezzanine design live load | Propose numeric value; obtain County confirmation | Structural Engineer + Owner |
| Steel plate embedment dimensions (thickness, plan size, anchoring) | Confirm County's equipment types; establish plate specs | Structural Engineer + Owner |
| Concrete construction method (cast-in-place vs. tilt-up vs. precast) | Confirm with General Contractor; document selection | Design-Builder + Structural Engineer |
| Crane runway beam size and rail type | Obtain from confirmed crane supplier | Structural Engineer + Crane Supplier |

#### Step 4 — Confirm Structural System and Material Specifications
- Confirm concrete structure system type (per Step 3 outcome).
- Establish concrete design strengths, reinforcing grade, and structural steel grades for use in details.
- Cross-reference against DEL-002-12 (Structural Specification) to ensure material designations are consistent.
- **Output:** Material specification matrix (for drawing notes and calculation coordination).

### Phase 2 — Detail Drawing Production

#### Step 5 — Produce Building Cross-Sections
- Draw transverse building section(s) cutting through main shop repair bays.
- Show: overall building height, clear height of 35 ft, wall system, roof structure, floor slab, repair bay width (24 ft each).
- Annotate all critical dimensions; reference structural member designations from DEL-002-03.
- **Output:** Building cross-section sheet(s).

#### Step 6 — Produce Building Longitudinal Section
- Draw longitudinal section showing crane runway elevation, mezzanine level, wash bay roof, and service pit location.
- Show: crane runway beam elevation, mezzanine floor level, relative floor levels if any variation.
- **Output:** Longitudinal section sheet.

#### Step 7 — Produce Service Pit / Trench Section and Details
- Draw section through service pit showing: pit depth (from Step 3), concrete wall thickness, floor slab, waterproofing interface, ventilation/lighting penetration sleeves, drain connection point.
- Include enlarged details for pit wall-to-floor-slab junction, top-of-pit edge condition, and pit end wall.
- Coordinate penetration locations with PKG-003 (mechanical), PKG-004 (electrical), PKG-006 (plumbing).
- **Output:** Service pit section and detail sheet(s). Cross-reference to DEL-002-06 if a dedicated deliverable is issued.

#### Step 8 — Produce Mezzanine Framing Section and Connection Details
- Draw section through mezzanine showing floor system, connection to supporting walls/columns, edge conditions, and stair opening.
- Annotate design live load on drawings (per Step 3 resolution).
- Include connection details for mezzanine beam/girder-to-column or beam-to-wall connections.
- **Output:** Mezzanine section and detail sheet(s). Cross-reference to DEL-002-05 if a dedicated deliverable is issued.

#### Step 9 — Produce Wash Bay Structural Section and Details
- Draw section through wash bay showing: 24 ft overhead door opening, wall structure, roof/ceiling interface, steel plate floor embedment, and wash bay sump drain penetration.
- Coordinate overhead door header structural requirements.
- **Output:** Wash bay section and detail sheet(s).

#### Step 10 — Produce 5-Tonne Overhead Crane Runway / Support Structure Details
- Draw crane runway beam section, bracket or column-cap connection detail, and rail fastening detail for the two 5-Tonne Overhead Cranes.
- Input: crane supplier's runway beam size and load data (from Step 3 / procurement). See Guidance T-04 for fallback strategy if crane supplier data is unavailable.
- Include lateral brace details if required by span and loading.
- **Output:** Crane support detail sheet(s). Cross-reference to DEL-002-07 if a dedicated deliverable is issued.

#### Step 11 — Produce Steel Plate Embedment Details
- Draw steel plate plan view, section, and anchor detail showing: plate dimensions, thickness, anchor type and pattern, edge distances, and installation tolerance.
- Note plate material grade and surface finish requirement (e.g., abrasion-resistant plate if applicable — TBD).
- Cross-reference plate locations to DEL-002-08 (Embedment Plan).
- **Output:** Steel plate embedment detail sheet(s). Cross-reference to DEL-002-08.

#### Step 12 — Produce Stair Details
- Draw stair structural framing plan, section, and connection details for mezzanine access stair.
- Coordinate with architectural stair design (PKG-001) for tread/riser configuration.
- **Output:** Stair detail sheet(s). Cross-reference to DEL-002-09 if a dedicated deliverable is issued.

#### Step 13 — Produce Miscellaneous Connection and Typical Details
- Identify any typical or recurring connection details not covered in Steps 5-12 (e.g., typical wall-to-slab joint, typical overhead door header, typical post base plate).
- Draw details as required.
- **Output:** Miscellaneous detail sheet(s) as needed.

#### Step 13A — Cross-Reference Reconciliation (lensing item X-002)
- Review all cross-reference callouts between DEL-002-04 sheets and companion deliverables (DEL-002-02, DEL-002-05 through DEL-002-09).
- Confirm all cross-reference callouts are **bidirectional**: every reference from this deliverable to a companion deliverable has a corresponding back-reference in the companion deliverable.
- Confirm all section cut designations referenced on DEL-002-03 (Structural Framing Plans) have corresponding section sheets in this deliverable or an explicit cross-reference to the relevant companion deliverable.
- Resolve any orphaned references (callouts pointing to non-existent sheets) or missing back-references.
- **Output:** Cross-reference reconciliation checklist (completed); any discrepancies corrected.

### Phase 3 — Quality Review and IFC Issue

#### Step 14 — Internal Quality Check (Structural Engineer)

**Internal QC Checklist — Minimum Items (lensing item A-005):**

| # | Check Item | Source Cross-Ref | Pass/Fail |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | All section cut designations on this set match section cut lines on DEL-002-03 (Framing Plans) | Step 1 schedule; R-015 | ___ |
| 2 | All member sizes, concrete strengths, and bar sizes shown on detail drawings match DEL-002-10 (Calculation Package) | P-01 | ___ |
| 3 | All material designations are consistent with DEL-002-12 (Structural Specification) | Step 4 | ___ |
| 4 | All required detail elements are present: dimensions, reference dimensions, section marks, detail references, notes, scale bars | Drawing standard | ___ |
| 5 | Design live load is annotated on mezzanine details | R-006; P-06 | ___ |
| 6 | Concrete construction method is correctly reflected in all detail types | R-013; P-03 | ___ |
| 7 | Cross-references between this deliverable and companion deliverables are bidirectional and correct | Step 13A | ___ |
| 8 | Top-of-foundation elevation and interface details cross-referenced to DEL-002-02 | R-015; C-05 | ___ |
| 9 | Revision block present on each sheet; revision numbering consistent | R-014 | ___ |
| 10 | Code edition identified on drawing general notes sheet | R-010 | ___ |
| 11 | Canonical terminology used: "5-Tonne Overhead Crane" (not "Gantry") | C-04; B-004 | ___ |
| 12 | All TBD items from Step 3 either resolved or explicitly marked on drawings | Step 3 resolution log | ___ |

- Complete each check item above. Document pass/fail and any redline corrections required.
- Incorporate all redline corrections before proceeding to Step 15.
- **Output:** Internal QC checklist (completed per table above); redline corrections incorporated.

#### Step 15 — Multi-Discipline Coordination Review
- Distribute draft drawing set to Architectural (PKG-001), Mechanical (PKG-003), Electrical (PKG-004), and Plumbing (PKG-006) disciplines for penetration and interface coordination review.

**Scope of review per discipline (lensing item X-005):**

| Discipline | Specific Interfaces to Review | Expected Response |
|---|---|---|
| Architectural (PKG-001) | Wall section interfaces, overhead door header clearance, mezzanine guard/railing attachment, interior partition connections to structural walls | Confirm no conflicts between architectural sections and structural sections; identify any required structural provisions for architectural elements |
| Mechanical (PKG-003) | Duct penetrations through structural walls and roof, HVAC equipment support on mezzanine or roof, service pit ventilation penetration locations | Confirm all mechanical penetrations are shown with corresponding structural sleeves; identify any new penetrations |
| Electrical (PKG-004) | Conduit sleeves through structural walls, lighting penetrations in service pit, electrical panel mounting on structural walls, crane power supply routing | Confirm all electrical penetrations are shown with corresponding structural sleeves |
| Plumbing (PKG-006) | Floor drains in service pit and wash bay, sump penetrations, water supply penetrations through structural walls, sanitary connections | Confirm all plumbing penetrations are shown with corresponding structural sleeves; confirm floor drain locations in service pit match structural slab openings |

- Resolve any conflicts (structural member at proposed duct/pipe route, missing sleeve, etc.).
- Document and incorporate revisions. Each discipline's response should be recorded in the coordination review record.
- **Output:** Coordination review record (with discipline-specific responses documented); revised drawings.

#### Step 16 — P.Eng. Review and Stamp
- Reviewing P.Eng. (licensed in Alberta) performs design review of all detail drawings against the calculation package.
- P.Eng. signs and stamps all sheets in the DEL-002-04 set.
- **Output:** Stamped IFC drawing set (all sheets).

#### Step 17 — Issue for Construction
- Assign IFC revision designation (e.g., Rev. 0 IFC) to all sheets.
- Issue drawing set to General Contractor for construction.
- File copy transmitted to Owner (County) as part of project records.

**Drawing Transmittal Record — Minimum Content (lensing item E-003):**
The transmittal record shall include at minimum:
- Complete sheet list (sheet number, sheet title, revision status for each sheet)
- Date of transmittal
- Recipient name(s) and organization(s)
- Method of transmission (electronic, hard copy, or both)
- Transmittal number (for tracking)
- Sender name and contact information
- Any special notes or conditions of issue (e.g., "Issued for Construction — P.Eng. stamped originals")

- **Output:** Issued DEL-002-04 drawing set; transmittal record (per minimum content above).
