**Note (D-001):** The conceptual floor plan (Appendix B) labels these cranes as "Gantry." Per Decision D-001 in the project decomposition, the "Gantry" labels on the drawings refer to the same equipment as the two 5-tonne overhead cranes on trolley defined in SOW-0067. This deliverable covers the trolley-based overhead cranes. (Source: Decomposition Decision Log, D-001.)
