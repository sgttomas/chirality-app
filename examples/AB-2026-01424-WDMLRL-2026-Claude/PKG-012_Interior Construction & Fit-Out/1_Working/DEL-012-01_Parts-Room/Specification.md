## 1. Scope

### 1.1 What This Specification Covers

This specification governs the construction and fit-out of the Parts Storage Room (DEL-012-01) within the new West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill Maintenance Shop Addition. It covers:

- Interior construction of the parts storage room enclosure (partitions, door opening, roll-up door)
- Floor, wall, and ceiling finishes within the room
- Installation of shelving and storage infrastructure
- Coordination interfaces with structural, mechanical, and electrical scopes

### 1.2 Scope Exclusions

The following items are explicitly outside this deliverable's scope:

| Item | Governing Deliverable / Package |
|---|---|
| Mezzanine structure above the parts room | DEL-011-07 / PKG-011 |
| HVAC ductwork, heating unit, makeup air unit installation | PKG-013 |
| Electrical rough-in, lighting circuit wiring, fixture mounting (luminaires) | PKG-015 (SOW-0056, SOW-0060) |
| Plumbing rough-in | PKG-014 |
| Architectural design drawings and finish schedules | PKG-001 |
| Structural design | PKG-002 |
| Foundation | PKG-010 |
| Building shell (walls, roof, slab) | PKG-011 |

Source: Decomposition PKG-012 exclusions; _DEPENDENCIES.md.
