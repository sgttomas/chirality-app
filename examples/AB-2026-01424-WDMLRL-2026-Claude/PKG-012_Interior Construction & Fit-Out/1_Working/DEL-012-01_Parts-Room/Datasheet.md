## 4. Construction

### 4.1 Scope Summary (from SOW-0029)

Construct a parts storage room of approximately 400 sq ft within the new North Shop addition. (Note: "approximately 400 sq ft" is the standardized expression used across all documents per lensing item B-003.) The room shall be a secure area, accessed via a 6-foot wide roll-up door. Interior fit-out includes shelving infrastructure as shown on the conceptual floor plan. Lighting is provided by 6× 8' LED fixtures. The room is located on the ground floor adjacent to the Utility Room (DEL-012-02), with a load-bearing mezzanine above it.

Source: R-01 §3.4; App B (Shop) floor plan; Decomposition SOW-0029.

### 4.2 Scope Exclusions (PKG-012 boundary)

| Excluded Item | Responsible Package |
|---|---|
| Mezzanine structure and stairs above parts room | PKG-011 — Building Structure & Envelope (DEL-011-07) |
| HVAC system installation | PKG-013 — Mechanical & HVAC Installation |
| Electrical service, lighting supply circuits, and receptacle rough-in | PKG-015 — Electrical & Low-Voltage Installation |
| Plumbing rough-in (if any) | PKG-014 — Plumbing & Water Systems Installation |

Source: Decomposition PKG-012 exclusions table.

### 4.3 Downstream Use

The completed parts storage room serves as the operational parts inventory and storage area for the landfill maintenance shop. It provides secure storage for parts, tools, and consumables accessible to maintenance staff. The mezzanine above provides additional heavy-item storage (oil totes, pumping equipment).

Source: Decomposition PKG-012 scope description; R-01 §3.1; OBJ-001.
