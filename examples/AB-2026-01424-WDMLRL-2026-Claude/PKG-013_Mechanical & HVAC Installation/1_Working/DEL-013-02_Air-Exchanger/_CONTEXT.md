## Description
Installation of high-volume air exchanger for heat recovery ventilation and air quality management. Exchanges stale facility air with fresh outside air while recovering thermal energy.

## Scope
- Equipment procurement and delivery
- Equipment installation in utility space
- Fresh air intake ductwork installation
- Exhaust outlet ductwork installation
- Integration with heating system
- Controls and sensor setup
- Performance testing and commissioning

## Key Requirements
- High-volume air exchange capability
- Heat recovery efficiency
- Fresh air distribution to all facility areas
- Stale air removal and exhaust
- Code-compliant installation
