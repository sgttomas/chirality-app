## Description
Installation of heavy equipment exhaust system providing dedicated exhaust for large machinery and production equipment. System captures and safely removes hazardous fumes and particulates generated by equipment operation.

## Scope
- Equipment exhaust collection points at machinery locations
- Ductwork installation from exhaust sources
- Filtration and treatment systems installation
- Exhaust fan installation
- Exhaust outlet and stack installation
- Controls and monitoring
- Performance testing and commissioning

## Key Requirements
- Safe capture of equipment exhaust
- Proper fume and particulate handling
- Code-compliant installation
- Environmental and safety compliance
- Proper outlet location and height
