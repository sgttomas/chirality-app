## Terminology Note

**Standardized term for exhaust collection devices:** The production documents use "exhaust hose drops" as the baseline term for the flexible exhaust collection devices installed at equipment positions. Related terms appearing in trade-off or design option discussions include "retractable hose reel assemblies" (a specific product type) and "fixed pendant drops" (a specific configuration). The term "collection hoods" refers to the capture device at the hose termination point, not the hose itself. All documents should use "exhaust hose drops" for the general system element unless referring to a specific product variant.

Source: _SEMANTIC_LENSING.md B-004; terminology normalization applied across documents.
