## Scope

### In Scope

This specification governs the construction work covered by SOW-0023: site preparation, excavation, and foundation system installation for the New North Shop addition at the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill, SW 14–44–21–W4, Camrose County, Alberta.

Specifically in scope:
- All site preparation activities within the building footprint required before foundation construction (topsoil stripping, if not already performed by County)
- Excavation to depths specified by the Foundation Design Package (DEL-002-11) and geotechnical recommendations
- Foundation system installation including formwork, reinforcement, concrete placement, curing, and backfill
- Embedded items called out in the foundation design (e.g., anchor bolts, conduit sleeves — TBD per structural drawings)
- Coordination with County representative for all required inspections
- Quality documentation: records, inspection reports, as-built data

Source: _CONTEXT.md; Decomposition §7 PKG-010; RFP §3.3, §4.8.2

### REQ-010-01-004 — Structural Load Capacity
The foundation system shall be designed and constructed to support the following minimum loads:
- Concrete structure with 35' ceiling height (Source: RFP §3.4, App B)
- Two (2) 5-tonne overhead cranes on trolley (Source: RFP §3.4)
- Tracked and packer-type heavy equipment floor loads at steel plate embedment locations (Source: RFP §3.4)
- Service pit structural requirements (canonical term; alias: "service trench" per App B — scope boundary TBD per Guidance.md CF-010-01) (Source: RFP §3.4, App B)
- All dead, live, snow, wind, and seismic loads per applicable Alberta codes

- As-built dimensional tolerances: TBD — per structural drawings
