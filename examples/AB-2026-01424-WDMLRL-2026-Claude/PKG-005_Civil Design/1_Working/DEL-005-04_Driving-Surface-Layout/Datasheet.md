> **NOTE:** Pad dimensions shown on Appendix B (Shop) are conceptual only and should not be treated as design targets. The approximate gravel pad dimensions (~60' deep x 130' wide) are conceptual sketch dimensions from App B, not confirmed design values. Final dimensions and layout to be confirmed by the Civil Engineer with Owner/Architect input during design. Exact pad areas and extents require field survey and design development. The transition from conceptual to confirmed dimensions requires documented Owner sign-off (see Guidance, Principle 4).
