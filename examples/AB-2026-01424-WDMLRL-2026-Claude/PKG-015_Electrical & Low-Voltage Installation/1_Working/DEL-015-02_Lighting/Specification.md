## Scope

### In Scope

This deliverable covers the complete supply and installation of all lighting fixtures, associated branch circuit wiring, switching/control devices, and supporting hardware for the New North Shop building at the West Dried Meat Lake Regional Landfill, as specified in SOW-0052 through SOW-0056 (Appendix B-Electrical; Decomposition §7 DEL-015-02).

Zones covered:
- **Z-1:** Main Shop — 20 x LED high-bay fixtures (5 rows of 4)
- **Z-2:** Wash Bay — 6 x LED high-bay fixtures (2 rows of 3)
- **Z-3:** Office — 1 x 8-foot LED linear fixture
- **Z-4:** Utility Room — 2 x 8-foot LED linear fixtures
- **Z-5:** Parts / Tool Room — 6 x 8-foot LED linear fixtures

### Out of Scope

- Three-phase power service and main distribution panel (DEL-015-01)
- Receptacle installation (DEL-015-03)
- Equipment power circuits, crane power (DEL-015-04)
- Low-voltage wiring, security cameras, antenna (DEL-015-05)
- Ceiling fans (not part of DEL-015-02; separate scope item per App B-Elec — see CONF-L-03 in Guidance §Conflict Table for coordination interface and ownership determination)
- Old North Shop lighting — **explicitly excluded** unless formally added via owner-approved change order referencing issued construction drawings. *(Enrichment: X-001; source: App B-Elec scope is limited to New North Shop; _CONTEXT.md does not reference Old North Shop. The previous conditional language ("unless explicitly included in issued construction drawings — TBD") created scope ambiguity. This clarification records the default position; any inclusion requires a formal scope change.)* PROPOSAL: Owner to confirm scope exclusion.
- Emergency / exit lighting: scope inclusion is **unresolved** — see REQ-L-15 below and CONF-L-02 in Guidance §Conflict Table. ASSUMPTION: emergency lighting is required under Alberta Safety Codes for occupied commercial/industrial buildings; the question is whether it falls within this deliverable or is a separate scope item. PROPOSAL: Owner and AHJ to confirm during permit application. *(Enrichment: A-001, C-003; sources: RFP §3.3.2; Alberta Safety Codes Act; App B-Elec shows no emergency lighting)*

*Sources: _CONTEXT.md; Decomposition §7; App B-Elec*
