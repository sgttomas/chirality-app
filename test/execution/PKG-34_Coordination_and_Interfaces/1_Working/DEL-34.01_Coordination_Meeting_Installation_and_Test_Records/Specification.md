# Specification: DEL-34.01 Coordination Meeting Installation & Test Records

## Scope

This specification defines the requirements for **Coordination Meeting Installation & Test Records** within **PKG-34 Coordination & Interfaces**.

**Purpose:** Provides evidence of completion, inspection, and testing for weekly Terminal coordination meetings (per Decomposition line 749, `_CONTEXT.md`).

**Objective alignment:** Supports OBJ-5 "Terminal Continuity" — The Works minimize disturbance to existing Terminal commercial activities (Decomposition line 63, 784).

**Anticipated deliverable artifacts:**
- Weekly Terminal coordination meeting minutes

**Package scope context:** Terminal operations interface, adjacent project coordination, utility coordination, marine coordination (Decomposition line 743).

**Inclusions:**
- Meeting minutes preparation and distribution
- Attendance tracking
- Action item management
- Issue tracking and escalation
- Archival and retrieval

**Exclusions:**
- Detailed design coordination meetings (covered by discipline-specific packages)
- Marine-specific coordination records (see DEL-34.03)
- Security compliance meetings (see DEL-34.04)

## Requirements

### Functional Requirements

**FR-1: Meeting Cadence**
- Weekly Terminal coordination meetings shall be conducted throughout the construction phase
- **Source:** Decomposition line 749 ("Weekly Terminal coordination meeting minutes"); **ASSUMPTION**: Weekly frequency per industry practice for active terminal interface projects
- **TBD** — Confirm meeting schedule and duration per project execution plan

**FR-2: Meeting Attendees**
- Meetings shall include representation from:
  - D&B Contractor (project management, construction management)
  - DP World Fraser Surrey Inc. (Employer's representative)
  - Terminal Operations (operations manager or delegate)
  - Key subcontractors (as required by agenda)
  - Regulatory authorities (as required)
  - Utility representatives (as applicable)
- **Source:** PKG-34 scope (Decomposition line 743 — "Terminal operations interface, adjacent project coordination, utility coordination"); **ASSUMPTION**: Attendee list typical for terminal interface coordination
- **TBD** — Specific attendee roles and alternates to be confirmed per project organization chart

**FR-3: Meeting Content**
- Minutes shall document:
  - Meeting identification (number, date, time, location)
  - Attendee roster (with affiliations)
  - Agenda items
  - Discussion summary (key points and decisions)
  - Action items (description, responsible party, due date, status)
  - Open issues register
  - Upcoming activities and constraints
  - Next meeting details
- **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — Typical meeting minutes content per ISO 9001 quality documentation practices
- **TBD** — Employer's specific requirements for minutes content and format

**FR-4: Coordination Topics**
- Meetings shall address coordination topics including:
  - Terminal operations interface (work windows, access restrictions)
  - Construction phasing and sequencing to minimize operational disruption
  - Marine access and coordination (vessel schedules, loading arm outages)
  - Utility outages and shutdowns
  - Traffic management and access control
  - Health, safety, and environmental compliance
  - Permit and regulatory compliance updates
  - Interface with adjacent projects or tenants
- **Source:** OBJ-5 Terminal Continuity objective (Decomposition line 63); PKG-34 scope (line 743)
- **TBD** — Prioritization of coordination topics per project risk register

**FR-5: Action Item Tracking**
- All action items generated during meetings shall be tracked until closure
- Action items shall include: description, responsible party, due date, status
- Overdue action items shall be escalated per project procedures

- **Completeness check:** Verify all required sections per FR-3 are populated
