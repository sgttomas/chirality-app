# Specification: DEL-01.02 Demolition Technical Specification

## Scope

**Inclusions:**

This deliverable defines performance, materials, and workmanship requirements for demolition and removals in PKG-01, including:

- Technical requirements for demolition of Track 6 (rail infrastructure), Dolphin 2 (marine structure), perimeter fencing, and salt tent dismantling. Source: decomposition PKG-01 scope and _CONTEXT.md.
- Disposal requirements for all demolition waste including segregation, handling, documentation, and final disposition. Source: _CONTEXT.md anticipated artifacts.
- Performance criteria, execution standards, quality control requirements, and completion criteria for demolition operations. Source: _CONTEXT.md description (performance, materials, workmanship requirements).
- Interface coordination requirements with Employer-responsible items. Source: decomposition Section 1.2 Scope Focus.
- Project-specific requirements derived from Employer's Requirements (ER). Source: decomposition DEL-01.02 description; ER volumes TBD.

**Exclusions:**

- Spatial arrangement and limits of demolition (covered in DEL-01.01 Demolition Design Drawing Set).
- Detailed execution procedures and method statements (covered in DEL-01.03 Demolition Procedures).
- Inspection records and completion documentation (covered in DEL-01.04 Demolition Installation & Test Records).
- Employer-responsible items except interface coordination requirements. Source: decomposition Section 1.2 Scope Focus.
