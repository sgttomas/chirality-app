# Specification: DEL-01.03 Demolition Procedures

## Scope

**Inclusions:**

This deliverable defines demolition execution methods and controls for PKG-01 demolition works, including:

- Execution procedures and method statements implementing DEL-01.02 specification requirements for Track 6, Dolphin 2, fencing, and salt tent demolition. Source: _CONTEXT.md description and decomposition PKG-01 scope.
- Safety controls and hazard mitigation measures for all identified demolition hazards. Source: _CONTEXT.md (safety requirements).
- Quality controls, inspection hold points, and verification methods supporting DEL-01.04 inspection and completion verification. Source: _CONTEXT.md (quality requirements).
- Operational coordination procedures ensuring terminal operations continuity during demolition. Source: _CONTEXT.md (operational requirements) and decomposition Section 1.1 (terminal location).
- Structure-specific procedures for Track 6 removal and Dolphin 2 removal as prioritized in anticipated artifacts. Source: _CONTEXT.md anticipated artifacts.

**Exclusions:**

- Design requirements and acceptance criteria (covered in DEL-01.02 Demolition Technical Specification).
- Spatial limits, phasing, and disposal routing (covered in DEL-01.01 Demolition Design Drawing Set).
- Inspection records and completion documentation (covered in DEL-01.04 Demolition Installation & Test Records).
- Employer-responsible items except coordination procedures. Source: decomposition Section 1.2 Scope Focus.
