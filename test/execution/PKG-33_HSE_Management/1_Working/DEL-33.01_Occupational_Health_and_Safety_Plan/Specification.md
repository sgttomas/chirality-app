# Specification: DEL-33.01 Occupational Health & Safety Plan

## Scope

This specification defines the requirements for the **Occupational Health & Safety Plan** within **PKG-33 HSE Management**.

### Purpose

The OHS Plan shall define the planned approach and controls for occupational health & safety to meet Employer's Requirements during the Design & Build execution phase of the Canola Oil Transload Facility.

**Source:** `_CONTEXT.md`, Decomposition line 729

### Scope Boundaries

**Inclusions:**
- All construction activities within the Contractor's scope (36 packages)
- HSE management system framework and procedures
- Hazard identification and risk assessment processes
- Safe work procedures and method statements
- Emergency preparedness and response
- Training and competency management
- Incident management and investigation
- HSE monitoring, inspection, and audit programs
- Interfaces with environmental management (CEMP, SPPP, Waste Management)

**Source:** Decomposition PKG-33 scope (line 723), Package Summary (Section 4); Datasheet Plan Structure

**Exclusions:**
- Employer-responsible HSE activities (except interface coordination)
- Post-construction operational HSE management (Employer responsibility)
- Off-site manufacturing and fabrication HSE (subcontractor managed, verification only)

**Source:** Decomposition Scope Focus (Section 1.2)

### Anticipated Deliverable Artifacts

- Project OHS Plan (written document)
- Supporting annexes (organizational charts, contact lists, forms/templates)

**Source:** `_CONTEXT.md`, Decomposition line 729; Datasheet Plan Structure

#### FR-4: Hazard Identification and Risk Assessment

The OHS Plan shall establish processes for:
- Systematic hazard identification (task-based, area-based, change-driven)
- Risk assessment methodology (likelihood × consequence)
- Control hierarchy (elimination, substitution, engineering, administrative, PPE)
- Residual risk acceptance criteria

**Source:** Cross-reference: DEL-33.02 Risk Assessments; ASSUMPTION: Standard risk management framework; Datasheet Key Plan Elements (Hazard Identification, Risk Assessment)
**Verification:** Risk register review, hazard coverage audit (Specification Section 5); Procedure Step 2.2, Procedure Step 4.3
**Guidance Cross-Reference:** Guidance Principle 3 (Risk-Based Resource Allocation), Guidance Project-Specific Hazard Considerations (all subsections), Guidance Example 3 (Risk Assessment Trigger Criteria)

Anticipated project-specific hazards to be addressed:
- Marine operations (work on/near water, vessel operations)
- Rail works (working on active railway)
- Heavy lifting (crane operations, tank erection)
- Confined space (tank entry, underground utilities)
- Hazardous substances (canola oil, coatings, chemicals)
- Electrical work (high voltage installations)
- Terminal operations interface (work adjacent to active terminal)
- Excavation and groundworks (trenching, earthworks)

**Source:** Decomposition package scope (PKG-05 through PKG-26); OBJ-5 Terminal Continuity; Datasheet Project-Specific Hazards
