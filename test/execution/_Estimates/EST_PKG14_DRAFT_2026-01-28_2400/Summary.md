1. Total process piping quantity of ~1,700 linear meters based on typical terminal layout
2. Carbon steel construction throughout (no exotic alloys for canola oil service)
3. Shop prefabrication with field assembly and welding
4. ASME B31.3 process piping code compliance
5. 100% RT on headers; spot RT on smaller lines
6. Insulation and heat tracing on all product lines for cold weather protection
7. 32 railcar unloading stations on 2 tracks feeding collection headers
