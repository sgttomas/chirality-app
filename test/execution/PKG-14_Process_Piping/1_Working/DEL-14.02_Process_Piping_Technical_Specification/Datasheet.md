## Conditions

**Applicability / Context:**

This technical specification applies to all process piping within PKG-14 Process Piping scope (source: Decomposition PKG-14; scope: Specification.md Scope; rationale: Guidance.md Purpose):
- **Piping systems:** Headers, export lines, recycle lines, nitrogen distribution (scope: Specification.md Scope; rationale: Guidance.md Principles item 1; referenced in DEL-14.01)
- **Product:** CSD (Crude Super Degummed) canola oil (source: Decomposition Section 1.1; scope: Specification.md Product context; requirements: Specification.md FR-1; rationale: Guidance.md Principles item 1)
