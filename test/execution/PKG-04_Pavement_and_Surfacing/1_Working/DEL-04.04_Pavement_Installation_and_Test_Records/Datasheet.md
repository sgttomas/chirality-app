## Attributes

### Record Scope
This deliverable provides field documentation demonstrating that pavement construction complies with DEL-04.01 (drawings), DEL-04.02 (specifications), and DEL-04.03 (calculations), including:

- **Compaction test records**: Field density testing of subgrade, base, subbase, and asphalt pavement layers
- **Pavement core sample records**: Thickness verification and material property validation through core extraction and testing
- **Surface profile/thickness verification**: Survey verification of as-built pavement grades and layer thicknesses
- **Material certification records**: Asphalt mix design approvals, concrete batch tickets, aggregate source certifications
- **Construction quality control records**: Daily inspection reports, placement records, curing records, weather documentation
- **Non-conformance reports (NCRs)**: Documentation of deviations from specifications, root cause analysis, corrective actions, approvals
- **OBJ-8 future expansion documentation**: Enhanced testing and documentation for Phase 2 expansion corridor zones
- **Final acceptance documentation**: Substantial completion checklists, punch list items, final acceptance certificates

### Record Types and Artifacts

#### Compaction Test Records
- **Test method**: Nuclear density gauge (ASTM D6938) or sand cone method (ASTM D1556)
- **Test frequency**: Per DEL-04.02 specification requirements (reference Datasheet **ASSUMPTION**: 1 per 500 m² per layer)
- **Data captured**: Test location (station/offset or coordinates), test date, layer identification (subgrade, base, binder, etc.), test depth, wet density, dry density, moisture content, maximum dry density (Standard Proctor), percent compaction, acceptance criterion, pass/fail status, inspector name
- **Cross-references**: DEL-04.01 drawing sheet and section, DEL-04.02 specification clause, DEL-04.03 calculation output
- **Format**: Standardized compaction test form (one form per test location)

#### Core Sample Records
- **Core extraction**: Core locations, core date, core depth, layer thicknesses measured
- **Laboratory testing**: Asphalt core bulk density, maximum theoretical density, percent air voids; concrete core compressive strength (if applicable)
- **Data captured**: Core location (station/offset or coordinates), core date, core identification number, layer thicknesses (wearing, binder, base), total pavement thickness, bulk density, TMD, air voids %, compressive strength (concrete), acceptance criteria, pass/fail status
- **Cross-references**: DEL-04.01 drawing section, DEL-04.02 specification clause (thickness tolerance, density requirements), DEL-04.03 calculated thickness
- **Format**: Core sample report with photographs and laboratory test certificates

#### Surface Profile/Thickness Verification Records
- **Measurement method**: Laser profilometer, stringline and level, GPS/total station survey
- **Coverage**: 100% coverage for grade verification, targeted locations for thickness verification (coordinate with core sampling)
- **Data captured**: Station/offset or coordinates, design elevation, as-built elevation, deviation from design, tolerance limit, pass/fail status
- **Cross-references**: DEL-04.01 drawing plans and sections, DEL-04.02 grade tolerance requirements
- **Format**: Survey report with profile charts, cross-section comparisons, deviation summaries

#### Material Certification Records
- **Asphalt materials**: Mix design approval letter, binder certification (PG grade), aggregate gradation test results, daily batch plant quality control reports
- **Concrete materials** (if applicable): Mix design approval, cement certification, aggregate test results, batch tickets with air content and slump
- **Base/subbase materials**: Aggregate gradation, plasticity index, compaction test results (maximum dry density, optimum moisture content)
- **Line marking materials**: Material certification (paint or thermoplastic), color and reflectivity test results
- **Data captured**: Material type, supplier, certification date, test results, acceptance criteria, approval status
- **Cross-references**: DEL-04.02 specification material requirements
- **Format**: Manufacturer certificates, laboratory test reports, approval transmittals

#### Construction Quality Control Records
- **Daily QC reports**: Daily progress (area paved, tonnage placed), weather conditions, equipment used, issues encountered, inspector observations
- **Placement records**: Asphalt paving records (mix temperature at plant, at paver, ambient temperature), concrete placement records (batch tickets, ambient temperature, curing method)
- **Curing records**: Concrete curing method (moist curing, membrane compound), curing duration, curing condition monitoring
- **Weather records**: Temperature, precipitation, wind conditions (affecting paving operations and material acceptance)
- **Data captured**: Date, work performed, locations, quantities, test results summary, issues/resolutions, inspector name
- **Format**: Daily QC report form

#### Non-Conformance Reports (NCRs)
- **NCR initiation**: Identification of deviation from DEL-04.01, DEL-04.02, DEL-04.03 requirements
- **Root cause analysis**: Investigation and determination of cause (workmanship, materials, design, weather, etc.)
- **Corrective action**: Proposed remediation (rework, overlay, acceptance with reduced payment, engineering evaluation)
- **Approval**: Engineer review and approval of corrective action
- **Verification**: Re-testing after corrective action, final acceptance
- **Data captured**: NCR number, date, location, description of non-conformance, affected drawings/specifications/calculations, root cause, proposed corrective action, approval, verification test results, closure date
- **Format**: NCR form with photographs, test results, approval signatures

### OBJ-8 Future Expansion Documentation Requirements

For pavement zones designated as Phase 2 expansion corridors per OBJ-8:
- **Enhanced testing frequency**: Double standard testing frequency (e.g., 2 per 500 m² instead of 1 per 500 m²) to provide higher confidence in as-built quality (**ASSUMPTION** – to be confirmed from Owner requirements)
- **Enhanced as-built documentation**: Survey verification of pavement grades with tighter tolerances or additional survey points
- **Material records retention**: Enhanced material certification records (mix designs, material test reports, batch tickets) retained for Phase 2 reference
- **Compaction test location plans**: Detailed plans showing all compaction test locations with survey coordinates for Phase 2 design reference
- **Photographic documentation**: Photographs of critical construction stages (subgrade preparation, layer placement, interface details) for Phase 2 reference
- **Construction joint documentation**: Detailed documentation of Phase 2 tie-in joint locations (survey coordinates, photographs, joint details) per DEL-04.01 and DEL-04.02 requirements

## Conditions

### Acceptance Criteria (From DEL-04.02 and DEL-04.03)
All test results shall be evaluated against acceptance criteria specified in DEL-04.02 and calculated in DEL-04.03:

- **Subgrade compaction**: Minimum 95% Standard Proctor density (reference DEL-04.02 **ASSUMPTION**)
- **Base/subbase compaction**: Minimum 98% Standard Proctor density (reference DEL-04.02 **ASSUMPTION**)
- **Asphalt pavement compaction**: Minimum 95% TMD for wearing/binder courses, 93% TMD for base course (reference DEL-04.02 **ASSUMPTION**)
- **Pavement thickness**: Within tolerance specified in DEL-04.02 (reference **TBD** – typical ±10 mm per layer)
- **Surface grade**: Within tolerance specified in DEL-04.02 (reference **TBD** – typical ±10 mm)
- **Concrete strength** (if applicable): Minimum 28-day compressive strength per DEL-04.02 (reference **ASSUMPTION**: 30-35 MPa)

### Testing Requirements (From DEL-04.02)
- **Compaction testing frequency**: Reference DEL-04.02 specification (Datasheet **ASSUMPTION**: 1 per 500 m² per layer; increased to 2 per 500 m² for OBJ-8 expansion corridors)
- **Core sampling frequency**: Reference DEL-04.02 specification (Datasheet **ASSUMPTION**: 1 per 2000 m²; increased for OBJ-8 expansion corridors)
- **Testing methods**: ASTM D6938 (nuclear density), ASTM D1556 (sand cone), or equivalent methods per DEL-04.02
- **Laboratory accreditation**: Testing laboratory shall be accredited to ISO 17025 or equivalent (reference DEL-04.02 **ASSUMPTION**)

### Record Retention and Handoff
- **Record retention period**: Minimum 25 years or project design life (whichever is longer) – **ASSUMPTION** to be confirmed from contract requirements
- **Record format**: Paper records with digital backup (PDF scans), or digital records with paper backup per project document management plan
- **Handoff to Owner**: Final record package delivered to Owner upon substantial completion and final acceptance
- **OBJ-8 records**: Phase 2 expansion corridor records retained and indexed separately for easy retrieval during Phase 2 design

## Construction

### Testing Procedures and Quality Control

#### Pre-Construction Activities
- **Test method approval**: Testing methods, equipment, and laboratory accreditation approved by Engineer prior to construction start
- **Inspector qualifications**: Field inspectors and testing technicians qualified and certified per applicable standards (ACI, NICET, or equivalent)
- **Calibration verification**: Testing equipment (nuclear density gauges, load cells, thermometers) calibrated per manufacturer requirements; calibration certificates current

#### During Construction Activities
- **Field testing**: Compaction testing, surface profile verification, material sampling performed per DEL-04.02 frequency requirements
- **Laboratory testing**: Core samples, material samples sent to accredited laboratory for testing
- **Real-time documentation**: Daily QC reports completed same day; test results recorded and entered into database immediately
- **Non-conformance identification**: Deviations identified immediately and NCR process initiated

#### Post-Construction Activities
- **Final acceptance inspection**: Final walkthrough with Owner/Engineer to verify substantial completion
- **Punch list completion**: Punch list items addressed and closed
- **Record compilation**: All test records, material certifications, NCRs, photographs compiled into final record package
- **Record indexing**: Records indexed by location, date, test type, drawing reference for easy retrieval

### Record Organization and Format

**Recommended organization** (**TBD** – confirm from project quality plan):
1. **Cover page and index**: Project identification, deliverable identification, record summary
2. **Section 1: Material certifications**: Asphalt, concrete, base/subbase, line marking material certificates
3. **Section 2: Mix design approvals**: Asphalt mix design, concrete mix design approval letters
4. **Section 3: Compaction test records**: Organized by layer type (subgrade, base, binder, wearing), then by location
5. **Section 4: Core sample records**: Organized by location with laboratory test reports
6. **Section 5: Survey and profile verification**: Survey reports, profile charts, deviation summaries
7. **Section 6: Daily QC reports**: Chronological daily progress and inspection reports
8. **Section 7: Non-conformance reports**: All NCRs with corrective actions and closures
9. **Section 8: OBJ-8 Phase 2 expansion documentation**: Enhanced records for expansion corridors
10. **Section 9: Final acceptance documentation**: Substantial completion checklist, acceptance certificates
11. **Appendices**: Photographs, equipment calibration certificates, inspector certifications
