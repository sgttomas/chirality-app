# Specification: DEL-04.04 Pavement Installation & Test Records

## Scope

### Included
This specification defines the requirements for producing, organizing, and delivering the Pavement Installation & Test Records (DEL-04.04), which provide field documentation demonstrating pavement construction conformance to DEL-04.01 (drawings), DEL-04.02 (specifications), and DEL-04.03 (calculations).

The records shall include:
- Compaction test records for all pavement layers
- Core sample records for thickness and material property verification
- Surface profile and grade verification records
- Material certification records
- Daily construction quality control reports
- Non-conformance reports with corrective actions
- OBJ-8 Phase 2 expansion corridor enhanced documentation
- Final acceptance documentation

### Excluded
- Design drawings (covered by DEL-04.01)
- Material and workmanship specifications (covered by DEL-04.02)
- Design calculations (covered by DEL-04.03)
- Construction means and methods documentation (contractor responsibility, not part of permanent records)

## Requirements

### R1: Record Content Requirements

#### R1.1: Compaction Test Records
**Requirement**: For each compaction test performed:
- Test location (station/offset or survey coordinates)
- Test date and time
- Layer identification (subgrade, base, subbase, asphalt binder, asphalt wearing)
- Test depth below surface
- Test method (ASTM D6938 nuclear density, ASTM D1556 sand cone, or approved equivalent)
- Test equipment identification and calibration status
- Wet density, dry density, moisture content (measured)
- Maximum dry density and optimum moisture content (Standard Proctor reference, ASTM D698)
- Percent compaction (dry density / maximum dry density × 100%)
- Acceptance criterion per DEL-04.02 (e.g., 95% minimum for subgrade, 98% for base, 95% TMD for asphalt)
- Pass/fail status
- Inspector name and signature
- Cross-references: DEL-04.01 drawing sheet and section, DEL-04.02 specification clause, DEL-04.03 calculation (if applicable)

**Source**: DEL-04.02 testing requirements; ASTM D6938, D1556, D698 test methods
**Verification**: Guidance review checklist Step 1; verify all required fields are present and legible

#### R1.2: Core Sample Records
**Requirement**: For each pavement core extracted:
- Core location (station/offset or survey coordinates)
- Core date and core identification number
- Layer thicknesses measured (wearing course, binder course, base course) in mm
- Total pavement thickness in mm
- Asphalt core laboratory test results (if performed): bulk density, TMD, percent air voids
- Concrete core laboratory test results (if applicable): 28-day compressive strength in MPa
- Target values per DEL-04.03 calculation (design thicknesses) for comparison
- Tolerance limits per DEL-04.02 specification (e.g., ±10 mm per layer typical)
- Pass/fail status for thickness and material properties
- Photographs of core (both ends, full length)
- Laboratory test certificate with accreditation statement
- Technician name and signature
- Cross-references: DEL-04.01 drawing sheet and section, DEL-04.02 specification clause, DEL-04.03 calculation sheet

**Source**: DEL-04.02 testing requirements; ASTM D2726, D2041 (asphalt core testing), ASTM C39 (concrete core testing)
**Verification**: Guidance review checklist Step 2; verify all required fields, photographs, and laboratory certificates are included

#### R1.3: Surface Profile and Grade Verification Records
**Requirement**: Survey verification of as-built pavement surface elevations:
- Survey points (station/offset or coordinates, design elevation, as-built elevation, deviation)
- Measurement method (laser profilometer, GPS/total station, stringline and level)
- Tolerance limit per DEL-04.02 specification (e.g., ±10 mm typical)
- Pass/fail status for each survey point
- Profile charts showing longitudinal and cross-sectional profiles compared to design
- Deviation summary (maximum, minimum, average, standard deviation)
- Surveyor name, company, and seal (if required)
- Cross-references: DEL-04.01 drawing plans and sections with design elevations

**Source**: DEL-04.02 grade tolerance requirements; DEL-04.01 design elevations
**Verification**: Guidance review checklist Step 3; verify survey coverage is adequate and deviations are within tolerance

#### R1.4: Material Certification Records
**Requirement**: Material certifications demonstrating conformance to DEL-04.02 specifications:

**Asphalt Materials**:
- Mix design approval letter from Engineer
- Asphalt binder certification (PG grade, supplier, batch numbers)
- Aggregate gradation test results (sieve analysis per ASTM C136)
- Aggregate quality test results (Los Angeles abrasion, soundness, fractured faces)
- Daily batch plant quality control reports (mix temperature, gradation checks, binder content)

**Concrete Materials** (if applicable):
- Mix design approval letter from Engineer
- Cement certification (type, strength class, supplier, batch numbers)
- Aggregate test results (gradation, deleterious materials)
- Concrete batch tickets (batch number, date, time, quantity, slump, air content, water-cement ratio)

**Base/Subbase Materials**:
- Aggregate gradation test results per ASTM C136
- Plasticity index test results per ASTM D4318
- Compaction test results (maximum dry density, optimum moisture content per ASTM D698)

**Line Marking Materials**:
- Material certification (paint or thermoplastic type, manufacturer, batch numbers)
- Color and reflectivity test results per applicable standards

**Source**: DEL-04.02 material specifications; manufacturer certificates; laboratory test reports
**Verification**: Guidance review checklist Step 4; verify all material certifications are current and complete

#### R1.5: Daily Construction Quality Control Reports
**Requirement**: Daily QC reports documenting construction activities:
- Date, weather conditions (temperature, precipitation, wind), construction hours
- Work performed (area paved, tonnage placed, layer placed, equipment used)
- Test summary (number of compaction tests, pass/fail count, core samples taken)
- Observations (workmanship quality, issues encountered, resolutions)
- Non-conformances identified and NCRs initiated
- Inspector name and signature

**Source**: Daily field inspection and testing activities
**Verification**: Guidance review checklist Step 5; verify daily reports are complete and consistent with test records

#### R1.6: Non-Conformance Reports (NCRs)
**Requirement**: For each non-conformance identified:
- NCR number (unique identifier), date opened, location
- Description of non-conformance (what requirement was not met)
- Affected requirements: DEL-04.01 drawing reference, DEL-04.02 specification clause, DEL-04.03 calculation (if applicable)
- Root cause analysis (workmanship, materials, design, weather, equipment, other)
- Proposed corrective action (rework, overlay, remove and replace, engineering evaluation, acceptance with reduced payment, other)
- Engineer review and approval of proposed corrective action
- Implementation of corrective action (date, description, contractor signoff)
- Verification testing after corrective action (test results demonstrating conformance)
- NCR closure (date, Engineer signoff)

**Source**: Field inspection identifying deviations; engineering evaluation; corrective action implementation
**Verification**: Guidance review checklist Step 6; verify all NCRs are closed or have approved action plans prior to final acceptance

#### R1.7: OBJ-8 Phase 2 Expansion Corridor Enhanced Documentation
**Requirement**: For pavement zones designated as Phase 2 expansion corridors per OBJ-8:
- Enhanced testing frequency per DEL-04.02 (e.g., 2x standard frequency – **ASSUMPTION** to be confirmed)
- Detailed test location plans with survey coordinates (for Phase 2 reference)
- Enhanced material certification record retention (complete set of mix designs, material test reports, batch tickets)
- Photographic documentation of critical construction stages (subgrade preparation, layer placement, construction joints at Phase 2 tie-in locations)
- Construction joint documentation per DEL-04.01 and DEL-04.02 (Phase 2 tie-in joint locations surveyed and photographed)
- All records indexed and archived separately for easy retrieval during Phase 2 design

**Source**: OBJ-8 objectives (Decomposition Section 6); DEL-04.01 expansion corridor layout; DEL-04.02 OBJ-8 provisions
**Verification**: Guidance review checklist Step 7; verify OBJ-8 enhanced documentation is complete for all expansion corridor zones

### R2: Record Traceability Requirements

#### R2.1: Drawing Cross-References
**Requirement**: Every test record shall reference the corresponding DEL-04.01 drawing:
- Drawing sheet number
- Plan or section detail showing the tested location or element
- Drawing revision number (to ensure test was performed against correct design)

**Source**: Traceability principle; configuration management
**Verification**: Traceability matrix audit per Procedure; verify every test record has valid DEL-04.01 reference

#### R2.2: Specification Cross-References
**Requirement**: Every test record shall reference the corresponding DEL-04.02 specification:
- Specification section and clause number governing the test (material requirements, testing frequency, acceptance criteria)

**Source**: Traceability principle; DEL-04.02 specification requirements
**Verification**: Traceability matrix audit per Procedure; verify every test record has valid DEL-04.02 reference

#### R2.3: Calculation Cross-References
**Requirement**: Thickness verification records shall reference the corresponding DEL-04.03 calculation:
- Calculation sheet number with design thickness output
- Target value from calculation for comparison to measured value

**Source**: Traceability principle; DEL-04.03 calculation outputs
**Verification**: Traceability matrix audit per Procedure; verify thickness records have valid DEL-04.03 reference

#### R2.4: Traceability Matrix
**Requirement**: A traceability matrix shall be maintained linking:
- Test record identifier (test date, location, type)
- DEL-04.01 drawing reference (sheet, detail)
- DEL-04.02 specification reference (section, clause)
- DEL-04.03 calculation reference (sheet, output parameter)
- Test result (pass/fail)
- NCR reference (if test failed)

**Source**: Quality management system; record organization
**Verification**: Traceability matrix audit per Procedure Step 4; verify matrix is complete and accurate

### R3: Record Quality and Completeness Requirements

#### R3.1: Legibility and Clarity
**Requirement**: All records shall be:
- Legible (handwriting or digital text is readable)
- Complete (all required fields populated, no blank fields without explanation)
- Signed/approved (inspector, technician, engineer signatures where required)
- Dated (all entries have dates)

**Source**: Quality management system; record admissibility requirements
**Verification**: Guidance review checklist Step 8; QA spot-check of records for legibility and completeness

#### R3.2: Data Accuracy and Consistency
**Requirement**: All recorded data shall be:
- Accurate (no obvious data entry errors, units are correct, calculations are correct)
- Consistent (same test location does not have conflicting data in different records)
- Traceable (original field data sheets or laboratory worksheets are retained to support final records)

**Source**: Quality management system; data integrity requirements
**Verification**: Guidance review checklist Step 9; cross-check sample of records against original field/laboratory data

#### R3.3: Record Retention and Format
**Requirement**: Records shall be:
- Retained for minimum period per contract (reference Datasheet **ASSUMPTION**: 25 years or design life, whichever is longer)
- Stored in both paper and digital format (or as required by contract)
- Indexed for easy retrieval (by location, date, test type, drawing reference)
- Protected from loss or damage (secure storage, backup copies for digital records)

**Source**: Contract requirements; Owner document management requirements (**TBD** – confirm from contract)
**Verification**: Record storage and retention plan approved by Owner/Engineer prior to substantial completion

### R4: Record Review and Approval Requirements

#### R4.1: Contractor QA/QC Review
**Requirement**: Contractor QA/QC Manager shall review all records for:
- Completeness (all required test records are included)
- Compliance (test results meet acceptance criteria or have approved NCRs)
- Traceability (all cross-references are correct)
- Legibility (records are readable and understandable)
- Sign off on final record package prior to submission to Engineer

**Source**: Contract QA/QC requirements
**Verification**: Contractor QA/QC Manager signoff on final record package cover sheet

#### R4.2: Engineer Review and Acceptance
**Requirement**: Engineer shall review final record package for:
- Completeness (all required record types are included per contract requirements)
- Compliance (construction conformed to DEL-04.01, DEL-04.02, DEL-04.03 or approved deviations are documented)
- NCR closure (all NCRs are closed with approved corrective actions)
- OBJ-8 documentation (Phase 2 expansion corridor enhanced records are complete)
- Accept record package and issue acceptance certificate

**Source**: Contract review and acceptance requirements
**Verification**: Engineer acceptance certificate included in final record package

## Standards

### Testing Standards
- **ASTM D6938**: Nuclear Density Testing (field compaction verification)
- **ASTM D1556**: Sand Cone Density Testing (alternate compaction method)
- **ASTM D698**: Standard Proctor Compaction Test (laboratory reference for field testing)
- **ASTM D2726**: Bulk Specific Gravity of Compacted Asphalt (core density)
- **ASTM D2041**: Theoretical Maximum Specific Gravity of Asphalt (TMD for air voids)
- **ASTM C39**: Compressive Strength of Concrete Cylinders (concrete core strength)
- **ASTM C136**: Sieve Analysis (aggregate gradation)
- **ASTM D4318**: Plasticity Index (base/subbase material quality)

### Quality Management Standards
- **ISO 17025**: Laboratory Accreditation (testing laboratory requirements)
- **ISO 9001**: Quality Management Systems (overall QA/QC framework – if applicable)

### Acceptance Criteria
- All required record types per R1.1–R1.7 are included
- All test results meet acceptance criteria or have approved NCRs
- Traceability matrix is complete per R2.1–R2.4
- Records are legible, complete, accurate, consistent per R3.1–R3.2
- Record retention plan approved per R3.3
- Contractor QA/QC Manager signoff obtained per R4.1
- Engineer acceptance certificate issued per R4.2
