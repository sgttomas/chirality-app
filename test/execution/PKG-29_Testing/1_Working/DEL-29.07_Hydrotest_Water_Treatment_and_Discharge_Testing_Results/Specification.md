# Specification: DEL-29.07 Hydrotest Water Treatment & Discharge Testing Results

## Scope

Requirements for **Hydrotest Water Treatment & Discharge Testing Results** within **PKG-29 Testing**.

**Purpose:** Provides evidence of completion, inspection, and testing for hydrotest water treatment & discharge testing results. **Source:** Decomposition line 652

**Type:** Record | **Responsible:** D&B Contractor (QA/QC)

### Inclusions
- Water quality test results (pre-treatment and post-treatment)
- Treatment system operation logs
- Discharge monitoring records

### Exclusions
- Water management plan (DEL-29.06)
- Disposal hauling records (DEL-29.08)

## Requirements

### Functional Requirements

**FR-1: Sampling and Testing**
- Sampling per DEL-29.06 plan (pre-treatment, post-treatment, discharge)
- Testing by accredited laboratory (ISO 17025 **ASSUMPTION**)
- Parameters per discharge permit requirements
- Chain of custody for samples

**FR-2: Treatment Logging**
- Daily treatment system operation logs
- Flow rates, chemical dosing, maintenance activities
- Treatment efficiency calculations

**FR-3: Discharge Monitoring**
- Discharge water quality verified before each discharge event
- Compliance with permit limits documented
- Non-compliances reported and corrected

### Performance Requirements

**PR-1: Water Quality Compliance**
- Discharge water quality shall meet permit limits for all parameters
- If limits exceeded, discharge shall be stopped and corrective action taken

**PR-2: Record Completeness**
- All required samples collected and tested
- All test results documented
- All treatment activities logged

### Interface Requirements

**IR-1:** Records demonstrate compliance with DEL-29.06 water management plan
**IR-2:** Records support environmental compliance reporting to authorities

### Quality Requirements

**QR-1:** Laboratory reports from accredited lab
**QR-2:** Test results reviewed by environmental specialist before discharge
**QR-3:** Records permanent quality/environmental records

## Standards

- ISO 17025: Laboratory accreditation
- BC Environmental Management Act: Discharge monitoring
- Discharge permit conditions **location TBD**

## Verification

**VM-1:** All required samples collected
**VM-2:** Test results complete
**VM-3:** Discharge complied with permit limits

### Acceptance Criteria
**AC-1:** Testing complete per plan
**AC-2:** All discharges compliant (or non-compliances resolved)
**AC-3:** Records complete and filed

## Documentation

**Required Outputs:**
- Water quality test results (laboratory reports)
- Treatment logs
- Discharge testing records
- Compiled into environmental compliance package
