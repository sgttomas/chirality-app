## Construction

**Materials / Configuration:**

This deliverable produces execution procedures for the following coating operations:

### Surface Preparation Procedures

**Scope:** Methods for surface preparation to meet SSPC standards specified in DEL-26.01

**Applications covered:**
- **Tank internals:** SSPC-SP 10 (near-white blast) or SP-5 (white metal blast) — **Source:** DEL-26.01 (Datasheet — Tank Coatings Internal)
- **Tank externals:** SSPC-SP 6 (commercial blast) or SP-10 — **Source:** DEL-26.01 (Datasheet — Tank Coatings External)
- **Structural steel:** SSPC-SP 6 or SP-10 per corrosivity category — **Source:** DEL-26.01 (Datasheet — Structural Steel Coatings)
- **Marine structures:** SSPC-SP 10 for critical marine applications — **Source:** DEL-26.01 (Datasheet — Marine Coatings)

**Preparation methods:**
- **Abrasive blast cleaning:** Primary method for new steel surfaces
  - Abrasive selection: **TBD** — Coal slag, steel grit, garnet, or other per specification and environmental requirements
  - Equipment: Mobile blast equipment, blast pots, compressors
  - Containment: Tarpaulins, blast rooms, or enclosures for environmental control
  - **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — Standard blasting practice

- **Power tool cleaning:** For touch-up and field repair areas where blasting is impractical
  - Methods: SSPC-SP 3 (power tool cleaning), SP-11 (power tool cleaning to bare metal)
  - Equipment: Grinders, wire brushes, needle guns
  - **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — Supplemental to blasting — **location TBD**

- **Solvent cleaning:** For removal of oil, grease, and contaminants prior to blasting
  - Method: SSPC-SP 1 (solvent cleaning)
  - Materials: Approved solvents per environmental regulations
  - **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — Pre-blasting cleaning — **location TBD**

**Inspection and acceptance:**
- Visual inspection per SSPC-VIS 1 (Visual Standard for Abrasive Blast Cleaned Steel) — **Source:** DEL-26.01 (QR-1)
- Surface profile measurement per ASTM D4417 or SSPC-PA 17 — **Source:** DEL-26.01 (Specification)
- Hold points: Surface prep acceptance before coating application

**Safety and environmental controls:**
- Dust control and containment
- Respiratory protection for blast operators
- Hearing protection
- Fall protection (elevated work)
- Confined space entry procedures (tank internals)
- Waste abrasive disposal per environmental plan
- **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — Standard blasting safety — coordinate with PKG-33 (HSE Management)

### Coating Application Procedures

**Scope:** Methods for coating application to meet requirements specified in DEL-26.01

**Applications covered:**
- **Tank internal coatings:** Food-grade epoxy phenolic or FDA-compliant epoxy lining — **Source:** DEL-26.01 (Datasheet — Tank Coatings Internal)
- **Tank external coatings:** Multi-coat epoxy/polyurethane system (primer + intermediate + topcoat) — **Source:** DEL-26.01 (Datasheet — Tank Coatings External)
- **Structural steel coatings:** Zinc-rich epoxy primer + epoxy intermediate + polyurethane topcoat (or similar) — **Source:** DEL-26.01 (Datasheet — Structural Steel Coatings)
- **Marine coatings:** High-build epoxy or specialized marine coatings — **Source:** DEL-26.01 (Datasheet — Marine Coatings)
- **Field touch-up:** Compatible materials for field welds and damage repair — **Source:** DEL-26.01 (Datasheet — Field Painting)

**Application methods:**
- **Spray application:** Airless spray, conventional spray, or plural component spray
  - Equipment: Spray equipment per coating type and manufacturer recommendations
  - Technique: Overlap, mil build-up, environmental monitoring
  - **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — Primary application method for large areas

- **Brush and roller application:** For small areas, touch-up, and where spray is impractical
  - Equipment: Brushes, rollers appropriate to coating type
  - Technique: Film thickness control, avoiding runs and sags
  - **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — Supplemental method

**Environmental monitoring and control:**
- Ambient temperature monitoring (min/max per manufacturer)
- Relative humidity monitoring (typically ≤ 85% RH)
- Surface temperature monitoring (min 3°C above dew point)
- Dew point calculation and monitoring
- Weather protection (tenting, enclosures) as needed
- **Source:** DEL-26.01 (PR-6 — Application Conditions); **ASSUMPTION** — Standard monitoring practice

**Material handling:**
- Coating material storage (temperature control per manufacturer)
- Material mixing (pot life, induction time per manufacturer data sheet)
- Batch/lot number tracking and recording
- Waste disposal per environmental plan
- **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — Standard material handling practice

**Quality control during application:**
- Wet film thickness (WFT) measurement to control dry film thickness (DFT)
- DFT measurement per SSPC-PA 2 at specified frequency — **Source:** DEL-26.01 (QR-1)
- Visual inspection for defects (runs, sags, holidays, contamination)
- Inter-coat timing per manufacturer data sheet (minimum/maximum recoat windows)
- Cure time before handling or service
- **Source:** DEL-26.01 (QR-1); **ASSUMPTION** — Standard quality controls

**Inspection and acceptance:**
- DFT measurement and acceptance per DEL-26.01 requirements — **Source:** DEL-26.01 (PR-4, QR-1)
- Adhesion testing per ASTM D3359 at specified frequency — **Source:** DEL-26.01 (PR-3, QR-1)
- Holiday (pinhole) detection for immersion service coatings (tank internals, marine immersion) — **Source:** DEL-26.01 (QR-1)
- Final visual inspection per SSPC-PA 2 or equivalent
- Hold points: DFT and adhesion acceptance before final acceptance

**Safety and environmental controls:**
- Ventilation (natural or mechanical) for VOC control and worker safety
- Respiratory protection per coating type and ventilation adequacy
- Fire prevention (flammable coatings)
- Confined space entry procedures (tank internals)
- Fall protection (elevated work)
- **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — Standard coating application safety — coordinate with PKG-33 (HSE Management)

**Documentation and records:**
- Surface preparation records (environmental conditions, inspection results) — **Source:** DEL-26.04
- Application records (environmental conditions, batch/lot numbers, cure times) — **Source:** DEL-26.01 (QR-2)
- DFT measurement records — **Source:** DEL-26.04
- Adhesion test records — **Source:** DEL-26.04
- Holiday detection records (for immersion service) — **Source:** DEL-26.04
