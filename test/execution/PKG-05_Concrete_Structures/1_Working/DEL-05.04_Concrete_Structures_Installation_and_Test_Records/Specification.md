# Specification: DEL-05.04 Concrete Structures Installation & Test Records

## Scope

This deliverable documents evidence of completion, inspection, and testing for concrete structures in PKG-05 as required by the decomposition scope and Employer's Requirements. (Source: test/Canola_Oil_Transload_Facility_Decomposition_REVISED_v2.md:235.)

**Package scope context:** foundations, containment walls, equipment pads, thrust blocks, ground liner system. (Source: test/Canola_Oil_Transload_Facility_Decomposition_REVISED_v2.md:226.)

**Project context:** Canola Oil Transload Facility Phase 1, Fraser Surrey Terminal, Surrey, BC. Design & Build contract. Storage capacity 45,000 MT (3 × 15,000 MT tanks). (Source: test/Canola_Oil_Transload_Facility_Decomposition_REVISED_v2.md:10; test/Canola_Oil_Transload_Facility_Decomposition_REVISED_v2.md:44; test/Canola_Oil_Transload_Facility_Decomposition_REVISED_v2.md:41-42.)

**Anticipated record artifacts:**
- Concrete pour records
- Cylinder test results
- Rebar inspection records
- Supporting QA records (formwork, embedded items, joints, curing, repairs, NCRs)

(Source: test/Canola_Oil_Transload_Facility_Decomposition_REVISED_v2.md:235; Datasheet.md: Construction.)

## Requirements

### Functional Requirements

**FR-01: Record Artifact Coverage**
The record package shall include:
1. Concrete pour records for all PKG-05 concrete placements
2. Cylinder test results for all concrete placements per testing frequency requirements
3. Rebar inspection records for all PKG-05 reinforcement installations
4. Supporting QA records as required (formwork inspection, embedded items verification, joint preparation, curing records, repair records, NCRs)
(Source: test/Canola_Oil_Transload_Facility_Decomposition_REVISED_v2.md:235; Datasheet.md: Construction.)

**FR-02: Pour Record Content**
Concrete pour records shall document:
- Pour identification (number, date/time, location/element, drawing reference, specification reference)
- Pre-pour verification (rebar inspection approved, formwork inspection approved, embedded items verified, weather acceptable)
- Concrete delivery and placement (mix ID, delivery tickets, slump/air content/temperature tests, placement method, consolidation, issues/interruptions)
- Post-pour activities (finishing, curing method, cylinder samples, inspector approval)
- Special requirements for containment walls (waterstop verification, joint preparation) and equipment pads (anchor bolt positioning)
(Source: Datasheet.md: Construction; test/Canola_Oil_Transload_Facility_Decomposition_REVISED_v2.md:235; DEL-05.02 Specification: FR-06.)

**FR-03: Cylinder Test Result Content**
Cylinder test records shall document:
- Sample identification (cylinder set number, pour correlation, sampling date/location, sampler ID)
- Sample handling (field curing, lab curing)
- Test results (test date, age, individual/average strengths, required strength, pass/fail)
- Acceptance (comparison to acceptance criteria, engineering review if deficient, disposition, approver signature)
- Non-conformances (NCR if deficient, investigation, corrective action, re-testing)
(Source: Datasheet.md: Construction; test/Canola_Oil_Transload_Facility_Decomposition_REVISED_v2.md:235; DEL-05.02 Specification: FR-06.)

**FR-04: Rebar Inspection Record Content**
Rebar inspection records shall document:
- Inspection identification (record ID, date/time, location/element, drawing reference, specification reference)
- Inspection scope (bar sizes/grades, placement, cover measurements, spacing, lap splices, couplers, supports, cleanliness)
- Special verifications (containment wall joint reinforcement, equipment pad anchor bolt clearances, seismic detailing)
- Deficiencies and corrective actions (deficiencies identified, corrective actions, re-inspection, acceptance)
- Acceptance (inspector signature, witness if required, hold point release, approver signature)
(Source: Datasheet.md: Construction; test/Canola_Oil_Transload_Facility_Decomposition_REVISED_v2.md:235; DEL-05.02 Specification: FR-09.)

**FR-05: Traceability**
All records shall maintain traceability to design and specification:
- Reference DEL-05.01 drawings (drawing number, detail, revision)
- Reference DEL-05.02 specifications (concrete mix ID, reinforcement type, acceptance criteria)
- Reference DEL-05.03 calculations (design basis verification through field measurements)
- Unique identifiers for each record (pour number, inspection number, sample number)
(Source: test/Canola_Oil_Transload_Facility_Decomposition_REVISED_v2.md:232; test/Canola_Oil_Transload_Facility_Decomposition_REVISED_v2.md:233; test/Canola_Oil_Transload_Facility_Decomposition_REVISED_v2.md:234.)

**FR-06: Non-Conformance Documentation**
Records shall document non-conformances and dispositions:
- Deficiencies identified (insufficient cover, incorrect spacing, low strength, honeycombing, etc.)
- NCR number and NCR package reference
- Investigation and root cause analysis
- Corrective actions (repair, accept as-is, engineering disposition)
- Re-inspection and acceptance verification
- Approvals and sign-offs
(Source: test/Volume_2_Part_1_Employers_Requirements.pdf, location TBD; Datasheet.md: Construction.)

### Performance Requirements

**PR-01: Compliance Demonstration**
Records shall demonstrate that installed concrete meets requirements:
- Concrete strength meets DEL-05.02 specification requirements (cylinder test results demonstrate compliance)
- Reinforcement placement meets DEL-05.01 drawing requirements (rebar inspection records demonstrate compliance)
- Cover meets durability requirements (rebar inspection cover measurements demonstrate compliance)
- Quality meets acceptance criteria (pour records and test results demonstrate compliance)
(Source: test/Volume_2_Part_2_Employers_Requirements.pdf, location TBD; DEL-05.01 Specification; DEL-05.02 Specification.)

**PR-02: Containment Quality (OBJ-7 Environmental Protection)**
Records for containment structures shall provide enhanced documentation:
- Containment wall concrete quality (strength, placement quality, curing)
- Joint quality (construction joints, waterstop installation, joint preparation)
- Cover verification for durability and crack control
- Special inspection for containment-critical elements

### Interface Requirements

**IR-01: Dependency Coordination**
Dependencies are coordinated externally (NOT_TRACKED mode). Records shall coordinate with:
- DEL-05.01 (drawings): Records reference drawings for element locations and details
- DEL-05.02 (specifications): Records verify compliance with specification requirements
- DEL-05.03 (calculations): Records provide field verification of design assumptions (e.g., cover, concrete strength)
- Project QA system: Record formats and procedures align with project QA/document control

- Signatures and dates current (signed at time of inspection/testing)
- Legible and reproducible (handwritten records legible, electronic records accessible)

**QR-03: Verification and Approval**
Records shall be verified and approved:

- Independent QA review for critical elements (containment structures, tank foundations)
- Record completeness check before archival
