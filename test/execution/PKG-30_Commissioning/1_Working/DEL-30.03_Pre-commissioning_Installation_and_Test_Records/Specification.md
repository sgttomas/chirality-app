# Specification: DEL-30.03 Pre-commissioning Installation & Test Records

## Scope

Defines requirements for **Pre-commissioning Installation & Test Records** for the Canola Oil Transload Facility — Phase 1.

**Purpose:** Provides evidence of completion, inspection, and testing for pre-commissioning.

**Artifacts:** Flushing records, cleaning records, drying records

**Source:** Decomposition §5 PKG-30, DEL-30.03

## Requirements

### Functional Requirements

**FR-1: Record Completeness**
- Records shall document all pre-commissioning activities per DEL-30.01 procedures
- Records shall cover all systems: railcar unloading, tanks, transfer piping, marine loading, metering
- **Source:** Decomposition §1.1; DEL-30.01

**FR-2: Flushing Records Content**
- System ID and boundaries, flushing medium/flow/duration, completion criteria met, photos, sign-offs
- **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — Standard flushing documentation

**FR-3: Cleaning Records Content**
- System ID, cleaning agents/methods, cleanliness standards met (food-grade for CSD canola oil), verification method, photos, sign-offs
- **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — Food-grade product cleanliness requirements

**FR-4: Drying Records Content**
- System ID, drying method/duration, dryness verification (dewpoint measurement), acceptance criteria, sign-offs
- **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — Moisture control for product quality

### Performance Requirements

**PR-1: Record Quality**
- Records shall be legible, complete, accurate, and traceable
- Records shall provide objective evidence of acceptance criteria met
- **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — Quality record standards

### Interface Requirements

**IR-1: Pre-commissioning Procedures Interface**
- Records shall reference specific procedures from DEL-30.01
- **Source:** DEL-30.01

**IR-2: Commissioning Readiness Interface**
- Pre-commissioning records are prerequisite for system commissioning (DEL-30.04)
- **Source:** DEL-30.02 Commissioning Plan

**IR-3: Testing Interface**
- Coordinate with PKG-29 for hydrostatic/leak test records
- **Source:** Decomposition PKG-29

### Quality Requirements

**QR-1: Approval and Sign-off**
- Records require commissioning engineer and QC inspector sign-offs
- Authority witness required where specified — **TBD**
- **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — QA governance

**QR-2: Archival and Retention**
- Records shall be archived per project document control
- Retention: **TBD** — Permanent project records (warranty, regulatory reference)
- **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — Long-term retention required
