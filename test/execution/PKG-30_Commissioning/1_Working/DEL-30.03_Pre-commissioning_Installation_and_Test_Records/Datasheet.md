## Conditions

**Pre-commissioning Phase Context:**

Provides evidence of completion, inspection, and testing for pre-commissioning. Prepares systems for commissioning by ensuring cleanliness, tightness, and readiness. Critical for food-grade CSD canola oil quality, equipment warranty, and custody transfer metering accuracy.

**Facility:** 32 railcar stations, 3×15,000 MT tanks, product transfer piping, marine loading, metering systems.

**Source:** Decomposition §1.1, §5 PKG-30; **ASSUMPTION** — Pre-commissioning scope

## Construction

**Anticipated Artifacts:**

1. **Flushing Records:** System ID, flushing medium/flow/duration, completion criteria, photos, sign-offs
2. **Cleaning Records:** System ID, cleaning agents/methods, cleanliness standards (food-grade), verification, photos, sign-offs
3. **Drying Records:** System ID, drying method/duration, dryness verification (dewpoint), sign-offs

**Additional:** Hydrostatic test records, leak test records, pre-comm inspection records
