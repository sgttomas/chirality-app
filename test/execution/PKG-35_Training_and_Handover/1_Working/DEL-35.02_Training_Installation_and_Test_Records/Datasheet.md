## Conditions

**Training Record Context:**

Training records shall document training delivery and competency verification for personnel operating and maintaining the canola oil transload facility:

- **Facility capacity**: 1,000,000 MT per annum throughput, 45,000 MT storage
- **Key systems**: Railcar unloading, storage tanks, marine loading, product transfer and metering, control systems, electrical systems, safety systems
- **Product**: CSD (Crude Super Degummed) canola oil (food-grade vegetable oil)
- **Operating environment**: Marine terminal, Fraser Surrey Terminal, BC

*Source: Decomposition Section 1.1 (Project Overview)*

**ASSUMPTION: Training records shall cover:**
- **Operations personnel**: Console operators, field operators, shift supervisors

- **Management personnel**: Operations manager, maintenance manager, HSE manager
- **Support personnel**: QA/QC inspectors, laboratory technicians (if applicable), administrative staff with site access
- **Emergency response team**: Personnel designated for emergency response roles
- **OEM/supplier personnel**: OEM trainers and commissioning personnel (where training is provided by OEM)

## Construction

**Record Deliverables:**

Per _CONTEXT.md anticipated artifacts:
- **Training attendance records**: Evidence that personnel attended and completed required training sessions
- **Competency records**: Evidence that personnel demonstrated competency through assessments (written tests and/or practical demonstrations)

*Source: _CONTEXT.md (Anticipated Artifacts)*

**ASSUMPTION: Training attendance records shall include:**
- Training session details (date, time, location, duration)
- Training topic/module (cross-reference DEL-35.01 Training Materials)
- Instructor name and qualifications
- Trainee name and position
- Trainee signature (attendance confirmation)
- Instructor signature (training delivery confirmation)
- Training method (classroom, hands-on, simulator, e-learning)
- Training material revision used (cross-reference DEL-35.01)

*See Specification.md FR-01 for detailed attendance record requirements; Procedure.md Step 2.2 for record completion process.*

**ASSUMPTION: Competency records shall include:**
- Trainee identification (name, position, employee ID)
- Competency being assessed (specific skill or knowledge area)
- Assessment method (written test, practical demonstration, simulator exercise)
- Assessment date and location
- Assessor name and qualifications
- Assessment result (pass/fail, score if applicable)
- Trainee signature (acknowledgment of assessment)
- Assessor signature (verification of assessment)
- Re-assessment records (if initial assessment failed)
- Certificate of competency issued (if applicable)

**Additional Records (ASSUMPTION):**
- **Training program overview**: Summary of training program structure and modules
- **Trainer qualifications**: Records demonstrating trainer/instructor qualifications
- **Training schedule**: Actual training delivery schedule (as-delivered vs. as-planned)
- **Training material version log**: Record of which training material revision was used for each session (cross-reference DEL-35.01)
- **OEM training certificates**: If OEM-provided training includes certification (e.g., equipment-specific operator certification)
- **Refresher training plan**: **TBD** — Plan for post-handover refresher training (if required by Employer)

- **Confidentiality**: Training records may contain personal information; manage per privacy regulations
- **Accuracy**: Training records are legal evidence of competency; accuracy is critical
- **Completeness**: All required training sessions and competency assessments must be documented
- **Traceability**: Each record linked to specific trainee, training session, and training material revision
