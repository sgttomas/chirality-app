## Conditions

**Training Context:**

Training materials shall address operational and maintenance requirements for a canola oil transload facility with the following characteristics:

- **Throughput capacity**: 1,000,000 MT per annum (permitted)
- **Storage capacity**: 45,000 MT in three vertical storage tanks
- **Railcar unloading**: 32 unloading stations on 2 tracks
- **Marine loading**: Ship loading via marine loading arms at wharf
- **Product characteristics**: CSD canola oil (food-grade vegetable oil)
- **Operating environment**: Marine terminal environment, Fraser Surrey Terminal, BC
- **Climate**: Coastal British Columbia climate conditions
- **Regulatory context**: Canadian federal and BC provincial regulations for bulk liquid handling

*Source: Decomposition Section 1.1 (Project Overview)*

**Content Organization:**

**ASSUMPTION**: Training materials shall be organized by system/discipline:
1. **Process Systems Training**
   - Railcar unloading operations
   - Storage tank operations
   - Marine loading operations
   - Product transfer and metering systems

2. **Mechanical Equipment Training**
   - Pumps and rotating equipment
   - Valves and actuators
   - Loading arms and marine equipment
   - Storage tank equipment

3. **Electrical Systems Training**
   - Power distribution
   - Lighting systems
   - Emergency power systems

4. **Control and Instrumentation Training**
   - Control system architecture (DCS/PLC)
   - HMI operation
   - Alarm management
   - Safety instrumented systems (SIS)

5. **Safety Training**
   - Process safety management
   - Permit-to-work systems
   - Emergency response procedures
   - Spill response procedures
   - Personal protective equipment (PPE)
