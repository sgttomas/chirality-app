# Procedure: DEL-31.07 Health & Safety File

## Purpose

To develop and deliver Project Health and Safety File for the Canola Oil Transload Facility Phase 1.

**Source:** Decomposition DEL-31.07 (line 693)

**Deliverable type:** Document
**Responsible party:** D&B Contractor

## Prerequisites

### Dependencies

**Dependency Tracking:** See \`_DEPENDENCIES.md\` — **NOT_TRACKED**

**Upstream Dependencies:**
- HSE Management deliverables (PKG-33)
- Operation Manuals (DEL-31.03) — emergency procedures
- Maintenance Manuals (DEL-31.04) — safety precautions
- Design hazard studies (if conducted)
- Commissioning HSE documentation

- Operation and Maintenance Manuals (DEL-31.03, DEL-31.04)

## Steps

### Step 1: Planning and Structure Development

**Activities:**
1.1. Review Employer's Health & Safety File requirements
1.2. Define Health & Safety File structure and content requirements
1.3. Identify HSE information sources

**Deliverables:** Health & Safety File structure defined

### Step 2: HSE Information Collection

**Activities:**
2.1. Collect hazard identifications and risk assessments from design, construction, commissioning
2.2. Document safety design features and safety systems
2.3. Collect emergency response plans and procedures
2.4. Collect HSE procedures and safe work practices
2.5. Collect Material Safety Data Sheets
2.6. Collect regulatory compliance documentation (permits, approvals, inspections)
2.7. Collect HSE training records (if available at project handover)

**Deliverables:** HSE information compiled

### Step 3: Health & Safety File Development

**Activities:**
3.1. Organize HSE information per defined structure
3.2. Develop Health & Safety File document or database
3.3. Cross-reference HSE information to Operation Manuals (DEL-31.03) and Maintenance Manuals (DEL-31.04)

**Deliverables:** Draft Health & Safety File

### Step 4: Review and Validation

**Activities:**
4.1. HSE personnel review for completeness and accuracy
4.2. Regulatory compliance check
4.3. Address review comments
4.4. Final approval

**Deliverables:** Reviewed and approved Health & Safety File

### Step 5: Issuance and Handover

**Activities:**
5.1. Issue Health & Safety File
5.2. Handover to Employer with guidance on updates and maintenance
5.3. Establish process for lifecycle updates

**Deliverables:** Issued Health & Safety File; handover complete

## Verification

1. **Completeness Review** (Step 4.1) — All HSE information included
2. **HSE Review** (Step 4.1) — Content reviewed by HSE personnel
3. **Regulatory Compliance Check** (Step 4.2) — Documentation adequate

**Acceptance Criteria:** Complete; HSE-approved; regulatory compliant; Employer acceptance obtained
