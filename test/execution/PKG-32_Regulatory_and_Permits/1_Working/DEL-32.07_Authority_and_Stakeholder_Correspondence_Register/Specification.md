# Specification: DEL-32.07 Authority & Stakeholder Correspondence Register

## Scope

Defines requirements for **Authority & Stakeholder Correspondence Register** within **PKG-32**.

**Purpose:** Tracking log for all authority and stakeholder correspondence. (Source: _CONTEXT.md; Decomposition Section 5)
**Coverage:** Register (log) of all communications with regulatory authorities and key stakeholders
**Artifacts:** Correspondence log, meeting minutes register, RFI register (authority-facing) (Source: Decomposition Section 5)
**Objective:** OBJ-6: Regulatory Compliance (supports compliance tracking and audit trail)

## Requirements

### Functional Requirements

**FR-01: Register Establishment**
- A correspondence register shall be established at project start
- Register structure shall include: entry date, authority/stakeholder, correspondence type, reference number, subject, action required, responsible party, status, link to DEL-32.08 copy
- **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — project management and compliance tracking requirement
- **Verification:** Procedure Step 1

**FR-02: Correspondence Logging**
- All authority and stakeholder correspondence shall be logged in the register as it occurs
- Correspondence types include: permit applications, RFIs, permit receipts, compliance submittals, inspection requests, approvals, meeting minutes
- (See Datasheet Conditions for comprehensive list)
- **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — audit trail and compliance tracking requirement; referenced throughout DEL-32.01-32.06
- **Verification:** Procedure Step 2

**FR-03: Cross-Reference to DEL-32.08**
- Each register entry shall include a reference/link to the correspondence copy filed in DEL-32.08
- **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — document control requirement
- **Coordination:** Interface with DEL-32.08
- **Verification:** Procedure Step 2

**FR-04: Register Maintenance**
- Register shall be maintained (updated) as correspondence progresses
- Status updates: "Submitted," "Under Review," "Response Received," "Closed," etc.
- **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — project management requirement
- **Verification:** Procedure Step 3

**FR-05: Register Accessibility**
- Register shall be readily accessible to project team members requiring authority correspondence visibility
- **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — project communication requirement
- **Verification:** Procedure Step 4

**FR-06: Register Finalization**
- Register shall be finalized at project closeout with all correspondence logged and closed
- **Source:** **ASSUMPTION** — project closeout requirement
- **Verification:** Procedure Step 5

### Performance Requirements

**PR-01: Logging Timeliness**
- Correspondence shall be logged within [X] business days of occurrence
- **Acceptance criteria:** **TBD** — target: logged within 2 business days
- **Verification:** Register review; audit compliance

**PR-02: Register Completeness**
- 100% of authority correspondence shall be logged (no correspondence omitted from register)
- **Acceptance criteria:** All correspondence logged
- **Verification:** Cross-check against DEL-32.08 copies; all copies have register entries

### Interface Requirements

**IR-01: DEL-32.01 through DEL-32.06 (Permit and Compliance Deliverables)**
- Register logs correspondence generated by these deliverables (applications, submittals, inspections)
- These deliverables reference register entries in their procedures
- **Coordination:** Ensure all correspondence from these deliverables is logged
- **Verification:** Cross-check DEL-32.01-32.06 procedures against register

**IR-02: DEL-32.08 (Correspondence Copies)**
- Register entries reference correspondence copies in DEL-32.08
- DEL-32.08 copies reference register entry numbers
- Paired deliverables: register (DEL-32.07) + copies (DEL-32.08) = complete correspondence management
- **Coordination:** Maintain register-to-copy linkage
- **Verification:** All register entries link to DEL-32.08 copies; all DEL-32.08 copies link to register entries

### Quality Requirements

**QR-01: Register Accuracy**
- Register entries shall be accurate (dates, authorities, subjects, statuses)
- **Verification:** QA/QC spot checks
