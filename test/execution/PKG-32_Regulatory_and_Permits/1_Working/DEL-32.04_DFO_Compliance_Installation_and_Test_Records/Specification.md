# Specification: DEL-32.04 DFO Compliance Installation & Test Records

## Scope

Defines requirements for **DFO Compliance Installation & Test Records** within **PKG-32**.

**Purpose:** Provides evidence of DFO compliance. (Source: _CONTEXT.md; Decomposition Section 5)
**Coverage:** Compliance with DFO Fisheries Act authorization conditions
**Artifacts:** DFO permit compliance documentation (Source: Decomposition Section 5)
**Objective:** OBJ-6: Regulatory Compliance

## Requirements

### Functional Requirements

**FR-01: DFO Authorization and Condition Identification** — DFO authorization obtained (DEL-32.02); conditions extracted | **Verification:** Procedure Step 1

**FR-02: Compliance Tracking System** — Tracking system for DFO conditions established | **Verification:** Procedure Step 2

**FR-03: Compliance Evidence Collection** — Evidence collected per condition requirements (marine mammal observations, habitat protection measures, monitoring data) | **Verification:** Procedure Steps 3, 4

**FR-04: DFO Submittals** — Submittals to DFO per authorization conditions | **Verification:** Procedure Step 4

**FR-05: Compliance Register Maintenance** — Register maintained and updated | **Verification:** Procedure Step 5

**FR-06: Compliance Record Compilation** — Records compiled and filed for DFO inspections | **Verification:** Procedure Step 6

### Performance Requirements

**PR-01: Compliance Completeness** — 100% of DFO conditions demonstrated compliant | **Acceptance:** All conditions satisfied
**PR-02: Timeliness** — Per DFO condition schedules (timing windows, reporting deadlines) | **Acceptance:** **TBD**

### Interface Requirements

**IR-01: DEL-32.02 (Permits)** — DFO authorization (DEL-32.02) contains conditions requiring demonstration (DEL-32.04) | **Coordination:** Extract and assign conditions
**IR-02: DEL-32.07, DEL-32.08** — DFO submittals logged and filed | **Coordination:** Log all DFO interactions
**IR-03: Construction Packages (PKG-08, 09, 11)** — Marine works generate compliance evidence | **Coordination:** **TBD**

### Quality Requirements

**QR-01: Record Quality** — Records accurate, complete, legible | **Verification:** QA/QC review
**QR-02: Record Retention** — Filed per document control; accessible for DFO inspections | **Verification:** Document control procedures
