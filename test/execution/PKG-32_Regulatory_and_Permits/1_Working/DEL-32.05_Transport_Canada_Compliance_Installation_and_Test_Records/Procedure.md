# Procedure: DEL-32.05 Transport Canada Compliance Installation & Test Records

## Purpose

Defines process for **Transport Canada Compliance Installation & Test Records** within **PKG-32**.

**Purpose:** Demonstrates Transport Canada condition compliance (Source: _CONTEXT.md; Specification.md)
**Type:** Record | **Responsible:** D&B Contractor (QA/QC)

## Prerequisites

**Dependencies:** NOT_TRACKED
**Upstream:** DEL-32.02 (Permits) — Transport Canada approvals and conditions
**Personnel:** QA/QC Manager, rail safety personnel, marine safety personnel, construction team

## Steps

### Step 1: Extract Transport Canada Conditions from Approvals
(Implements Specification.md FR-01)
- Obtain Transport Canada approvals from DEL-32.02
- Extract all approval conditions (rail safety, marine safety, notifications, inspections, reporting)
- Review condition requirements and schedules
- **Output:** List of Transport Canada approval conditions

### Step 2: Establish Compliance Tracking System
(Implements Specification.md FR-02)
- Create compliance register for Transport Canada conditions
- Assign ID, responsible party, compliance method, target date for each condition
- Classify by jurisdiction (rail safety, marine safety) and phase
- **Output:** Transport Canada compliance register

### Step 3: Execute Compliance Activities
(Implements Specification.md FR-03)
- Rail safety compliance: rail infrastructure inspections, railcar unloading operations compliance, signaling system compliance
- Marine safety compliance: loading arm operations compliance, vessel interface compliance, marine safety equipment compliance
- Notifications to Transport Canada per conditions (work commencement, operational commencement, incidents)
- Collect compliance evidence (inspection reports, certifications, notification records, photos)
- **Output:** Compliance evidence

### Step 4: Prepare and Submit Transport Canada Submittals
(Implements Specification.md FR-04)
- Prepare submittals per Transport Canada conditions (notifications, inspection reports, operational reports)
- Submit to Transport Canada per schedule
- Obtain Transport Canada acknowledgment/confirmation
- Log in DEL-32.07; file in DEL-32.08
- **Output:** Transport Canada submittals and acknowledgments

### Step 5: Update Compliance Register
(Implements Specification.md FR-05)
- Update register as activities complete
- Record completion dates, evidence references, Transport Canada acknowledgments
- **Output:** Current compliance register

### Step 6: Compile and File Compliance Records
(Implements Specification.md FR-06)
- Organize evidence by condition (rail safety, marine safety)
- File per document control
- Ensure accessibility for Transport Canada inspections
- **Output:** Organized compliance record set

## Verification

| Activity | Acceptance Criteria | Procedure Ref |
|----------|---------------------|---------------|
| Conditions extracted | All Transport Canada conditions identified | Step 1 |
| Tracking established | Register complete | Step 2 |
| Evidence collected | Evidence per condition requirements | Steps 3, 4 |
| Register updated | Register current | Step 5 |
| Records filed | Records accessible | Step 6 |
