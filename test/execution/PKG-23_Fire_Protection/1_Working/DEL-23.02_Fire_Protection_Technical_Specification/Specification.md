## Requirements

### Functional Requirements

**FR-1: Comprehensive Coverage**
- The technical specification shall address all fire protection systems identified in the fire protection design drawings (DEL-23.01), including fire water distribution, hydrants, fire alarm, and foam systems
- Source: **ASSUMPTION**: Specification completeness requirement
- Verification: Cross-check with DEL-23.01 drawing list and system listing

**FR-2: Performance Criteria**
- The technical specification shall define performance requirements for all fire protection systems, including flow rates, pressures, response times, discharge rates, and reliability targets
- Performance criteria shall be consistent with fire protection design calculations (DEL-23.03) and code requirements
- Source: **ASSUMPTION**: Performance specification requirement for design-build
- Verification: Cross-check with DEL-23.03 calculation results and NFPA standards

**FR-3: Material Requirements**
- The technical specification shall define materials for all fire protection system components, including pipe, fittings, valves, hydrants, fire alarm devices, foam equipment, and accessories
- Materials shall meet applicable NFPA, AWWA, ASTM, ULC, and CSA standards
- Source: **ASSUMPTION**: Material specification requirement
- Verification: Material standards compliance review

**FR-4: Equipment Specification**
- The technical specification shall define equipment requirements for fire pumps (if applicable), fire alarm control panels, foam proportioning systems, and other fire protection equipment
- Equipment shall be listed/approved by recognized testing agencies (UL, ULC, FM) where applicable
- Source: **ASSUMPTION**: Equipment specification requirement and code requirement
- Verification: Equipment listing/approval verification

**FR-5: Installation and Workmanship Requirements**
- The technical specification shall define installation procedures, workmanship standards, and quality requirements for fire protection systems
- Installation requirements shall reference applicable NFPA installation standards
- Source: **ASSUMPTION**: Installation specification requirement
- Verification: Installation standards compliance review

**FR-6: Testing and Commissioning Requirements**
- The technical specification shall define testing, inspection, and commissioning requirements for all fire protection systems
- Testing requirements shall reference applicable NFPA testing standards (NFPA 24, NFPA 72, NFPA 291, NFPA 11)
- Source: **ASSUMPTION**: Testing specification requirement
- Verification: Testing standards compliance review; cross-check with DEL-23.05 (Installation & Test Records)

**FR-7: Code and Standard Compliance**
- The technical specification shall identify all applicable codes and standards governing fire protection systems
- Requirements shall comply with or exceed code minimums
- Source: **ASSUMPTION**: Code compliance specification requirement
- Verification: Code compliance review and analysis
