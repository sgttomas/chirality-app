# Specification: DEL-21.05 Building Installation & Test Records

## Scope

**Purpose:** Provides evidence of completion, inspection, and testing for building.
**Source:** Decomposition line 517

**Anticipated Record Artifacts:**
1. Building inspection records — Construction inspections and compliance verification
2. Envelope test records — Envelope performance testing (air leakage, water penetration)

## Requirements

### Functional Requirements

**FR-01: Inspection Records**
- Document all required construction inspections per project quality plan
- Record inspection results (pass/fail, observations, measurements)
- Document deficiencies and corrective actions
- Inspector sign-offs and dates
- **TBD** — Inspection hold points and frequencies per project quality plan

**FR-02: Test Records**
- Document all required testing per specifications (DEL-21.02)
- Record test results and pass/fail status
- Include test reports from accredited testing laboratories (if applicable)
- **TBD** — Test types, frequencies, acceptance criteria

**FR-03: Material Certificates and Test Reports**
- Collect material certificates (steel mill certificates, concrete mix designs)
- Collect product test reports (fire ratings, thermal performance, structural capacity)
- Verify compliance with specifications

**FR-04: Non-Conformance Records**
- Document all non-conformances (NCRs)
- Track corrective actions and resolutions
- Verify corrective actions by re-inspection/re-testing

**FR-05: Photographic Records**
- Provide photographic documentation of key construction stages
- Document completed work before covering/concealing
- Document problem areas and corrective actions
- **TBD** — Photo requirements and labeling

### Performance Requirements

**PR-01: Completeness**
- All required inspections documented
- All required tests performed and documented
- No missing records or gaps in documentation

**PR-02: Traceability**
- Records traceable to specific work activities, locations, dates
- Inspector/tester identification clear
- Cross-references to drawings and specifications

**PR-03: Compliance Verification**
- Records demonstrate compliance with NBC 2020, specifications, and drawings
- Test results meet acceptance criteria
- Deficiencies corrected and verified

**PR-04: Envelope Performance Testing**
- Air leakage testing per CSA A123.21 or ASTM E283 — **TBD**: Test method and acceptance criteria
- Water penetration testing per ASTM E331 or field spray testing — **TBD**
- Thermal imaging (if required) — **TBD**

### Interface Requirements

**IR-01: Coordination with Specifications**
- Inspection and testing per requirements in DEL-21.02
- Acceptance criteria per specifications

**IR-02: Coordination with Drawings**
- Inspections verify compliance with DEL-21.01 drawings
- Locations and dimensions verified

**IR-03: Coordination with Quality Plan**
- Records per project Quality Management Plan — **TBD**

### Quality Requirements

**QR-01: Record Quality**
- Records legible and complete
- Forms filled out properly (no blank fields unless N/A)
- Inspector/tester qualifications documented

**QR-02: Record Review**
- QA/QC manager reviews records for completeness
- Deficiencies identified and corrected

**QR-03: Record Retention**
- Records retained per project and regulatory requirements — **TBD**
- Records archived and accessible

## Verification

**VM-01: Record Review**
- QA/QC manager reviews records for completeness and compliance
- Missing records identified and obtained

**VM-02: Third-Party Review** (if required)
- Independent testing agency reviews test results
- Building inspector reviews inspection records

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- All required inspections and tests documented
