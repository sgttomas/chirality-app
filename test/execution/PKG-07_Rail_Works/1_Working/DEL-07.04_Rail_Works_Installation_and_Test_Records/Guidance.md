## Principles
- Treat each record as part of a traceable chain linking the installed work to drawings (DEL-07.01), specification requirements (DEL-07.02), and calculation basis (DEL-07.03) (Source: test/Canola_Oil_Transload_Facility_Decomposition_REVISED_v2.md:265-268).

- Maintain consistent location referencing so surveys/tests/inspections can be tied back to drawing identifiers and limits of work (TBD until drawing numbering is defined).
