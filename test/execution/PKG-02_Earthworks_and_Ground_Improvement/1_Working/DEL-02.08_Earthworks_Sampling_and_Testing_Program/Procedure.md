# Procedure: DEL-02.08 Earthworks Sampling & Testing Program

Develop the Earthworks Sampling & Testing Program for PKG-02 Earthworks & Ground Improvement while satisfying Specification requirements (R1-R10) and supporting Guidance principles (P1-P4). Source: _CONTEXT.md; decomposition DEL-02.08 entry (location TBD).

## Steps

### Phase 1: Information Gathering

| Step | Action | Requirements | Guidance | Inputs | Outputs |
|------|--------|--------------|----------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Review ER testing requirements, DEL-02.02 (Specifications — acceptance criteria), DEL-02.04 (Geotechnical Reports — material properties, Proctor values) to understand testing scope and criteria | R1, R3, R6, R8, R9 | P1, P3 | ER volumes, DEL-02.02, DEL-02.04 | Understanding of testing requirements; **TBD** until ER provided |
| 2 | Identify PKG-02 earthworks areas requiring testing (grading zones, fill placement areas, structural fill zones, subgrade locations); coordinate with DEL-02.01, DEL-02.07 for scope definition | R6, R10 | P1 | DEL-02.01, DEL-02.07 | Coverage area definition |

### Phase 2: Sampling Plan Development

| Step | Action | Requirements | Guidance | Inputs | Outputs |
|------|--------|--------------|----------|--------|---------|
| 3 | Develop sampling plan per Datasheet Sampling Plan table: Define sampling objectives (material characterization, acceptance testing, QC); establish sample location criteria (systematic grid, random, judgment-based for suspect areas); document collection procedures (sample size, methods, preservation, labeling per ASTM D4220); establish chain of custody protocols; identify sample types needed (disturbed, undisturbed, bulk) | R2 | P3, P4, C1 | Step 1-2 outputs, ASTM D4220 | Sampling plan content |

### Phase 3: Frequency Matrix Development

| Step | Action | Requirements | Guidance | Inputs | Outputs |
|------|--------|--------------|----------|--------|---------|
| 4 | Develop frequency matrix per Datasheet Frequency Matrix table: Create test type vs. frequency table; establish minimum frequencies per ER requirements (e.g., compaction: one per 500 m³ or per lift; gradation: one per source change/1000 m³); define phase-specific frequencies (subgrade, structural fill, general fill, final grading) | R3, R6, R8 | P1, P2, C2, T1 | Step 1 outputs, ER requirements, industry standards | Draft frequency matrix; **TBD**: ER minimum frequencies |
| 5 | Define additional testing triggers per Datasheet Frequency Matrix item 4: Material source change, visual non-conformance, failed test requiring retest, Employer/Engineer direction | R3 | P1, P3 | Step 4 output | Complete frequency matrix with triggers |

### Phase 4: Laboratory Testing Scope

| Step | Action | Requirements | Guidance | Inputs | Outputs |
|------|--------|--------------|----------|--------|---------|
| 6 | Define laboratory testing scope per Datasheet Laboratory Testing table: Soil classification tests (ASTM D2487 gradation, ASTM D4318 Atterberg limits); moisture-density tests (ASTM D698 standard Proctor or ASTM D1557 modified Proctor per DEL-02.04 recommendations); strength tests if required (ASTM D2166 unconfined compression, ASTM D3080 direct shear); chemical tests if contamination suspected (pH, sulfate, organic content) | R4 | P4, C4 | Step 1 outputs, DEL-02.04 | Laboratory testing scope |
| 7 | Establish laboratory test standards and turnaround requirements per Datasheet Laboratory Testing items 6-7: Document ASTM/AASHTO test method numbers; define standard turnaround time (typically 3-5 days); identify tests requiring expedited turnaround for schedule-critical decisions | R4 | P4, T2 | Step 6 output, schedule requirements | Test standards list, turnaround requirements |

### Phase 5: Field Testing Scope

| Step | Action | Requirements | Guidance | Inputs | Outputs |
|------|--------|--------------|----------|--------|---------|
| 8 | Define field testing scope per Datasheet Field Testing table: In-situ density tests (ASTM D6938 nuclear gauge primary, ASTM D1556 sand cone for verification/calibration); field moisture tests (nuclear gauge, oven-dry verification); proof rolling procedures and criteria (roller type/weight, speed, observation criteria); visual inspections (subgrade conditions, placement quality, segregation); personnel qualification requirements (certified nuclear gauge operators); equipment calibration requirements (annual calibration, daily standardization) | R5 | P3, P4, C4 | Steps 4-7 outputs, equipment resources | Field testing scope |

### Phase 6: Laboratory Selection

| Step | Action | Requirements | Guidance | Inputs | Outputs |
|------|--------|--------------|----------|--------|---------|
| 9 | Identify and evaluate candidate laboratories per Guidance C3: Verify accreditation (ISO 17025 or equivalent); evaluate proximity and turnaround capability; confirm capacity for project testing volume; verify experience with similar earthworks projects; obtain pricing and service agreements | R9 | P4, C3 | Laboratory market research, accreditation databases | Candidate laboratory list |
| 10 | Select qualified laboratory; obtain ER approval if required per R9; document laboratory qualifications in Datasheet Laboratory Testing item 5 | R9 | P4, C3 | Step 9 output, ER approval requirements | Selected laboratory, approval documentation; **TBD**: ER laboratory approval process |

### Phase 7: Documentation and Approval

| Step | Action | Requirements | Guidance | Inputs | Outputs |
|------|--------|--------------|----------|--------|---------|
| 11 | Prepare testing program document per Datasheet and R7: Include document control elements (plan number, revision, coverage area, effective date); compile sampling plan, frequency matrix, laboratory testing scope, field testing scope, laboratory qualifications; ensure coordination with DEL-02.06, DEL-02.07, DEL-02.09 per R10 | R1, R7, R10 | P4, T3 | Steps 3-10 outputs | Draft testing program document |
| 12 | Perform internal verification per V1-V2: V1 content completeness check (all Datasheet content elements addressed, test standards documented, laboratory qualifications included); V2 scope and frequency alignment check (PKG-02 areas covered, frequencies meet ER minimums, coordination documented); obtain QA/QC Manager approval | R1-R10 | P1, P2, P3, P4 | Draft testing program, Specification V1-V2 criteria | Review comments, QA/QC approval |
| 13 | Perform ER compliance verification (V3) once ER volumes available; submit to Employer for review/approval if required by ER | R1, R3, R8, R9 | P4 | Testing program, ER requirements | ER compliance documentation, Employer approval; **TBD**: ER compliance criteria |

### Phase 8: Implementation

| Step | Action | Requirements | Guidance | Inputs | Outputs |
|------|--------|--------------|----------|--------|---------|
| 14 | Issue approved testing program to construction team, QA/QC inspectors, and laboratory; conduct pre-construction briefing on testing requirements, frequencies, and reporting | R1, R7 | P3, T3 | Approved testing program | Issued program, briefing records |
| 15 | Implement testing program during earthworks execution per DEL-02.07; document results per DEL-02.06; update program if material sources change, unusual conditions encountered, or ER/design revisions issued | R1, R10 | P3, T3 | Issued program, construction activities | Test records, program updates as needed |
