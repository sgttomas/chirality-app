# Specification: DEL-02.07 Earthworks Excavation & Fill Placement Method Statement

## Scope

This deliverable defines the requirements for the Earthworks Excavation & Fill Placement Method Statement within PKG-02 Earthworks & Ground Improvement. Source: _CONTEXT.md; decomposition DEL-02.07 entry (location TBD).

**Included:**
- Excavation method statement (scope, equipment, sequencing, stability, dewatering, material handling, utility protection, safety, acceptance)
- Fill placement and lift thickness procedure (sources, testing, sequencing, lift thickness, moisture, compaction, criteria, QC, weather)
- Material handling procedure (flow plan, segregation, stockpiles, import/export, dust/erosion/environmental controls)

**Package Context:**
Package scope includes Site grading, excavation, filling, ground improvements, geotechnical works, surveys. Source: decomposition PKG-02 scope (location TBD).

**Excluded:**
This decomposition covers the Contractor's scope of work only. Employer-responsible items (permits obtained by Employer, nitrogen skid supply) are excluded except for interface connections. Source: decomposition Section 1.2 Scope Focus (location TBD).
