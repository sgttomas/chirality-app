# Specification: DEL-02.07 Earthworks Excavation & Fill Placement Method Statement

    ## Scope

    This deliverable defines the requirements for Earthworks Excavation & Fill Placement Method Statement within PKG-02 Earthworks & Ground Improvement. Source: _CONTEXT.md; decomposition DEL-02 entry (location TBD).

    Package scope context includes Site grading, excavation, filling, ground improvements, geotechnical works, surveys. Source: decomposition PKG-02 scope (location TBD).

    Exclusions: This decomposition covers the Contractor's scope of work only. Employer-responsible items (permits obtained by Employer, nitrogen skid supply) are excluded except for interface connections. Source: decomposition Section 1.2 Scope Focus (location TBD).

    ## Requirements

    - R1: Method statement shall define execution methods and controls for earthworks excavation and fill placement to meet safety, quality, and operational requirements; provide clear, actionable instruction for field crews. Source: decomposition DEL-02 entry and _CONTEXT.md (location TBD). Verified by V1, V2, V3.
    - R2: Excavation method statement shall address excavation scope/limits, equipment/methods, sequencing, slope stability/shoring, dewatering, excavated material handling, utility protection, safety requirements, and inspection/acceptance criteria. Source: _CONTEXT.md; **ASSUMPTION**: Comprehensive excavation methodology per construction best practice. Verified by V1, V2.
    - R3: Fill placement and lift thickness procedure shall address fill material sources/suitability, testing requirements, placement sequence, lift thickness specifications, moisture conditioning, compaction equipment/methods, acceptance criteria, quality control, and weather constraints. Source: _CONTEXT.md; **ASSUMPTION**: Systematic fill placement methodology per geotechnical standards. Verified by V1, V2.
    - R4: Material handling procedure shall address material flow plan, excavated material segregation, stockpile management, import material delivery, export/disposal, dust/erosion control, and environmental controls. Source: _CONTEXT.md; **ASSUMPTION**: Material logistics and environmental compliance. Verified by V1, V2.
    - R5: Method statement shall address earthworks activities within PKG-02 scope (grading, excavation, fill placement areas) consistent with DEL-02.01 (Drawings) and DEL-02.02 (Specifications). Source: decomposition PKG-02 scope (location TBD). Verified by V2.
    - R6: Method statement shall include document control fields (procedure number, revision, method statement ID, applicable scope, approval status, effective date) per D&B Contractor standards. Source: **ASSUMPTION**: Standard D&B document control; details **TBD**. Verified by V1.
    - R7: Method statement shall reference and comply with design requirements from DEL-02.01 (Earthworks Drawings), DEL-02.02 (Specifications), and DEL-02.04 (Geotechnical Reports). Source: **ASSUMPTION**: Design-construction integration requirement. Verified by V2.
    - R8: Method statement shall address safety requirements per applicable regulations (e.g., OSHA excavation/trenching standards, confined space, protective systems) and project safety plan. Source: **ASSUMPTION**: Regulatory compliance and project safety; **TBD**: ER may specify additional safety requirements. Verified by V1, V3.
    - R9: Method statement shall address environmental controls (erosion/sediment control, dust suppression, contaminated soil handling if encountered) per environmental management plan and permits. Source: **ASSUMPTION**: Environmental compliance; **TBD**: ER will specify environmental restrictions. Verified by V1, V3.
    - R10: Method statement shall be approved by D&B Contractor construction manager and QA/QC; submitted to Employer for review/approval if required by ER. Source: **ASSUMPTION**: Method statement approval workflow; **TBD**: ER will specify submittal requirements. Verified by V1, V3.
