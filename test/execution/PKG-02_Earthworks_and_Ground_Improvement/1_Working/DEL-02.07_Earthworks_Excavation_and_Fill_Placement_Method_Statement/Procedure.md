## Steps

### Phase 1: Information Gathering and Analysis

| Step | Action | Requirements | Guidance | Inputs | Outputs |
|------|--------|--------------|----------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Review ER safety/environmental/approval requirements, DEL-02.01 (Drawings), DEL-02.02 (Specifications), DEL-02.04 (Geotechnical Reports), DEL-02.05 (Survey Reports), and DEL-02.08 (Sampling & Testing Program) to understand scope, design requirements, site conditions, and constraints | R1, R2, R3, R4, R5, R7 | P5 | ER volumes, design documents, geotechnical data | Understanding of scope and constraints |
| 2 | Identify site-specific conditions affecting excavation and fill placement: soil types and properties, groundwater levels, existing utilities, adjacent structures/work, access constraints, environmental sensitive areas, seasonal/weather considerations | R2, R3, R4 | C1 | DEL-02.04, DEL-02.05, site reconnaissance | Site conditions summary; update Datasheet Site-Specific Conditions attribute |
| 3 | Coordinate with project safety manager to identify safety requirements (OSHA excavation/trenching standards, shoring, confined space, protective systems) and with environmental coordinator to identify environmental controls (erosion/sediment control, dust suppression, contaminated soil protocols, permit conditions) | R8, R9 | P2 | Safety plan, environmental management plan, permits | Safety/environmental requirements list; **TBD**: ER will specify requirements |
| 4 | Assess equipment availability and suitability for site conditions; identify excavation equipment, compaction equipment, haul trucks, dewatering equipment (if needed), utility protection materials | R2, R3 | C2 | Contractor equipment resources, rental sources | Equipment list; **ASSUMPTION**: Contractor resources or rental sources identified |

### Phase 2: Method Statement Content Development

| Step | Action | Requirements | Guidance | Inputs | Outputs |
|------|--------|--------------|----------|--------|---------|
| 5 | **Develop Excavation Method Statement** per Datasheet Excavation Method Statement table: Define excavation scope/limits (from DEL-02.01/DEL-02.03), select equipment and methods, define sequencing/phasing, specify slope stability/shoring requirements (reference DEL-02.04 soil strength; comply with OSHA or engineered shoring), define dewatering approach (reference DEL-02.04 hydrogeology), define excavated material handling (stockpile locations, segregation per DEL-02.04, haul routes), specify utility protection measures (per DEL-02.05), define safety requirements (per Step 3), define inspection/acceptance criteria | R2, R7, R8 | P2, P3, P4, C1, C3, C4, C6 | Steps 1-4 outputs, DEL-02.01, DEL-02.03, DEL-02.04, DEL-02.05 | Excavation Method Statement content |
| 6 | **Develop Fill Placement and Lift Thickness Procedure** per Datasheet Fill Placement table: Define fill material sources and suitability criteria (per DEL-02.04), define testing requirements (per DEL-02.08), define placement sequence/limits (from DEL-02.01), specify lift thickness (150-300 mm depending on equipment/soil per DEL-02.04), define moisture conditioning procedures, specify compaction equipment and methods, define acceptance criteria (per DEL-02.02), define QC procedures (per DEL-02.06, DEL-02.08), define weather constraints | R3, R7 | P3, P4, C5, C7, T1 | Steps 1-4 outputs, DEL-02.01, DEL-02.02, DEL-02.04, DEL-02.08, DEL-02.09 | Fill Placement Procedure content |
| 7 | **Develop Material Handling Procedure** per Datasheet Material Handling table: Define material flow plan (excavation to stockpile to placement, haul routes, traffic control), define excavated material segregation (per DEL-02.04), define stockpile management (erosion control, contamination protection), define import material delivery (sources, acceptance testing per DEL-02.08), define export/disposal (off-site locations, waste classification), define dust/erosion control measures, define environmental controls (per Step 3) | R4, R9 | P2, T2 | Steps 1-4 outputs, DEL-02.04, DEL-02.08, environmental management plan | Material Handling Procedure content |
| 8 | Prepare method statement document per D&B Contractor procedure format; include document control elements (procedure number, revision, method statement ID, applicable scope, effective date) per R6; use clear language, logical sequence, and visual aids (diagrams, photos, checklists) to enhance field usability per P1 | R1, R6 | P1, T1 | Steps 5-7 outputs | Draft method statement document |
| 9 | Reference project drawings (DEL-02.01), specifications (DEL-02.02), geotechnical recommendations (DEL-02.04), and related procedures (DEL-02.08, DEL-02.09) within method statement to ensure integration per R7; update Datasheet Applicable Scope attribute | R5, R7 | P5 | Steps 5-8 outputs | Method statement with design references |

### Phase 4: Implementation and Field Update
